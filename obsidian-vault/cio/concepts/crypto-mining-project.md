# Crypto Mining Project (BBD / BitcoinMiner Thailand)

## Summary

A proposed digital-asset operating business: invest in mining equipment to mine coins,
classified as a *company operation* (not an investment) so that, at scale, it can count
as core business. The project is run through subsidiary **BBD** and operating company
**[[bitcoinminer-thailand]]**. The plan, internal controls and cash-conversion policy
are captured in the `Mining Summary.pptx` deck.

## Three-Phase Plan

| Phase | Timing | Budget | Goal |
|-------|--------|--------|------|
| Pilot | Q3–Q4 2021 | 70 MB (THB) | Pilot-scale mine |
| Phase 1–2 | Q4 2021 – Q1 2022 | 300–500 MB | >10% of assets to qualify as core business |
| Phase 3 | 2022+ | M&A | Acquire mines with top global players; host to buy machines on the dip |

## Source & Use of Funds (Pilot)

- Pilot: **60 MB internal cash** (100%).
- Phase 1: ~500 MB from crypto profit. Phase 2: ~500 MB from crypto profit / RO / convertible loan. Phase 3: M&A.
- Use of pilot funds: containers, 200× L3+ machines (~6.7 MB), 108× S19, land/rent, working capital, construction (~2 MB).

## Machine Plan

- L7: 50–60 machines @ 600–700k = ~42 MB
- L3+: 192 machines @ 30k = ~7 MB
- CMP70 GPU mining = ~4 MB
- S19 BTC in Chiang Mai (liquid immersion): 24 machines = ~10 MB
- Total ~63 MB for machines; remainder for warehouse / MME

## Site Locations & Power

| Site | Power cost | Capacity | Rent |
|------|-----------|----------|------|
| ACE | 3.1–3.3 baht/kWh (methane gas) | 30 MW (~1bn baht) | 100,000 baht/month |
| UBON | 3.5 baht/kWh | 10 MW | service-inclusive; 1,000 baht/machine/month |

ACE burns methane gas — described as a **negative carbon footprint** (the project's
only ESG point). FX hedging policy: none. Insurance: "nothing practical."

## Projection

~2 LTC/day @ $244 + ~3,600 DOGE/day @ $0.2850 = **$1,514/day** (~50,416 baht @ FX 33.3).
Annualised (no decay/difficulty): revenue ~**18.4 MB**, costs ~**6.9 MB**,
profit ~**11.5 MB**.

## Treasury & Cash-Conversion Policy

- Mining revenue is swept **weekly (Monday)** from ViaBTC pools to a cold Ledger wallet.
- "Plan A — build cash": sell end-of-month into THB to fill a 3–6 month expense reserve
  (~Bt 300,000 reserve). "Plan B — build coin" held in reserve as a later option.
- Excess revenue converted to stablecoin, deployed in low-risk/arb strategies; yield used
  for CAPEX, buy-the-dip, or dividends — consistent with the [[stay-liquid-doctrine]] ladder.
- Treasury management considers selling call options (overlaps with [[dat-sell-call-strategy]]),
  lending, and borrowing stablecoin for electricity payments.

## Internal Controls

- ViaBTC pool: password + 2FA; daily auto-withdrawal to exchange; email alert on
  withdrawal-address change to `bbd@brookergroup` (forwarded to 3 people).
- Ledger cold wallet: requires **all 3 inputs** — seed phrase (Accounting safe), login
  password (Varut Bulakul), PIN (Varit Bulakul).
- Segregation of duties: Transfers = Tech Officer; 2FA = Accounting Officer; signed
  Income & Transfers Report to Accounting, Investment and DAD.

## Approvals

- **70 MB pilot** mine: **approved by IC, acknowledged by BOD**.
- **300–500 MB** expansion: to be proposed by IC for BOD approval after the EGM.
