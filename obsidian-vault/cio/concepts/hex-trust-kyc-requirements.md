# Hex Trust KYC Requirements — Corporate Clients

## Summary

The KYC documentation checklist Brooker International Company Limited ([[bicl]]) must
satisfy to onboard with Hex Trust for custody and/or trading. Hex Trust is a fully
licensed custodian with strict KYC requirements aimed at identifying who manages, owns,
and ultimately benefits from a client entity. Documents are uploaded to Hex Trust's KYC
portal (Sumsub). This checklist is the gate in front of both the
[[hex-trust-custody-arrangement]] and the [[ht-markets-trading-arrangement]].
(Document version: Nov 2025.)

## General Documents — All Hex Trust Entities

**Corporate clients:**
- Certificate of Incorporation
- Certificate of Good Standing / Business Registration
- Certificate of Incumbency / Register of Shareholders & Directors
- Memorandum and Articles of Association
- Directors and UBOs KYC verification
- Certified ownership structure up to the UBO (natural person) level

Separate checklists exist for **Trusts** (trust deed; ID for trustees/settlors/protectors),
**Partnerships** (LP agreement; full LP list; GP verification), **Foundations**
(foundation charter; registers of beneficiaries/councillors), and **Listed Companies**
(listing proof; ID for any 25%+ shareholder).

## Jurisdiction-Specific Additions

| Jurisdiction | Additional requirements |
|--------------|-------------------------|
| MENA | Constitutional docs attested by UAE authorities; liveness check; Investor Classification Form; TRN; VARA-license follow-up |
| Singapore | 3rd-party-certified passport & address proof; Investor Classification Form; Source of Wealth for UBOs |
| Hong Kong | NAR1 / NNC1 form; Business Registration Ordinance |
| Cayman Islands | 3rd-party-certified Register of Directors/Shareholders; source-of-funds proof |
| British Virgin Islands | 3rd-party-certified Register of Directors/Shareholders or Certificate of Incumbency |
| Delaware | 3rd-party-certified ownership structure and Register of Directors/Shareholders |

Onboarding contact: `onboarding@hextrust.com`. Submission via the Sumsub KYC portal,
with separate links for HK/Italy/SVG, Singapore, MENA Custody and MENA Trading.

## Restricted Jurisdictions

Hex Trust will **not onboard** entities whose UBOs, directors, authorised persons or
account users reside in or are nationals of: **Ukraine, Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria,
Myanmar**.

## Why It Matters

Both the custody account and the HT Markets trading account are contingent on completing
Hex Trust's onboarding "to HT's satisfaction" (MTA Clause 3.8). The CIO must assemble
the corporate document pack for [[bicl]] (a Hong Kong company) — including the NAR1/NNC1
form and a certified ownership structure up to the natural-person UBO — before any
account goes live.
