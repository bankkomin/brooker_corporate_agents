# Brooker Group Investment Policy (Feb 2024)

## Summary

The standing Investment Policy of Brooker Group PCL, adopted by Board of Directors
Meeting Resolution No. 1 dated **29 February 2024** and signed by CEO Chan Bulakul.
It is the single source of truth for asset-class ratio ceilings, the Red Flag and
concentration rules, and the loan/high-yield authority matrix that the CIO and the
Investment Committee apply to every proposal. It is the upstream policy behind the
IC's [[investment-holding-limit]], [[red-flag-policy]] and [[concentration-policy]]
articles.

## The Four Investment Categories

The policy divides all investments into four groups:

1. **Equity Securities**
2. **Fixed Income**
3. **Digital Asset Treasury**
4. **Loans**

## Asset-Class Ratio Ceilings (% of Total Assets)

| Category | Ceiling | IC top-up | Notes |
|----------|---------|-----------|-------|
| Equity Securities | 60% | +5% by IC | Excludes Strategic Investments |
| Fixed Income | 30% | +5% by IC | Treasuries, corporate bonds, property/infra funds, structured products |
| Digital Asset Treasury | 50% | +5% by IC | Crypto, tokens, SAFE notes, stablecoins; see DAT Fund Program |
| Loans | (see authority matrix) | — | Loan to others vs loan to subsidiaries |

The Feb 2026 dashboard ([[dashboard-cio-feb-2026]]) shows Equity at ~32%, Fixed at 0%,
Structured Loan at ~28% and Digital Asset Treasury at ~57% — a **breach of the 50% DAT
ceiling** that drives [[digital-asset-treasury-divestment]].

## Equity Securities Rules

- **Equity Ratio:** up to 60% of Total Assets for liquidity management across equities,
  equity funds, private equity, venture capital, special situation, mezzanine/Pre-IPO,
  or risky investments. Excludes Strategic Investments.
- **Strategic Investments** are investments providing benefits other than capital gain
  and dividend income (e.g. Investment-Banking-related deals); determined by the IC and
  modifiable over time.
- **Red Flag Policy:** any individual investment with **>25% loss vs cost** must be
  reported with a rationale plan of action to the IC and the Board. Excludes Strategic
  Investments. See [[red-flag-policy]].
- **Concentration:** no individual stock position may exceed **25% of Investments**
  without prior approval of a convened IC + Board meeting. See [[concentration-policy]].
- **Liquidity test:** aim to keep a liquidity test below **10 trading days** for Clause-1
  equity (excludes Strategic Investments and non-listed). See [[liquidity-management-policy]].

> Liquidity Test Formula: Weighted Average of [(50% of shares) / ((daily average 7-day
> volume) / 3)]

## Agent Notes

Every CIO investment, loan or high-yield proposal must be checked against the relevant
ratio ceiling AND the authority matrix row. The "[bracketed]" ratio numbers (60/30/50)
are the live policy values. The 40% Investment-Company cap is a *separate* regulatory
limit, not part of this policy — see [[investment-holding-limit]].
