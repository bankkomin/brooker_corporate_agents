# Brooker Listed Portfolio — Weekly & BSFL Monthly Jan–Apr 2026

## Summary

Two parallel monthly series tracking Brooker's listed-equity books from Jan to Apr 2026:
the **Weekly BG** spreadsheets (Brooker Group's own SET-listed positions held via SCBS)
and the **BSFL Monthly** spreadsheets (the [[bsfl-portfolio|Brooker Sukhothai Funds
Limited]] book of Thai equities held in foreign/NVDR form).

## Weekly BG — Brooker Group SET Portfolio

The Weekly BG files track Brooker Group's own listed holdings: **B, B-W8, CV, MILL,
WAVE, WAVE-W3, WAVE-W4** — a deeply impaired equity book.

|------|----------:|-------------------:|-------|
| 31 Jan 2026 | 1,325.62 | 27,024,305 | Month profit −1.55M; NAV cost 254.0M, deeply underwater |
| 28 Feb 2026 | — | ~27.3M | per dashboard `Feb 26` listed total |
| 27 Mar 2026 | — | ~26.4M | per dashboard `Mar 26` listed total |
| 30 Apr 2026 | 1,493.69 | ~23.1M | per dashboard `Apr 26` listed total |

The book carries Red Flag positions (MILL −95%, WAVE −87%, B −84%, WAVE-W3 −100%,
CV −100%) — see [[red-flag-policy]]. The "DEC ONLY" tab tracks a separate watch-list of
~24 SET names (AMATA, AQUA, BFIT, GPSC, HANA, KCE, KKP, KTC, etc.), all currently zero.

## BSFL Monthly — Sukhothai Fund Book

The BSFL Monthly files ("Brooker Sukhothai Funds Limited — Summary portfolio for fund")
track a large book of SET-listed Thai equities held in foreign/NVDR form, marked to
market in USD and Baht, via brokers GPPA, UBS and Marex.

| Month | Bt/US$ | SET Index | MAI Index |
|-------|-------:|----------:|----------:|
| Jan 2026 | — | 1,325.62 | — |
| Apr 2026 | 32.494 | 1,493.69 | 214.57 |

Each line records beginning/buy/sell/ending shares, average cost, MTM and MoM price
change for names such as AGE, AKR, AMARC, BANPU, BIZ, BOL and many more. The fund's
dashboard summary value ("Sukhothai") rose from Bt 129.35M (Feb) to Bt 141.60M (Mar/Apr).
