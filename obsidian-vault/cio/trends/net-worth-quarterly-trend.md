# Brooker Consolidated Net Worth — Quarterly Series 2014–2026

## Summary

A long-run quarterly record of **The Brooker Group PLC's consolidated net worth**,
parent-company net worth, total assets and the major investment line items, running from
2014 to early 2026. It is the historical balance-sheet backbone behind the monthly
[[dashboard-cio-feb-2026]] and the [[investment-holding-limit|40% Investment Company
ratio]] calculation.

## Structured Loan Book (history embedded in this file)

The file also carries the long-run "Loan to Others" history — Sorapoj, Kingdom Property,
Ekkapong (Phongphan), Moonshot, Nanvarin, Eastern Power, Saithsiri (CV), Damri, Bacelona,
Chillspace, Wave Expo, K. Viwat, Robinhood (PPV) — declining from a peak of
~Bt 1,085M to **Bt 693.44M** after reserve at Q1 2026. See [[structured-loan-portfolio]].

## Notable Long-Run Movements

- Net worth peaked around **Bt 3.6bn** (CONSO) in the mid-2020s rally, then fell to
  ~Bt 2.5bn by Q4 2025 / Q1 2026.
- The Civetta Fund line declined from ~Bt 163M (2014) to **zero** by ~2022 (fully exited).
- Several legacy funds (Brook Global Diversified, OP Crypto, UVM Signum, Nomad) ran off
  to zero over 2024–2025.
- The Sorapoj loan shows a **partial settlement of Bt 171.9M on 31 Jul 2025**.
