# Asian Finance (Advance Finance PLC)

## Overview

A non-deposit-taking financial institution in Brooker's non-listed portfolio, valued at
**Bt 185,000,000** on the CIO dashboards (carried at cost 1.48). The entity is rebranding
toward "**The Asian Finance**" and is executing a 5-year rolling business plan
([[asian-finance-5-year-plan]]) presented to its Board on 15 December 2025. It is the
same holding the IC tracks as **Advance Finance / [[adfin]]**.

## Strategic Intents (To-Be)

The 5-year plan reframes the institution around six strategic intents: fast customer
response (rapid approval), low fixed cost (ability to pass through economic cycles),
efficient performance-driven people incentives, an independent non-deposit platform,
a selective risk/return-balanced portfolio, and simple financial products.

## Portfolio Shift

The plan targets a **dramatic mix shift away from real estate** — from ~45% of the
portfolio in 2025 down to ~4% by 2030 — while enlarging the overall loan portfolio
roughly **13×** by 2030. New focus segments include working-capital lending, bill-of-
exchange, supply-chain finance, EV-user lending and SME credit. See
[[asian-finance-5-year-plan]] for the detailed targets and funding strategy.
