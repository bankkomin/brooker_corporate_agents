# Brooker Sukhothai Funds Limited (BSFL) Portfolio

## Overview

**Brooker Sukhothai Funds Limited (BSFL)** is the offshore fund vehicle whose holdings
are tracked monthly in the `BSFL Monthly` spreadsheets. The BSFL portfolio is a large
book of SET-listed Thai equities held in foreign (NVDR / foreign-board) form, marked to
market and reported in both USD and Baht. It is the operating book behind the IC's
[[sukhothai-fund]] line item.

## Monthly Series

The `BSFL Monthly 2601xx–2604xx.xlsx` files form a Jan–Apr 2026 monthly series. Each
sheet ("Summary portfolio for fund") lists every holding with beginning/buy/sell/ending
shares, average cost, MTM, broker exposure, and month-on-month price change, headed by
the month's SET index and Bt/US$ rate. The series is decomposed into the
[[brooker-portfolio-trend]] trend article.

## Net-Worth Context

The `NET WORTH EVERY QUARTER (2025_26).xls` file records "Investment in BSFL Portfolio"
as a long-running line of Brooker's consolidated net worth — peaking around Bt 500M in
2016–2017 and declining to ~Bt 130–142M by early 2026. See [[net-worth-quarterly-trend]].
