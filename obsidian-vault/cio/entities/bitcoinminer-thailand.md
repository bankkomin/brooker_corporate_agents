# BitcoinMiner Thailand Co., Ltd. (BBD Mining)

## Overview

The operating vehicle for Brooker's [[crypto-mining-project]]. Mining equipment is
procured and operated under **BitcoinMiner Thailand Co., Ltd.**, with subsidiary **BBD**
as the funding/holding entity. Brooker Group Plc provides an intercompany loan or capital
increase; BBD repays via loan repayment and future dividends.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |

| Operating company | BitcoinMiner Thailand Co., Ltd. |

| Activity | Crypto mining (BTC, LTC, DOGE) — proof-of-stake/work coins |

## Operations

- Mining sites: ACE (Site A raw land / B low-rise / C warehouse) and UBON (containers).
- Pools split by site into subaccounts for data separation (UBON, ACE, R&D).
- Revenue swept weekly (Monday) from ViaBTC pools to a cold Ledger wallet held in
  Accounting; converted to THB / USDC monthly via Bitkub.
- Staffing: Khun Suchot (IT), new hire Khun Sumet, ~5 junior engineers.
- Service & maintenance: Hidden Mine (Thailand) for the UBON site.

## Suppliers (Preliminary)

- **Bitmain** (China) — wholesale Antminer; MOQ 100; 100% deposit.
- **BitcoinMiner Thailand**, **Chuanying Supercomputing** (small trial orders).
- **Bart / Jibb** — GPU; **Hidden Mine** (Ubon) — containers + maintenance.
