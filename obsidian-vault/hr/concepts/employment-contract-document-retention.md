# Employment Contract Document Retention

## Summary

Employment contract document retention (การเก็บเอกสารสัญญาจ้าง) is the HR control
domain covering how Brooker Group stores, approves, restricts access to, and audits
employee employment contracts. The control expectations are captured in an internal-control
self-assessment questionnaire — see [[internal-control-questionnaire-employment-contract-storage]].
This article describes the control objectives that the questionnaire surveys; it is the
considered control standard, not a separately published policy document.

## Definition

The domain treats employment contracts as sensitive HR records that must be (a) stored
securely with backup, (b) properly authorised, (c) access-restricted, and (d) traceable
through an audit trail. The questionnaire frames these as ten "internal control topics"
(หัวข้อควบคุมภายใน), each with a control description that the HR function self-assesses
as present (มีใช่) or absent (ไม่มี / ไม่ใช่).

## Control Topics Surveyed

The questionnaire enumerates ten control points. As completed, items 1–8 and 10 were
marked present; item 9 (government-officer contact records) was marked absent / not
applicable.

| # | Control topic (Thai) | Control expectation | Status (as completed) |
|---|----------------------|---------------------|-----------------------|
| 1 | การจัดเก็บสัญญาจ้าง — Contract storage | Contracts stored in a secure system with data backup | Present |
| 2 | การอนุมัติสัญญา — Contract approval | Contracts bear complete signatures of authorised persons | Present |
| 3 | การจำกัดสิทธิ์เข้าถึง — Access restriction | Only authorised persons may access the documents | Present |
| 4 | ระบบบันทึก Log — Logging system | Access/changes recorded via an audit trail | Present |
| 5 | การตรวจสอบภายใน — Internal audit | Contracts sample-checked at least quarterly | Present |
| 6 | การฝึกอบรมพนักงาน — Staff training | Staff trained on labour law and ethics | Present |
| 7 | ระบบแจ้งเบาะแส (Whistleblower) | Anonymous channel for reporting improper conduct | Present |
| 8 | นโยบายต่อต้านคอร์รัปชัน — Anti-corruption | Clear Anti-Bribery and Corruption (ABC) policy in place | Present |
| 9 | การพบเจ้าหน้าที่รัฐ — Government-officer contact | Formal records of meetings/contact with state officials | Absent / N/A |
| 10 | การทบทวนนโยบาย — Policy review | Control policy reviewed and updated annually | Present |

## Why It Matters

Employment contracts are legally significant HR records. Secure storage, authorised
approval, access control, and an audit trail reduce the risk of tampering, unauthorised
disclosure, and disputes over employment terms. Items 5–8 extend the domain into broader
governance — internal audit, labour-law and ethics training, whistleblowing, and
anti-corruption — reflecting that contract handling is treated as part of Brooker's
internal-control and compliance posture. The single gap (item 9) indicates that formal
logging of government-officer contact was not in place at the time of assessment.

## Key Controls / Thresholds

| Control | Expectation |
|---------|-------------|
| Storage | Secure system with data backup |
| Approval | Complete signatures of all authorised signatories |
| Access | Restricted to authorised persons only |
| Audit trail | Logging of every access and change |
| Sample audit | At least once per quarter |
| Policy review | Annual |

## Agent Notes

Items 1–10 describe control *expectations* the HR function self-assessed against. Where an
agent is asked whether a given control "exists," cite the questionnaire's marked status
(item 9 was the only gap). Do not state these as formally published policies — they are a
self-assessment of controls in practice.
