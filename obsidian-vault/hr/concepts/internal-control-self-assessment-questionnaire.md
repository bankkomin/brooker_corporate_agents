# Internal Control Self-Assessment Questionnaire

## Summary

The internal control self-assessment questionnaire (แบบสอบถามการควบคุมภายใน) is the
recurring HR instrument Brooker Group uses to self-assess whether specific control
expectations are in place for a given HR topic. Each questionnaire covers one subject,
lists control points, and is marked by an HR officer with a present/absent (or
Yes/No/Don't Know) status. Two completed instances are held in the HR knowledge base:
[[internal-control-questionnaire-employment-contract-storage]] and
[[internal-control-questionnaire-wfh-rights]].

## Definition

The questionnaire is a single-page checklist format with a fixed structure:

- A title — `แบบสอบถามการควบคุมภายใน เรื่อง <subject>` ("Internal Control Questionnaire on
  <subject>").
- A table of control points. Each row gives a control topic and a control description.
- A response column. The employment-contract version uses **มีใช่ / ไม่มี-ไม่ใช่**
  (present / absent). The WFH version uses **ใช่ / ไม่ใช่ / ไม่ทราบ** (Yes / No / Don't
  Know).
- A free-text remarks column (หมายเหตุ).
- A sign-off block: signature (ลงชื่อ), position (ตำแหน่ง), and date (วันที่).

## Common Themes Across Both Questionnaires

Although the two instances cover different subjects, they share a recurring set of
governance and anti-corruption control points:

| Recurring theme | Employment contract Q | WFH Q |
|-----------------|-----------------------|-------|
| Secure storage / segregation of duties | Item 1 | Item 3.1 |
| Authorised approval & access restriction | Items 2–3 | Item 3.1 |
| Audit trail / spending audit | Items 4–5 | Item 3.2 |
| Ethics & anti-corruption training | Item 6 | Item 3.3 |
| Whistleblower channel | Item 7 | Item 3.4 |
| Anti-Bribery & Corruption (ABC) commitment | Item 8 | Item 3.5 |
| Government-officer contact records | Item 9 (gap) | Section 2 (no prior inspection) |
| Annual policy review | Item 10 | — |

This overlap indicates the questionnaire is a standardised compliance instrument, with
each subject-specific version reusing a core anti-corruption / internal-control block.

## Why It Matters

The questionnaire is how HR control posture is evidenced and surfaced. It is a
self-assessment — a "Yes" records that HR believes a control exists, not an independently
audited fact. For the agent, the questionnaires are the authoritative record of *which
controls were assessed as present and which were flagged as gaps* at the assessment date.

## Known Gaps Recorded

| Questionnaire | Gap | Item |
|---------------|-----|------|
| Employment contract storage | No formal records of government-officer contact | Item 9 |
| WFH rights | No dedicated WFH request form/system (handled via general HR practice) | Item 1.3 |

When a new `แบบสอบถามการควบคุมภายใน` source arrives, expect this same checklist structure.
Always preserve the marked status per item and treat results as self-assessment, not
independent audit findings.
