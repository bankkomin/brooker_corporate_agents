# Internal Control Questionnaire — WFH Rights

## Overview

A single-page completed internal-control self-assessment questionnaire titled
`แบบสอบถามการควบคุมภายใน เรื่อง การให้สิทธิพนักงานทำงานที่บ้าน (WFH)` ("Internal Control
Questionnaire on Granting Employees Work-From-Home Rights"). It is one of two HR
questionnaire instances in the knowledge base — see
[[internal-control-self-assessment-questionnaire]] for the format and
[[work-from-home-policy]] for the control domain it surveys.

## Contents

Section 1 (5 items) checks compliance with Thai Labour Protection Act **Section 23/1**:
written WFH policy, employee awareness of the legal right, a request form/system, formal
reasoned review of requests, and employee briefing. Section 2 (4 items) checks
government-inspection risk. Section 3 (5 items) checks internal controls and employee
conduct: segregation of duties, spending audit, ethics/anti-corruption training,
whistleblower channel, and senior-management anti-corruption commitment. Full item-level
detail is in [[work-from-home-policy]].
