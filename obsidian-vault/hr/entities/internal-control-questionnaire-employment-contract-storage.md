# Internal Control Questionnaire — Employment Contract Storage

## Overview

A single-page completed internal-control self-assessment questionnaire titled
`แบบสอบถามการควบคุมภายใน เรื่อง การเก็บเอกสารสัญญาจ้าง` ("Internal Control Questionnaire on
Employment Contract Storage"). It is one of two HR questionnaire instances in the
knowledge base — see [[internal-control-self-assessment-questionnaire]] for the format and
[[employment-contract-document-retention]] for the control domain it surveys.

## Contents

The ten control points (full descriptions in [[employment-contract-document-retention]]):
contract storage with backup, complete-signature approval, access restriction, audit-trail
logging, quarterly sample audit, labour-law and ethics training, anonymous whistleblower
channel, an Anti-Bribery and Corruption (ABC) policy, government-officer contact records,
and annual policy review.
