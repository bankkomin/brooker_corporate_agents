# 2026 Budget — Operating & Digital Asset

## Summary

Two budget views for FY2026: the corporate operating budget from the Khao Yai retreat
(~THB 95.7M) and the Digital Asset division budget (`DA budget plan 2026.xlsx`). Management
requested a total operating + strategic budget envelope of ~THB 100M, funded from existing
cash, stagnant-asset liquidation, DAT rebalancing, and yield income.

Digital Asset revenue target ~THB 236.48M (advisory/token fees, Metaverse Jackson, NFT
project, Treehouse sale, VC FoF capital gain, Morpho curated vault, BNB launchpad + vault
yield). VCC management/performance fees ~THB 30.4M (Brook Limited Partners FoF I + Brook
Turtle FoF). **Estimated revenue ~THB 257.28M**, total budget surplus ~THB 213.34M —
excluding up to THB 2bn of potential digital-asset treasury sales (subject to EGM
approval).
