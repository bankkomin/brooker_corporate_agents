# R-07 — Approval of the 2026 12-Month OKRs

## Decision

The Board adopts the 2026 OKR framework as the primary measure of management performance —
five objectives laddering up to the [[north-star-2028]].

## Change Details — The Five OKRs

| OKR | Objective | Key results |
|-----|-----------|-------------|
| OKR 1 — Revenue Transformation | Shift the valuation driver to recurring fee income | THB 500M annualised run-rate by Q4 2026; launch & seed 2 VCC funds (AUM $50M + $100M); ≥3 advisory mandates |
| OKR 2 — Structural & Regulatory Re-alignment | Return to 40%-rule "Safe Harbor" | Reduce investment-asset exposure from 50% to **<38% by June 2026**; divest 100% of stagnant assets |
| OKR 3 — Institutional-Grade Treasury | Modernise the DA treasury | ≥10% native yield (~THB 100M annualised); deploy melt-up triggers; new dollar loan at <10% LTV |
| OKR 4 — Talent Bench & Org Design | Fill critical leadership gaps | Onboard CIO/Treasury Risk Lead and Compliance Lead by end Q2; risk-adjusted incentives; wind down the coding incubator |
| OKR 5 — Market Narrative & IR | Re-rate the share price | Publish a standardised Institutional KPI Pack each quarter; run 2 accredited-investor flagship events; raise analyst/institutional coverage |

## Rationale

The OKRs translate the Three-Engine strategy into quantifiable execution, ambitious yet
grounded in the current balance sheet and "New Regime" macro environment.

## Impact

- **Affected systems:** company-wide performance management.
- **Timeline:** 12 months from the retreat; quarterly OKR review at Board level.
- Budget: see [[2026-budget]].
