# R-07 — Approval of the 2026 12-Month OKRs

## Decision

The Board adopts the 2026 OKR framework as the primary measure of management performance —
five objectives laddering up to the [[north-star-2028]], reviewed quarterly at Board level.

## Change Details — The Five OKRs

### OKR 1 — Revenue Transformation (The Fee Machine)

**Objective:** shift Brooker's primary valuation driver from "Balance Sheet Appreciation"
to "Recurring Service & Fee Income."

- Achieve an annualised income run-rate of **THB 500M by Q4 2026**.
- Launch and seed **two VCC funds** (VC FoF and 14% Yield FoF) — AUM targets $50M and
  $100M in 2026.
- Secure **≥3 advisory mandates** from Tier-1 Thai corporates or family offices.

### OKR 2 — Structural & Regulatory Re-alignment

**Objective:** return the group to "Safe Harbor" on the 40% Investment-Company rule.

- Reduce "Investment Asset" exposure from **50% to under 38% by June 2026**.
- Complete the liquidation / divestment of **100% of stagnant assets** (Varuna, trading
  securities, legacy bad loans). See [[2026-02-21-strategic-stop-list]].

### OKR 3 — Institutional-Grade Treasury (Engine 3)

**Objective:** modernise the DA Treasury to generate sustainable native yield while
protecting the downside.

- Generate **≥10% native yield** on the core treasury (staking, options, ICO/launchpad)
  — or THB 100M annualised income.
- Deploy and **test the "Melt-up Monetization" triggers** with programmatic execution
  capability.
- Take out a **new dollar loan via digital-asset rails at LTV below 10%**.

### OKR 4 — Talent Bench & Org Design

**Objective:** institutionalise the firm by filling critical leadership gaps with
"A-Player" talent. (Rushing this is a named pre-mortem risk —
[[failure-mode-talent-execution-gap]].)

- Onboard a **CIO/Treasury Risk Lead** and an **Institutional Compliance Lead by end Q2**.
- Implement a new **risk-adjusted incentive structure** for investment and advisory teams.
- Complete the **wind-down of the Coding Incubator** and reallocate identified personnel.
- Transition key executive workflows to a **centralized digital management system** for
  AI-agent training.
- Implement an **Incident Response Plan (IRP)** with quarterly "Red Team" stress tests.
- Recruit an **Investor Relations Director** with regional language coverage (Korean,
  Japanese, Chinese, Vietnamese, Indonesian, Filipino).

### OKR 5 — Market Narrative & Investor Relations

**Objective:** re-rate the share price by communicating the transition to a three-engine
recurring-fee model.

- Publish a standardised **"Institutional KPI Pack"** with every quarterly earnings report
  and Opportunity Day video.
- Execute **two "Accredited Investor" flagship events** to seed the VCC ecosystem and
  advisory funnel (candidates: Gala, Money20/20, World Bank).
- **Increase analyst coverage or institutional shareholder percentage by 3.**

## Rationale

The OKRs translate the Three-Engine strategy into quantifiable execution — ambitious yet
grounded in the current balance sheet and the "New Regime" macro environment.

## Impact

- **Affected systems:** company-wide performance management.
- **Timeline:** 12 months from the retreat; quarterly OKR review at Board level.
- **Budget:** see [[2026-budget]].
