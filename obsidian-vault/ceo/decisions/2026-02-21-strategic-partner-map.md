# R-06 — Ratification of the Strategic Partner Map

## Decision

The Board ratifies the engagement of the critical counterparties that form the "rails" of
Brooker's new operating environment.

## Change Details

| Role | Partner(s) |
|------|-----------|
| Distribution (placement agents) | [[eastbound]] (Singapore), [[finex]] (Hong Kong) — primary agents for the global USD 600M AUM target |
| Custody & on-chain liquidity | Aave, [[hex-trust]], Anchorage |
| Branding & cultural capital | Film-financing funds, digital IP (CryptoPunks) merchandising for institutional stickiness |

## Rationale

Moving from one-off relationships to an orchestrated network makes Brooker the
coordination hub of a "capital intelligence network." Placement agents provide the
distribution reach (Eastbound ~500 clients; Finex ~40 professionals, ~700 clients) needed
to raise the VCC funds.

## Impact

- **Affected systems:** VCC fundraising, treasury custody.
- **Affected departments:** vcc, cio, comms.
- Quarterly counterparty risk dashboard to be maintained per the operating model.
