# R-06 — Ratification of the Strategic Partner Map

## Decision

The Board ratifies the engagement of the critical counterparties that form the "rails" of
Brooker's new operating environment, across three roles: distribution, custody &
on-chain liquidity, and branding / cultural capital.

## Change Details

| Role | Partner(s) |
|------|-----------|
| Distribution (placement agents) | [[eastbound]] (Singapore), [[finex]] (Hong Kong) — primary agents for the global USD 600M AUM target |
| Custody & on-chain liquidity | [[aave]], [[hex-trust]] (formalised); [[anchorage]] (named custodian option) |
| Branding & cultural capital | Film-financing funds, digital IP (CryptoPunks) merchandising for institutional stickiness |

### Distribution — Placement Agents

- **[[eastbound]] (Singapore):** ~500 clients across Asia and Europe. Channel for the
  Singapore/Europe accredited-investor base.
- **[[finex]] (Hong Kong):** 40+ full-time professionals, ~700 clients, average ticket
  size USD 5–15M. Channel for the Hong Kong / Greater China base.
- Together the two agents are tasked with raising ~USD 60M into the VCC funds as the first
  tranche toward the USD 600M AUM target. Go-to-market also runs through LP/FO events,
  word of mouth, existing clients, and YPO (current LP list: 117).

### Custody & On-Chain Liquidity

- **[[aave]]:** on-chain lending protocol — the "DAT Bridge" for collateralised
  stablecoin borrowing against the 100 BTC buffer. Formalised in the resolution.
- **[[hex-trust]]:** regulated digital-asset custodian — formalised in the resolution as
  the primary custody tie.
- **[[anchorage]]:** named as a custodian option in Session 7. Referenced but **not named
  in the formal R-06 resolution text** — treat as a candidate.

### Branding & Cultural Capital

Approval of the "Cultural Capital" budget: film-financing funds and digital IP
(CryptoPunks) specialised merchandising, intended to build institutional "stickiness" and
brand differentiation.

### Due-Diligence Support

The firm has also engaged a third-party fund due-diligence service, **Crypto Insights
Group**, to help screen the risk-management processes of candidate yield managers.

## Rationale

Moving from one-off relationships to an orchestrated network makes Brooker the
coordination hub of a "capital intelligence network." Placement agents provide the
distribution reach needed to raise the VCC funds; custody and on-chain liquidity partners
provide the treasury "rails"; cultural-capital partners provide brand differentiation.

## Impact

- **Affected systems:** VCC fundraising, treasury custody.
- **Affected departments:** vcc, cio, comms.
- A quarterly counterparty risk dashboard is maintained per the operating model — see
  [[2026-02-21-committee-governance-structure]]. Custody / counterparty risk is a named
  pre-mortem failure mode ([[failure-mode-custody-counterparty-breach]]).
