# Strategic Stop List — Deprioritization & Wind-Downs

## Decision

The Board recognises that focus is achieved by subtraction and terminates or deprioritises
the following initiatives to prevent resource fragmentation.

## Change Details

| Item | Action |
|------|--------|
| 14th Floor Coding Development Incubator | Immediate wind-down; reallocate budget and headcount to the VCC Platform and branding teams |
| Carbon & legacy ESG initiatives (IREC, Laos carbon) | Total cessation of new capital / management time; "Harvest-only" |
| Thai Equities, Sukhothai Fund, private investments | Reclassified as "Harvest-only" assets |
| Varuna (THB 100M), Advance Finance, trading securities | Divestment candidates (see [[2026-02-21-melt-up-divestment-plan]]) |

## Rationale

The 2021–2025 retrospective ("the Brutal Truth") found over-diversification into
low-conviction assets that did not compound back into income. Engine 2 advisory will
absorb relevant coding-incubator personnel; the rest is reallocated to the priority
engines.

## Impact

- **Affected departments:** cio, ic, vcc.
- Personnel from the coding incubator are reallocated to priority engines per OKR 4.
