# R-05 — Committee-Led Governance & Decision Rights

## Decision

The Board adopts a committee-led governance structure to decentralise expertise, enhance
oversight velocity, and reduce key-person risk, together with a formal Decision Rights
Matrix.

## Change Details — Committee Structure

**Board-level committees:** Investment Committee (IC), Risk Committee (RC), Audit
Committee (AC), Nomination & Remuneration Committee (NRC), Corporate Governance &
Sustainability Committee (CGSC).

**Management committees:** Executive Committee (EXCOM), Leadership Committee (LC, monthly
management forum), **Capital Allocation & ALCO Committee (CAC)**, Operations & Product
Committee (OPC).

| Committee | Core mandate |
|-----------|--------------|
| IC | Approve principal investment & DA treasury matters; oversee portfolio risk limits |
| RC | Enterprise risk framework; monitor the 40% rule and key exposures |
| AC | Financial-reporting integrity, internal control, external auditor |
| CAC | Balance sheet, liquidity, funding structure, asset-liability risk, the Stay Liquid doctrine |
| OPC | Operational readiness and product governance / kill criteria for new platforms |

### Decision Rights Matrix (proposed thresholds)

| Decision category | Management (CEO/CIO) | Committee approval | Board approval |
|-------------------|----------------------|--------------------|----------------|
| DA treasury trading | < THB 30M within limits | Breach of limits | Strategy shift |
| VCC fund launch | < THB 10M / US$1M (track record) | Product terms & risk | Final launch & seeding |
| Credit / lending | < THB 10M secured | > THB 100M | Unsecured / non-core |
| Operational capex | Within budget | > 10% budget variance | Major M&A / divestment |
| On-chain borrowing | < 10% DTV | 10–25% DTV | > 25% DTV |

## Rationale

Brooker historically operated with high agility but low structural consistency. Scaling
Engines 1 and 2 while safely managing Engine 3 requires governance-led decision-making
rather than reliance on key individuals.

## Impact

- **Affected systems:** all decision workflows; the [[brooker-operating-system]] digitises
  this governance layer. The Capital Allocation & ALCO Committee (CAC) is the committee
  the corporate AI agent system's Phase 1 was built for.
