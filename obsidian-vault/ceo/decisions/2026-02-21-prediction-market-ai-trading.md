# R-04 — Approval of AI-Driven Prediction Market Arbitrage

## Decision

The Board grants specific approval for the deployment of proprietary AI algorithmic
trading strategies of **up to THB 10 million**, effective **after 31 March 2026**.

## Change Details

- **Focus:** capitalising on a "cognitive edge" in prediction markets — macro events and
  geopolitical hedging on platforms such as Polymarket / Forecast.
- **Capital cap:** THB 10M; a budgeted $1M pilot for new trading strategies.
- **Risk guardrails:** capital exposure capped at pre-determined limits with a mandatory
  **circuit breaker at 15% drawdown**.

## Rationale

Prediction-market arbitrage is a low-drawdown algorithmic strategy and a first-mover
innovative profit source aligned with Engine 3's yield mandate. The post-Q1 start date
allows the strategy and guardrails to be tested before scaling.

## Impact

- **Affected systems:** DA treasury, algorithmic trading infrastructure.
- **Timeline:** deployment after 2026-03-31.
