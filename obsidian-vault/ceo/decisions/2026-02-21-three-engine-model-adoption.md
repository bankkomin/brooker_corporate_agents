# R-01 — Adoption of the Three-Engine Strategic Model

## Decision

The Board formally adopts the Product–Service–Value framework as the organisation's
primary operational architecture, superseding opportunistic "deal-making" with a
systematic, repeatable revenue model. See the framework concept: [[three-engine-model]].

## Change Details

- **Engine 1 (Product) — VCC Platform:** transition the core business toward asset-management products (VC Fund of Funds, 14% Yield Fund) generating recurring management and performance fees.
- **Engine 2 (Service) — Tech Integration & Sovereign Advisory:** position the firm as education and technical "rails" for Thai corporates and family offices.
- **Engine 3 (Value) — Strategic Digital Asset Treasury:** manage the internal balance sheet as a disciplined, high-alpha portfolio supplying dry powder and native yield.

## Rationale

Brooker over-diversified into low-conviction assets in 2021–2025 and was hamstrung by
regulatory friction. The "New Regime" (pro-AI/crypto US policy, legislative clarity,
institutional onboarding) allows a focus on institutional-grade fund structures, network
events, and trusted innovation solutions. Concentration of force beats fragmentation.

## Impact

- **Affected systems:** all three engines; valuation narrative shifts to an earnings multiple.
- **Affected departments:** cio, vcc, ic, finance, comms.
- **Timeline:** 2026–2028 strategic cycle.
