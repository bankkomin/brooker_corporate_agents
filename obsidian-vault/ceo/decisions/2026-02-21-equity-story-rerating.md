# R-08 — The Institutional Innovation Machine Narrative

## Decision

Management is directed to transition the public "Equity Story" away from a "Holding
Company" identity (subject to NAV discounts) toward an "Institutional Innovation Machine,"
seeking a valuation re-rating commensurate with high-growth asset-management platforms.

## Change Details

- Reposition the narrative around recurring fee-based earnings rather than balance-sheet
  holdings.
- Adopt the new Massive Transformative Purpose (MTP):
  - **Vision:** to be the Sovereign Innovation Operating System for the Second Renaissance.
  - **Mission:** architecting a high-fidelity "Vessel" that synthesises Cultural Alpha
    with Institutional Rigor.
- Publish a standardised Institutional KPI Pack with every quarterly earnings report and
  Opp Day video.

## Rationale

A holding-company narrative caps valuation at a discount to NAV. A fee-machine narrative
supports an earnings multiple — the difference between the current valuation and the
[[north-star-2028]] market-narrative target. The re-rating is delivered through the
[[brooker-vessel-framework]] narrative machine.

## Impact

- **Affected departments:** comms (investor relations), finance.
- **Standard dashboard metrics:** NAV per share (liquid + private), AUM growth, cost of
  funds vs. ROE, engagement score.
