## Discussion Summary

The retreat reframed Brooker from a balance-sheet-driven holding company into a
fee-centric institutional innovation platform. Session 2's "Brutal Truth" retrospective
acknowledged over-diversification into low-conviction assets (IREC/carbon, IP/NFTs,
Robinhood) and regulatory friction from the [[investment-company-40-percent-rule]].
Session 3 set the macro backdrop — compulsory monetary debasement, an $85T AI
infrastructure cycle, and global liquidity as the market's operating system. Session 10's
pre-mortem identified six failure modes: regulatory re-classification, the liquidity/margin
spiral, custody/counterparty breach, the "HODL" monetization mirage, the talent gap, and a
structured-loan blowup.
