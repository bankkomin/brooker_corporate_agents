# Failure Mode 6 — The Structured Loan Blowup

Failure mode 6 of 6 from the [[risk-pre-mortem-feb-2028|Khao Yai Risk Pre-Mortem]].

## What Happened (Hypothetical, Feb 2028)

**All borrowers default** and go into a 10-year lawsuit to claim collateral.

## The Blind Spot

Brooker believed it could act as a **"white knight"** to save target companies. But due
to a poor economic outcome the companies could not turn around, and the **collateral
value became illiquid**.

## The Consequence

The company is forced to **write off a significant amount**, triggering **another round
of [[investment-company-40-percent-rule|40% investment-company ratio]] default**.

## Pre-Mortem Mitigation

- Implement a **rapid restructuring program**.
- Focus on **strengthening the balance sheet through other channels**.
