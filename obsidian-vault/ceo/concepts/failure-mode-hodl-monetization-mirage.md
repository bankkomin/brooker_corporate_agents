# Failure Mode 4 — The HODL Trap (The Monetization Mirage)

Failure mode 4 of 6 from the [[risk-pre-mortem-feb-2028|Khao Yai Risk Pre-Mortem]].

## What Happened (Hypothetical, Feb 2028)

The melt-up occurred — **BTC hit $200k** — but Brooker **did not sell**.

## The Blind Spot

**"Greed and Momentum."** Management convinced the Board that "this time is different"
and that the exit triggers should be **raised to $300k**.

## The Consequence

The market turned. Brooker rode the cycle all the way down, ending back at the **$60k
level** with a bloated cost base and **no realized cash** to fund the VCC engines.

## Pre-Mortem Mitigation

- **Programmatic, non-discretionary "Sell Orders"** triggered by the Board.
- The CIO must **not** have the authority to "cancel" a monetization trigger without a
  **full Board vote**.
- **No major expansion** until early profit triggers have been hit.
