# Failure Mode 2 — The Liquidity & Margin Spiral

Failure mode 2 of 6 from the [[risk-pre-mortem-feb-2028|Khao Yai Risk Pre-Mortem]].

## What Happened (Hypothetical, Feb 2028)

The [[stay-liquid-doctrine|"Stay Liquid" doctrine]] was tested by a black-swan event — a
**60% flash crash in BTC/ETH over a weekend**.

## The Blind Spot

Brooker **over-leveraged its on-chain collateral**. Although it held 100 BTC, it moved to
a **50% Debt-to-Value (DTV) ratio** to fund the 14% Yield Fund's expansion. When the
crash hit, the "Atomic De-leveraging" that had been praised became an automatic
**liquidation engine**.

## The Consequence

The "Sovereignty Buffer" was lost in minutes. Simultaneously, local banks (SCB/BBL) froze
Brooker's THB working-capital lines over "contagion fears," leaving the firm unable to
meet payroll.

## Pre-Mortem Mitigation

- A **hard, Board-level cap on the DTV ratio — never exceeding 25%**.
- Maintain **6 months of THB burn in "offline" cash**, held outside contagion-exposed
  banks.
