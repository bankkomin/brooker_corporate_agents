# The Brooker Worldview 2026

## Summary

The Brooker Worldview is a set of ten interlocking macro theses that function as a single
explanatory model of how the world is reorganising around programmable systems. It is not
merely a list of investment ideas — it is the firm's "narrative machine": a coherent map
that explains macro, geopolitics, AI, crypto, demographics, liquidity, and capital flows,
and that every news event is meant to confirm. It underpins Brooker's branding, content,
and product strategy (see [[brooker-vessel-framework]]).

## Definition

A "signature thesis" in the Brooker sense connects unrelated developments, feels
counter-intuitive at first, becomes obvious over time, and creates prediction power —
explaining current events better than mainstream narratives. The worldview is structured
in three tiers: **Tier 1 World Models** (explanatory frameworks), **Tier 2 Strategic
Consequences** (how institutions should respond), and **Tier 3 Product Systems** (what
Brooker builds).

## The Ten 2026 Worldviews

| # | Thesis | Core claim |
|---|--------|-----------|
| 1 | Hyper-Dollarization | The world is not abandoning the dollar; stablecoins are the largest distribution expansion of the dollar in history. De-dollarization is "a well-executed press release." |
| 2 | Infinite Intelligence | AI makes intelligence, software, and content abundant; scarcity shifts to energy, distribution, trust, compute, liquidity, and cryptographic verification. |
| 3 | Global Liquidity Operating System | Most market narratives are downstream of liquidity expansion/contraction; the global liquidity cycle is the hidden OS of markets. |
| 4 | The $85T Infrastructure Cycle | The AI arms race creates an ~$85 trillion physical infrastructure build-out — hardware, energy, semiconductors, rare earths. |
| 5 | Demographics Are Destiny | Developed nations cannot sustain GDP/debt through demographics alone; AI agents and robots become synthetic productivity growth. |
| 6 | Blockchain Is the Internet of Value | Blockchain turns money, ownership, settlement, and coordination into native internet functions. |
| 7 | Tokenization Is Coordination | Tokenization is not about fractional ownership — it is programmable coordination at scale. |
| 8 | Distribution Is the Moat | When intelligence and content are abundant, distribution and trust become the primary economic moat. |
| 9 | The Fourth Turning | Debt saturation, generational turnover, and institutional distrust force the rebuilding of economic systems. |
| 10 | Corporation 2.0 | The corporation evolves from hierarchy into a coordination system / operating system — see [[corporation-2-0]]. |

## Why It Matters

The worldview is the intellectual "Safe Harbor" for Brooker's branding. It gives the firm
intellectual gravity, trust, and authority, and converts every market event into "proof of
the worldview." Strategically it drives the pivot toward frontier tech (blockchain, AI,
longevity, space, physical AI, defense) and away from disrupted legacy businesses.
