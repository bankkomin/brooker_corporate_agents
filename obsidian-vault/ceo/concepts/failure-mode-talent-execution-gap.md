# Failure Mode 5 — The Talent & Execution Gap

Failure mode 5 of 6 from the [[risk-pre-mortem-feb-2028|Khao Yai Risk Pre-Mortem]].

## What Happened (Hypothetical, Feb 2028)

Brooker **hired "B-Players" at "A-Player" prices.**

## The Blind Spot

Management **rushed the hiring** of the CIO and Compliance Lead. These individuals lacked
the specific **on-chain experience** required, leading to sloppy risk management and
missed regulatory filings.

## The Consequence

**Key-person risk intensified.** When the CEO was incapacitated, there was no
"Institutional Memory" or secondary leadership to navigate the crisis.

## Pre-Mortem Mitigation

- Use a **specialized global headhunter** for the CIO / Risk roles.
- Implement a **"Shadow Leadership" program** so every committee has redundancy.
- Set up an **AI-agent shadow for each key C-suite role**.
