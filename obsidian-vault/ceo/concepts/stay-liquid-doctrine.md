# The Stay Liquid Doctrine

## Summary

The Stay Liquid Doctrine holds that in a chaotic global regime a firm's survival depends
not on book value but on continuous, friction-free access to liquidity. Brooker therefore
maintains a **dual-track liquidity profile**: on-chain sovereignty (permissionless reserves
for instant survival) and institutional stability (local THB banking partnerships). It was
formalised at the Khao Yai retreat — see [[2026-02-21-capital-sovereignty-resolution]].

## Definition

"Liquidity Sovereignty" is the state of never facing a rollover failure or credit crunch
caused by a third party's balance-sheet constraints or shifting regulatory sentiment. The
doctrine treats centralised counterparties (banks, lenders) as potential risk factors —
because of time latency, covenant/term changes under stress, and uncontrollable
regulatory externalities — rather than reliable backstops.

## How It Works — The Funding Hierarchy (Liquidity Ladder)

| Level | Source | Use |
|-------|--------|-----|
| 1 | Internal cash & working capital (THB/USD + SCB/BBL lines) | Immediate local obligations |
| 2 | DAT Bridge — borrowing against the 100 BTC buffer / large-cap tokens | Instant, global, 24/7 liquidity |
| 3 | Debt markets — rated debentures / corporate bonds | Long-term structured capital |
| 4 | Strategic asset sales — non-core holdings | Last resort (excludes harvest-mode assets) |

### The 100 BTC Sovereignty Buffer

A symbolic anchor of 100 BTC held in institutional-grade self-custody, usable as
permissionless collateral on protocols (Aave, Sky, Morpho/Spark) to generate instant
stablecoin liquidity without selling the underlying asset. On-chain pools are deep enough
that 100 BTC is <0.1% of the pool — effectively zero slippage.

## Why It Matters

The doctrine makes Brooker's capital structure "antifragile" — combining the survival
speed of on-chain markets with the working-capital stability of Thai banks (SCB, BBL).
It also pushes the firm toward a formal credit rating (target BBB+ or higher) to access
public debt markets.

## Key Metrics / Thresholds

| Metric | Threshold | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| On-chain Debt-to-Value (DTV) | Hard board cap ~25% | Pre-mortem failure mode: margin spiral |
| New dollar loan LTV | < 10% at transaction date | 2026 OKR target |
| Offline THB cash | 6 months of burn | Held outside contagion-exposed banks |
| Custody | Multi-sig, no single key-holder | "No re-hypothecation" for core reserves |
