# 14% Yield Fund of Funds

## Overview

The 14% Yield Fund of Funds is the second VCC product of Engine 1 (see
[[three-engine-model]]) — a fund providing non-correlated, consistent diversified yield
above traditional markets with quarterly redemptions.

## Key Attributes

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Type | Yield Fund of Funds (VCC subfund) |
| Target return | ~14% p.a., no negative months target |
| AUM target | $500M (USD 250M used in the THB 500M income calculation) |
| Economics | 0.5–0.8% advisor fee + 10% performance fee |
| Redemptions | Quarterly |
| Internal seed | $10M priority allocation, starting 1 April 2026 |

## Relationships

- **[[brook-limited-partners-fof]]** — sister fund (VC FoF), first VCC product
