# BICL

## Overview

BICL is a Brooker Group subsidiary used as a contracting entity for the firm's
fee-generating activities and as the seed investor for the VC Fund of Funds. It also
appears in the finance department records (audited reports, loan agreements, corporate
registers).

## Key Attributes

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Type | Brooker Group subsidiary |
| Roles | Signs "Technical/Infrastructure Services" and events contracts (with VCC); GP/seed investor |
| VC FoF seed | $11.2M committed (Q3 2025) into [[brook-limited-partners-fof]] |

## Relationships

- **[[brooker-group]]** — parent company
- **[[brook-limited-partners-fof]]** — BICL provided the $11.2M GP seed commitment
- Has its own audited financial reports and loan agreements (see finance department).

## Historical Notes

- Q3 2025: BICL seeded $11.2M into the VC Fund of Funds, providing "social proof" for
  external LPs under the institutional flywheel.
