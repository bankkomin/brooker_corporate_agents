# Brook Technology Capital VCC

## Overview

Brook Technology Capital VCC is the Singapore Variable Capital Company that houses
Brooker's fund products. It is the structural "vent" that lets Brooker move assets off the
direct balance sheet and into managed fund structures — the solution to the
[[investment-company-40-percent-rule]].

## Key Attributes

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Type | Variable Capital Company (VCC) |
| Jurisdiction | Singapore |
| Role | Umbrella structure for VCC subfunds; Brooker acts as General Partner |
| Subfunds | [[brook-limited-partners-fof]] (VC FoF), [[yield-fund-of-funds]] (14% Yield FoF) |

## Relationships

- **[[brooker-group]]** — parent; acts as GP, deriving service-fee income
- **[[brook-limited-partners-fof]]** — Fund of Funds I subfund
- Subject of an ELCID disclosure: "Notification of Investment Restructuring through Brook
  Technology Capital VCC and Brook Limited Partners Fund of Funds 1" — 16 December 2025.

## Historical Notes

- 2025-12-16: ELCID disclosure on the restructuring of Brooker's VC funds into the VCC
  structure, following consultation with the Thai SEC and SET.
