# Finex (Hong Kong Placement Agent)

## Overview

Finex is a Hong Kong-based placement agent engaged by Brooker Group to raise external LP
capital for the VCC fund platform. It was ratified as a primary distribution partner in
[[2026-02-21-strategic-partner-map]].
