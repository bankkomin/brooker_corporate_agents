# Aave (On-Chain Lending Protocol)

## Overview

Aave is a decentralised on-chain lending protocol — a "global bank of code." It was
ratified as a custody & on-chain-liquidity counterparty in
[[2026-02-21-strategic-partner-map|R-06]] and is the primary venue for the **DAT Bridge**
(Level 2 of the [[stay-liquid-doctrine|Liquidity Ladder]]): borrowing stablecoins against
the 100 BTC Sovereignty Buffer for instant, 24/7 liquidity without selling the underlying.

## Key Attributes

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Type | Decentralised on-chain lending protocol |
| Role for Brooker | On-chain treasury management; collateralised stablecoin borrowing |
| Total Value Locked | Often exceeds USD 15 billion |
| Brooker footprint | A 100 BTC position is **<0.1% of the pool** — effectively zero slippage |
| Comparable protocols | Compound (peer "global bank"); Sky / USDS-DAI; Morpho / Spark (institutional lending layers) |

## Role in the Funding Stack

The Session 7 "Stay Liquid" doctrine names Aave as a core platform of the on-chain
funding stack, alongside Compound, Sky (formerly MakerDAO), and Morpho/Spark. Aave gives
Brooker access to **global market rates and instant settlement** — liquidity in minutes,
24/7, to meet margin calls, operational expenses, or fire-sale M&A opportunities.
