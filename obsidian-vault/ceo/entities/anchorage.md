# Anchorage (Digital-Asset Custodian)

## Overview

Anchorage is a regulated digital-asset custodian named in the Khao Yai retreat pack as a
**custodian option** for the Digital Asset Treasury, alongside [[hex-trust]]. It appears
in the Session 7 "Stay Liquid" funding stack as a custody venue.

| Engagement status | **Candidate** — named as a custodian option in Session 7. The formal R-06 resolution text lists **Aave and Hex Trust** as the custody/liquidity ties being formalised; Anchorage is referenced but not named in the resolution itself |
