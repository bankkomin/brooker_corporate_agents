# Source Summary — The Future of (Crypto) Gaming (Delphi)

_As of 20 July 2022. Publisher: [[delphi-digital]]._

## Document Overview

A Delphi Digital deep dive on the intersection of crypto and video games, written by a
team of self-described gamers and early blockchain-game supporters.

## Key Findings

- Concedes heavy gamer backlash against crypto (Ubisoft Quartz, Dr DisRespect's Midnight
  Society) is "valid" — early play-to-earn over-financialized games.
- Traces the history of game monetization and what blockchain genuinely adds: true
  digital ownership of in-game assets.
- Proposes an evolved thesis — crypto should monetize *around* games rather than via
  extractive token loops.

## Relevance

The core source for the [[crypto-gaming-play-to-earn]] concept; a self-critical,
post-hype framing of blockchain gaming.
