# Source Summary — ARK Invest Big Ideas 2021

_As of 26 January 2021. Publisher: [[ark-invest]]._

## Document Overview

ARK's 2021 annual report — 15 Big Ideas spanning deep learning, data centers, virtual
worlds, digital wallets, Bitcoin, EVs, automation, aerospace, 3D printing and genomics.
Two full chapters address Bitcoin: "Bitcoin's Fundamentals" and "Bitcoin: Preparing for
Institutions."

## Key Findings

- Deep learning forecast to add $30T to global equity market cap over 15-20 years
  ("Software 2.0").
- Dedicated Bitcoin chapters argue Bitcoin's fundamentals are strengthening and the
  asset is preparing for institutional adoption — published at the start of the 2021
  bull market.
- Virtual Worlds and Digital Wallets chapters foreshadow the metaverse and on-chain
  payments theses.

## Relevance

The earliest ARK report in this set; establishes ARK's disruptive-innovation framework
and its early institutional-Bitcoin call. Pairs with [[source-ark-big-ideas-2025]] to
show the four-year evolution of ARK's crypto thesis.
