# Source Summary — Real Vision: How NFTs Are Going To Change the Book and Film Industries

**As of: February 2022** · Ben Mezrich & Raoul Pal · Real Vision transcript

## Document Overview

A Real Vision interview with bestselling author/screenwriter Ben Mezrich (author of "The
Accidental Billionaires" / "The Social Network" and "Bitcoin Billionaires") on tokenising
his next screenplay.

## Key Findings

- Mezrich is tokenising a screenplay about NFTs and Web3 — making the creator a
  participant rather than a fly-on-the-wall.
- Argues NFTs will upend publishing and filmmaking power dynamics, putting content
  creators and their communities front and centre.

## Relevance

A book/film case study for [[nft-creator-economy]] — direct creator monetisation in
publishing and film.
