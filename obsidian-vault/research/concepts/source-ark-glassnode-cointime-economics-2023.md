# Source Summary — Cointime Economics (ARK x Glassnode)

_As of 24 August 2023. Authors: David Puell ([[ark-invest]]) & James Check ([[glassnode]])._
_Covers two source files: the broad-investor white paper and the on-chain-specialist edition._

## Document Overview

A new economics framework for Bitcoin on-chain analysis developed jointly by ARK and
Glassnode in 2022-23. It introduces "coinblocks" as a fungible unit of measurement that
weights each bitcoin by the time it has been unmoved.

## Key Findings

- **Coinblock** = number of coins × number of blocks held. One coin generates ~144
  coinblocks/day.
- **Liveliness / Vaultedness**: share of coinblocks destroyed vs. stored. As of 7 May
  2023, liveliness 0.6, vaultedness 0.4 → active supply 11.6M BTC, vaulted supply 7.7M.
- **AVIV Ratio** (Active-Value-to-Investor-Value): a cointime-enhanced MVRV. >2.5
  overbought, <0.55 oversold.
- **Cointime-adjusted inflation**: 2.48% vs. nominal 1.64% — inflation slightly
  underestimated since 2017.
- **Cointime Price**: a time- and volume-weighted floor model — $17,568 as of May 2023
  (12.6% below realized price).

## Relevance

The most advanced on-chain valuation work in this set; extends the
[[on-chain-analysis-framework]]. The metrics are now live on Glassnode.
