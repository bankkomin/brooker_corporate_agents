# Source Summary — EQUUS Investment SICAV Prospectus (February 2026)

**As of: February 2026** (Prospectus; file modified 31 March 2026)

## Document Overview

The February 2026 prospectus for **EQUUS Investment SICAV**, an open-ended investment
company ("Société d'Investissement à Capital Variable") organized under the laws of
Luxembourg as a UCITS umbrella fund. See the fund entity [[equus-investment-sicav]].

## Key Findings

- **Structure.** A Luxembourg UCITS "umbrella fund" — a single SICAV with multiple
  separate Sub-Funds, each a distinct portfolio with its own investment objective.
  Investors choose Sub-Fund(s) by risk/return preference; Shares are issued, redeemed and
  converted at Net Asset Value.
- **Three Sub-Funds** at prospectus date:
  - Ascender Asia Value Fund
  - Momentum Opportunities Fund
  - IMP Emerging Equity Trends
- **Regulatory status.** Registered under Part I of the Luxembourg Law of 17 December
  2010; an Undertaking for Collective Investment in Transferable Securities ("UCITS")
  under EU Directive EC/2009/65, marketable across the EU under the UCITS passport.
- The Board of Directors may create additional Sub-Funds and Share Classes; the prospectus
  is updated accordingly.

## Relevance

A worked example of a regulated, EU-passportable UCITS umbrella structure — a contrast to
the offshore (BVI / Cayman) private-fund structures elsewhere in the collection, and a
reference point for Brooker's VCC fund-platform structuring decisions. See
[[crypto-fund-structuring]].
