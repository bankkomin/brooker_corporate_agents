# Source Summary — Grayscale: The Modern Portfolio (2019)

_As of September 2019. Publisher: [[grayscale]] Investments. Author: Matthew Beck, CFA._

## Document Overview

A Grayscale Insights thesis paper — *The Modern Portfolio: The Case for Allocating to
Digital Assets* — and the earliest-dated report in this research collection.

## Key Findings

- New asset classes are rare and powerful because they offer a unique, uncorrelated
  return stream — a genuine diversification "free lunch" (citing Cliff Asness).
- Argues the optimal beta portfolio lies on a *new* efficient frontier that includes
  digital assets — the "missing piece of the puzzle."
- Investors are generally under-allocated to digital assets relative to this frontier.
- Predates the 2020-21 COVID monetary expansion and institutional adoption wave.

## Relevance

A pre-cycle intellectual anchor for [[crypto-portfolio-allocation]] and
[[crypto-as-institutional-asset-class]].
