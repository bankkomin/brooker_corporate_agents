# Source Summary — Coinbase Q1 2026 Earnings Deck

_As of 7 May 2026 (covering Q1 2026). Publisher: [[coinbase]]._

## Document Overview

Coinbase's Q1 2026 earnings presentation, framing crypto as "updating all financial
services" with AI as an accelerant.

## Key Findings

- The "Onchain Economy" rests on four pillars: Trading, Stablecoins, Tokenization, and
  AI-Native Finance.
- Total crypto trading volume has grown >50x over 7 years (~$14T as of Q1'26).
- Stablecoin market cap ~$305B (Q1'26); Treasury Secretary Bessent cited a 10x path to
  $3T by 2030.
- Tokenized RWA supply (ex-stablecoin) ~$29B (Q1'26); BCG/ADDX forecast $16T by 2030.
- AI agents forecast (McKinsey) to process $35T of transactions by 2030; crypto framed
  as the "native execution rail" for the agent economy — global, programmable, 24/7.

The most current data point in this set for the [[tokenization-real-world-assets]]
thesis and crypto's convergence with AI.
