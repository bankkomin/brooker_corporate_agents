# Source Summary — Real Vision: NFTs Are Revolutionizing Old Media (Time)

**As of: April 2022** · Keith A. Grossman (President, Time) & Raoul Pal · Real Vision transcript

## Document Overview

A Real Vision interview with Time magazine president Keith Grossman on pivoting a
century-old publisher into a Web3 leader (the TIMEPieces NFT initiative).

## Key Findings

- Time embraced NFTs in 2021 to reinvent a legacy media business — community ownership
  and direct fan relationships rather than ad-funded distribution.
- A case study in how incumbent media can use NFTs to change its business model.
