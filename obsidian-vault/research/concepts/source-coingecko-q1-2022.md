# Source Summary — CoinGecko Q1 2022 Cryptocurrency Report

_As of Q1 2022 (published 6 June 2022). Publisher: [[coingecko]]._

## Document Overview

CoinGecko's Q1 2022 quarterly. Crypto markets weathered high inflation, Russia's
invasion of Ukraine and a Fed rate hike, closing the quarter near where they started at
~$2.2T total market cap.

## Key Findings

- Crypto moved in lockstep with TradFi growth/tech stocks — LUNA, ETC, FTT and WAVES
  were among the few to close green.
- Dedicated "Rise of UST" section: decentralized/algorithmic stablecoins, why UST
  adoption was "mooning," and UST's planned Bitcoin backing — published weeks before
  the May 2022 Terra collapse.
- Cross-chain bridges and the multichain DeFi landscape profiled.

## Relevance

Captures the pre-collapse UST optimism that the [[algorithmic-stablecoin-death-spiral]]
concept analyses with hindsight.
