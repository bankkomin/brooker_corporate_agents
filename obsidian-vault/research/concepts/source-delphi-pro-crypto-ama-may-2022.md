# Source Summary — Delphi Pro Crypto AMA (31 May 2022)

**As of: 31 May 2022** · Delphi Research team (Kevin Kelly, Jason Pagoulatos et al.)

## Document Overview

A transcript of Delphi's biweekly Pro Crypto AMA, recorded shortly after the Terra
collapse.

## Key Findings

- Macro: markets awaiting Fed inflation data to gauge rate-hike path.
- Crypto market commentary: Bitcoin rallied ~12% off range lows (~$28.5k to ~$32k) after
  nine straight red weeks; analyst flags resistance at $34–36k and advises caution /
  de-risking into strength.
- Reflects the immediate post-Terra bear-market mood.

## Relevance

A point-in-time market-sentiment snapshot supporting [[crypto-cycles-and-liquidity]]; sits
alongside the Terra post-mortems ([[algorithmic-stablecoin-death-spiral]]).

## Extracted Data

- BTC range lows ~$28.5k; rallied to ~$32k; resistance ~$34–36k (late May 2022).
- Nine consecutive weeks of BTC declines prior to the rally.
