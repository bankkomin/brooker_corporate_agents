# Source Summary — Guide to Crypto Markets Q1 2025 (Glassnode x Coinbase)

_As of Q1 2025. Publishers: [[glassnode]] & [[coinbase]] Institutional._

## Document Overview

A joint Glassnode / Coinbase quarterly guide to crypto markets, with partner-focus
sections (a16z, Grayscale, Tephra Digital, CoinFund). Sections: Market Overview, Bitcoin,
Ethereum.

## Key Findings

- Spotlights: "Ownership in Transition" (BTC/ETH holding durations), "Stablecoins Take
  Off" (supply and volumes), and "Layer-2s Lead the Way" (ETH + L2 active addresses,
  transactions, fees).
- Tracks BTC and ETH spot ETF balances, NUPL ("fear & greed"), supply profitability,
  derivatives (open interest, funding, liquidations), and ETH staking yield and DeFi TVL.
- A practical applied use of the [[on-chain-analysis-framework]] for institutional
  allocators.

## Relevance

A current institutional market dashboard; documents stablecoin growth and L2 scaling
within the broader market cycle.
