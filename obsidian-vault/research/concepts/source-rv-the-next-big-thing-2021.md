# Source Summary — Real Vision: The Next Big Thing (Raoul Pal)

**As of: 28 October 2021** (republished 31 Dec 2021) · Raoul Pal & Ash Bennington · Real Vision transcript

## Document Overview

A Real Vision "Adventures in Crypto" transcript — Raoul Pal synthesises his evolving
crypto thesis across Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Luna, NFTs and the metaverse.

## Key Findings

- **Fastest adoption in history**: crypto at 150M users (late 2021) grew ~113%/yr vs the
  internet's 63%/yr at the same user count.
- The "Exponential Age" framing — crypto a ~$2.5T asset class that should scale toward
  other major asset classes (equities $150–300T) if adoption continues.
- Covers the rapid emergence of DeFi, NFTs, DAOs, CBDCs and stablecoins.
- Builds a personal framework to navigate the information overload rather than be an
  expert on everything.

## Relevance

A foundational statement of the adoption-curve / [[crypto-cycles-and-liquidity]] thesis
at the 2021 peak; touches [[web3-ownership-thesis]] and [[metaverse-thesis]].

## Extracted Data

- Crypto users late 2021: ~150M, growing ~113%/yr.
- Asset-class size cited: ~$2.5T.
