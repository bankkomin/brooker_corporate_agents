# Source Summary — Messari: State of BNB Chain Q1 2023

**As of: Q1 2023** (report 19 April 2023) · Messari, commissioned by BNB Chain

## Document Overview

A Messari quarterly "Protocol Reporting" community report on BNB Chain — network,
financial, ecosystem, DeFi, staking and roadmap analysis. Commissioned by BNB Chain but
authored independently.

## Key Findings

- BNB Chain market cap rebounded +24.9% QoQ with the broader market, but **revenue (in
  BNB) fell 11.7% QoQ** — value driven by sentiment more than network utility.
- The market-cap-to-quarterly-revenue ratio worsened from 670x (Q4 2022) to 887x
  (Q1 2023).
- Network decentralised further: avg validators +10.7%, total stake +12.1% (BEPs
  131/153/159).
- **BUSD collapse**: after US regulators forced Paxos to stop issuing BUSD, BUSD market
  cap on BNB Chain fell ~$2.5B (~54%) QoQ; dominance shifted to USDT.
- DeFi: Uniswap launched on BNB Chain (~$50M volume, $11M TVL in two weeks); PancakeSwap
  dominance fell to ~43%. GameFi wallet activity down 41% QoQ.
- Jump Crypto found and disclosed a Beacon Chain vulnerability (patched within 24h);
  CFTC sued Binance/CZ in March 2023.
- 2023 roadmap: modular rollup L2s (ZK + optimistic), BNB Greenfield storage chain,
  account abstraction; gas limit raise toward 5,000 TPS; validators 29 → 100.
