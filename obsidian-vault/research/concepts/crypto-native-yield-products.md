# Crypto Native-Yield Products

## Summary

A recurring theme across the Counterparties and Brainstorm research: the menu of
**native-yield products** available on a crypto treasury — the ways idle digital assets
generate return without disposal. Documented most fully in the Binance Earn masterclass
([[source-binance-vip-masterclass]]).

## The Product Menu

| Product type | Mechanism | Indicative yield | Risk profile |
|--------------|-----------|------------------|--------------|
| Flexible savings | Instant-redeemable deposits, real-time APR | ~1–10% | Low (value fluctuates) |
| Locked savings | Fixed-term deposits (14–150 days), higher APR | ~1–15% | Moderate (early redemption forfeits rewards) |
| Liquid staking | Stake ETH/SOL for LSTs (WBETH, BNSOL) that auto-compound | ~3–8% | Moderate (asset volatility) |
| Funding-rate arbitrage | Delta-neutral perp/spot positions collecting funding | variable (~20% annualized in one worked example) | Tail/spread/liquidity risk |

LSTs are notable for **capital efficiency** — they continue earning staking rewards while
also serving as collateral for loans, margin, or futures.

## Why It Matters

These products are the toolkit for generating native yield on a digital-asset treasury
(Engine 3 of the [[three-engine-model]]) — staking, structured deposits, and
market-neutral strategies. The yield/risk trade-offs inform treasury allocation and
counterparty selection.
