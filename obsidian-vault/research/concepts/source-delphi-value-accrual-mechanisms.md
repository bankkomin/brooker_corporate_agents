# Source Summary — Delphi: More Than Just Governance — Unpacking Value Accrual Mechanisms

**As of: October 2023** · Delphi Digital · Crypto Insiders Deep Dive (~33 pages)

## Document Overview

A Delphi "Crypto Insiders" deep dive examining how tokens accrue value — arguing that
governance rights alone are a weak basis for token value.

## Key Findings

- "Governance" as a sole value-accrual mechanism is insufficient — the 2022 bear market
  punished governance-only tokens.
- Surveys stronger mechanisms: fee capture / buyback-and-burn, real-yield staking
  distributions, and collateral/backing demand.
- Argues for distinguishing **fundamental value accrual** (cash-flow-like) from
  **sentiment-driven** value.

## Relevance

The primary source for the [[token-value-accrual]] concept. Part of the tokenomics strand
of [[web3-ownership-thesis]].
