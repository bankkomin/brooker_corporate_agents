# Source Summary — Delphi: Synthetix Protocol Deep Dive

**As of: ~2023** · Delphi Digital · protocol deep dive

## Document Overview

A Delphi deep dive on Synthetix — a DeFi protocol for synthetic assets, where the SNX
token is staked as collateral backing synthetic asset issuance.

## Key Findings

- Synthetix is a leading example of a DeFi protocol with a **collateral-backed value
  accrual** model: SNX must be staked to mint synthetic assets (synths), creating
  structural token demand beyond governance.
- Stakers earn protocol trading fees — a "real yield" design rather than pure inflation.

## Relevance

A protocol case study for [[token-value-accrual]] — the collateral-demand and fee-capture
mechanisms. Part of the DeFi cluster under [[web3-ownership-thesis]].

- Protocol: Synthetix; token: SNX (staked collateral for synthetic assets).
