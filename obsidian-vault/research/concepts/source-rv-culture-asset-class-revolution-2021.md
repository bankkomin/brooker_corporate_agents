# Source Summary — Culture: An Asset Class Revolution (Real Vision, Yat Siu)

_As of 15 October 2021. Publisher: [[real-vision]]. Interview: Yat Siu (Animoca Brands) & Raoul Pal._

## Document Overview

A Real Vision interview transcript with Yat Siu, co-founder and chairman of Animoca
Brands, on NFTs, the metaverse, and culture as an emerging asset class.

## Key Findings

- NFTs are not "JPEGs" — they represent culture, community, sovereignty and collective
  futures; people should see what NFTs *represent*, not what they are now.
- Argues digital communities are creating new "sovereign societies" built around culture.
- Frames the metaverse and NFTs as a revolution in how value and identity are owned.

## Relevance

The defining articulation of the [[nft-culture-asset-class]] thesis from one of its
most influential proponents.
