# Source Summary — Delphi: The Complete Guide to Rollups

**As of: 4 August 2022** · Author: Jon Charbonneau, Delphi Digital

## Document Overview

A Delphi "Crypto Insiders" premium report (the collection holds two near-identical
extracts of it) analysing the design space of rollups on Ethereum and Celestia.

## Key Findings

- Defines **modular blockchains**: stacks that split blockchain tasks — Data Availability
  (DA), settlement, execution, consensus — into separate components.
- **Part I — Modular Economics**: rollup fees, base-layer fees (Ethereum vs Celestia),
  MEV in the modular stack, DA-layer economic security.
- **Part II — Ethereum rollup stacks**: smart-contract rollups, recursive rollups,
  "enshrined" rollups, settlement & DA layers.
- **Part III — Celestia rollup stacks**: sovereign rollups, sovereign settlement rollups.
- Notes other rollup-stack players: Polygon (Avail), Tezos (likely first to ship
  "enshrined" rollups), Milkomeda.

## Relevance

The deepest technical source for [[ethereum-scaling-rollups]] — the modular-stack and
rollup-economics detail behind the scaling thesis.
