# Source Summary — Generative Art Takes Off (Delphi, Jul 2022)

_As of 20 July 2022. Publisher: [[delphi-digital]] (Crypto Insiders In Focus)._

## Document Overview

A Delphi Digital report on the rise of generative art within the NFT market.

## Key Findings

- Generative art sits at the intersection of technology and culture — positioned as a
  potential defining art movement of the 21st century.
- Drivers of the 2022 narrative: changing mindsets around NFTs, USD price falls, and the
  establishment of NFT funds/DAOs.
- Art Blocks is the top curation platform and launchpad; Chromie Squiggles reached
  all-time-high ETH floor prices.

## Relevance

A focused case study within the [[nft-culture-asset-class]] thesis — generative art as
a flight-to-quality NFT category during the bear market.
