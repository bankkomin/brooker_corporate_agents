# Source Summary — SAFE Agreement Template ($10M Valuation Cap, 2022)

**As of: 3 October 2022**

## Document Overview

A **SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity)** instrument — a standard early-stage
investment contract. The template is filled in for **Momentum Mining Inc.** (a Delaware
corporation; CEO Benjamin Holloway) with a US$10,000,000 valuation cap.

## Key Findings

- **Instrument.** A SAFE gives the Investor the right to future shares in exchange for an
  upfront Purchase Amount — not a loan and not equity until a triggering event.
- **Valuation Cap.** US$10,000,000 (pre-money valuation cap).
- **Triggering events:**
  - **Equity Financing** — converts to Standard Preferred Stock (if pre-money ≤ cap) or
    Safe Preferred Stock (if pre-money > cap), priced via the Safe Price (= cap ÷ company
    capitalization).
  - **Liquidity Event** (change of control or IPO) — Investor elects cash equal to the
    Purchase Amount, or Common Stock at the Liquidity Price.
  - **Dissolution Event** — Purchase Amount repaid in preference to capital-stock holders.
- **Investor status.** Must be an accredited investor (Rule 501, Regulation D); securities
  unregistered under the Securities Act; governed by Delaware law.

## Relevance

A reference template for an early-stage investment instrument — relevant to how Brooker
(or a Brooker advisory client) would structure or receive a SAFE in a venture round.
