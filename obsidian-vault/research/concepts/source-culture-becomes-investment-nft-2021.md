# Source Summary — Culture Becomes Investment Through NFT (Notes)

_As of 23 October 2021. Format: informal notes (apparently distilled from the Yat Siu / Real Vision interview)._

## Document Overview

A short notes document capturing the thesis that culture becomes investable through
NFTs. Reads as personal bullet-point notes rather than a formal report.

## Key Findings

- Metaverse is "data"; data is the new commodity; DeFi lets users benefit from their
  data and equity ownership — a redistribution-of-wealth framing.
- Ownership drives care ("if you own NFT you will defend it; if you rent, no one
  cares").
- Game studios are best-positioned for the metaverse (storytelling + simulation).
- Virtual land is valuable because of network effects and proximity; NFTs represent
  culture, history and interaction of feelings.
- "Bootleg copies make the original more valuable" (Mona Lisa analogy).
