# Source Summary — Searching for Market Bottoms (Delphi, Jun 2022)

_As of 24 June 2022. Publisher: [[delphi-digital]] (Crypto Insiders In Focus)._

## Document Overview

A Delphi Digital report on identifying signs of a crypto market bottom during one of the
sharpest drawdowns on record.

## Key Findings

- Most crypto assets were down 60-90% from prior highs; sentiment was poor, liquidity
  drying up, funding scarce.
- The market shifted from "ape-at-any-cost" to capital preservation and risk
  management.
- Discusses the signals analysts watch to identify a market bottom.

## Relevance

A bear-market-bottom framework; complements [[crypto-cycles-and-liquidity]] and the
[[on-chain-analysis-framework]] valuation metrics.
