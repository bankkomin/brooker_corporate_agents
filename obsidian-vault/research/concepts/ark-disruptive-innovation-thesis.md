# Disruptive Innovation & Technology Convergence Thesis

## Summary

A recurring thesis across the AI / tech-innovation research collection: a small number of
**innovation platforms are evolving and converging simultaneously**, and their
intermeshing accelerates the pace of change faster than consensus expects. The thesis is
most fully articulated by ARK Invest's Big Ideas reports ([[source-ark-big-ideas-2022]],
[[source-ark-big-ideas-2026]]) and reinforced from the crypto side by Messari's State of
AI ([[source-state-of-ai-messari-2025]]).

## Core Argument

1. **Platform convergence.** Five platforms — AI, Public Blockchains, Robotics, Energy
   Storage, Multiomics (DNA Sequencing in the 2022 framing) — catalyze one another;
   performance gains in one unlock new capabilities in the others.
2. **AI is the central dynamo.** AI is the critical enabling platform; the 2026 framing
   ("The Great Acceleration") puts AI at the center, with robotics inflecting as a
   catalyst in 2025.
3. **Step-change in growth.** Convergence drives an unprecedented technology investment
   cycle and a structural increase in real GDP growth (ARK forecasts ~7.3% global real
   GDP growth by 2030 vs an IMF ~3.1%).
4. **Crypto as machine-native infrastructure.** Smart contracts and stablecoins provide
   the coordination, verification, payments, and identity rails that let AI agents direct
   real-world resources — the bridge between the AI and blockchain platforms.

## How the Framing Evolved

| Element | 2022 (Big Ideas) | 2026 (Big Ideas) |
|---------|------------------|------------------|
| 5th platform | DNA Sequencing | Multiomics |
| Robotics role | One of five | Inflecting catalyst (2025) |
| New frontier | Web3 / Digital Wallets | Space-based AI compute |
| Macro framing | "Change & Convergence" | "The Great Acceleration" |

## Why It Matters

The convergence thesis is the macro-innovation backdrop for Brooker's technology and
digital-asset positioning. The "agents transacting in stablecoins" sub-thesis directly
supports the advisory and digital-asset lines of the [[three-engine-model]].
