# Source Summary — Why Bitcoin Is Behaving Like It Should (Delphi, Jan 2022)

_As of 11 January 2022. Publisher: [[delphi-digital]] (Crypto Insiders Flash Update)._

## Document Overview

A Delphi Digital flash update arguing that Bitcoin's correlated, risk-off behaviour at
the start of 2022 was consistent with macro fundamentals.

## Key Findings

- Bitcoin and the broader crypto market are not isolated from macro risks — especially
  global liquidity and financial conditions.
- History suggests it is tighter liquidity and risk-off volatility, not rate hikes per
  se, that adversely impact BTC.
- Consumer price inflation and currency debasement are "two sides of the same coin" but
  affect asset prices differently.

## Relevance

A clean statement of the macro-liquidity lens on crypto — supports
[[crypto-cycles-and-liquidity]].
