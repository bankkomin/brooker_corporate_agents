# Source Summary — Coinbase Institutional H1 2021 in Review

_As of July 2021 (covering H1 2021). Publisher: [[coinbase]] Institutional._

## Document Overview

Coinbase Institutional's semi-annual market review covering one of the most active
periods on record for crypto.

## Key Findings

- Total crypto market cap began 2021 at $769B, peaked at $2.4T in May, ended H1 at
  $1.4T (+86% YTD).
- Coinbase launched its integrated Prime Broker (multi-venue execution, cold-storage
  custody for 120+ assets, financing); acquired skew and Bison Trails.
- Coinbase made its public-market debut in April 2021 via direct listing (NASDAQ: COIN)
  — the largest pure-play crypto platform to do so.
- Institutional investment accelerated; clients transacted nine- and ten-figure trades
  with minimal market impact.
- Covers Bitcoin/Ethereum metrics, ETFs, stablecoins, DeFi, Bitcoin and the
  environment, and crypto + politics.

## Relevance

Documents the institutionalization of crypto in H1 2021 and Coinbase's IPO — core
evidence for [[crypto-as-institutional-asset-class]].
