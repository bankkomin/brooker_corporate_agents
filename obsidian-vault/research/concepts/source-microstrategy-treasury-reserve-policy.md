# Source Summary — MicroStrategy Treasury Reserve Policy & Bitcoin Trading Policy (2020)

**As of: Board-approved 2020; document set dated 4 February 2021**

## Document Overview

Two governance documents from MicroStrategy Incorporated's corporate-Bitcoin program: the
**Amended and Restated Treasury Reserve Policy** and its Exhibit A, the **Bitcoin Trading
Policy**. Together they set out how a public company holds BTC as its primary treasury
reserve asset with appropriate controls. Part of the wider
[[corporate-bitcoin-treasury-playbook]].

## Key Findings

**Treasury Reserve Policy**
- BTC is designated the Company's **primary Treasury Reserve Asset**, with the objective
  of holding a majority of Treasury Reserve Assets in BTC, subject to market conditions.
- Two precautionary thresholds: a **Working Capital Threshold** (cash held for day-to-day
  needs; Board approval required to adjust) and a **Cash Reserve Threshold** (minimum cash
  the Designated Officers maintain; Board approval required to lower below a set floor).
- **Designated Officers** pre-approve every transaction via a Transaction Approval Form,
  with pre-transaction legal/tax/accounting review and Investments Committee consultation
  above rolling acquisition increments.
- BTC must be held with **Permitted Custodians** (the Company never self-custodies);
  transactions only with Permitted Brokers / Permitted OTC Counterparties.
- **Prohibited Activities** include transmitting/storing BTC for others, customer
  exchange business, mining/issuing BTC, front-running, and price manipulation.
- Records retained ≥6 years; detailed broker/custodian/OTC evaluation criteria in
  Exhibit E (including "qualified custodian" status and UCC Article 8 treatment).

**Bitcoin Trading Policy**
- Applies to all directors, officers, and employees ("Covered Persons") with access to
  BTC plans/activities — an insider-trading-style control for BTC.
- **Non-Trading Periods** are imposed whenever the Company transacts in BTC or material
  non-public information ("MNPI") is likely; no personal BTC trading on MNPI; no tipping.
- Prohibits market manipulation and conduct adverse to the Company; violations can lead
  to termination and criminal/civil liability.

## Relevance

A reference template for governing a corporate digital-asset treasury — directly relevant
to Brooker's digital-asset treasury (Engine 3 of the [[three-engine-model]]) and to the
custody/counterparty controls a treasury function requires.
