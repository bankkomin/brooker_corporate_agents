# Source Summary — Brooker Fund Raising Advisory Mandate Template (2022)

**As of: 24 October 2022**

## Document Overview

A **template engagement letter** by which The Brooker Group Plc is appointed to advise a
client on a fund-raising transaction. A reusable advisory-mandate contract template (with
bracketed placeholders for client-specific terms).

## Key Findings

- **Scope.** Brooker assists in fund-raising in additional rounds and introduces strategic
  partners adding value (branding, marketing, networking, ecosystem).
- **Brooker's roles.** Review the business plan, corporate structure, tokenomics, budget
  and financial projections; advise on terms acceptable to investors; advise on valuation
  and assist negotiation; coordinate legal advisors and closing; recommend strategic
  partnerships.
- **Fee structure (success-based):**
  - Under US$35M equity valuation: **5.00%** of total equity or token amount, due on
    signing of the shareholder/partnership agreement.
  - Over US$100M equity valuation: **3.00%** of total equity or token amount.
  - Fees exclude out-of-pocket expenses and third-party fees; VAT added.
- **Term.** Effective on signing, valid 24 months with automatic extension by agreement;
  **exclusive** for the term. Because fees are largely success-based, the client agrees
  not to terminate without Brooker's written consent.
- Brooker reserves the right to assign the contract to a subsidiary within the Group.

## Relevance

A reference template for Brooker's own advisory engagements — directly relevant to Engine
2 (Advisory) of the [[three-engine-model]]. The success-fee schedule documents Brooker's
standard fund-raising advisory economics. See [[fund-raising-advisory-economics]].
