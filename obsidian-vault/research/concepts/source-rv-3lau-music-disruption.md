# Source Summary — Real Vision: The World Famous DJ Disrupting the Music Industry

**As of: 16 December 2021** · Justin Blau (3LAU) & Raoul Pal · Real Vision transcript

## Document Overview

A Real Vision interview with DJ Justin Blau (3LAU) on tokenising music and disrupting the
music industry from the inside.

## Key Findings

- 3LAU's journey from festival headliner to tokenisation pioneer.
- Argues the future of music is "a joint venture between artists and their fans" — a step
  toward "Universal Basic Equity," with NFTs/tokens letting fans share in artists' upside.

## Relevance

A flagship music case study for [[nft-creator-economy]] — direct artist-to-fan
monetisation.
