# Source Summary — Community-Based Content / Jenkins the Valet (Real Vision)

_As of 3 June 2022. Publisher: [[real-vision]]. Interview: Tally Labs co-CEOs & Raoul Pal._

## Document Overview

A Real Vision interview transcript with the co-CEOs of Tally Labs ("SEE APE FOLLOW APE"
and "Valet Jones") — founders of The Writer's Room, a media company built around the
IP rights of the Bored Ape "Jenkins the Valet."

## Key Findings

- Web3 and NFTs enable a new model of community-owned IP and "content for and by the
  people."
- Jenkins the Valet — a single Bored Ape NFT — became the basis for a community-driven
  media company.
- Frames the future of content creation and media companies around community-first
  ownership.

## Relevance

A concrete case study of community-owned IP within the [[nft-culture-asset-class]]
thesis.
