# Source Summary — The Aftermath of FTX's Downfall (Delphi)

_As of 11 November 2022. Publisher: [[delphi-digital]] (Crypto Insiders Deep Dive)._

## Document Overview

A Delphi Digital "Crypto Insiders" deep-dive report published the evening of 11 Nov 2022,
analysing the collapse of FTX in real time.

## Key Findings

- Sections: deciphering on-chain flows, collateral damage ("who's left holding the
  bag?"), Solana caught in the crossfire, impact on NFT markets, where the market
  stands, and the silver lining for DeFi.
- One of the worst weeks in crypto history; the report assesses contagion and
  forward implications.
- Frames DeFi's transparency as a potential beneficiary of the CeFi blow-up.

## Relevance
