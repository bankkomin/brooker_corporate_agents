# Tokenization of Real-World Assets (RWA)

## Summary

Tokenization is the thesis that all money and contracts will, at scale, migrate onto
public blockchains — turning real-world assets (treasuries, credit, equities, funds)
into programmable, 24/7-tradable on-chain tokens. ARK frames public blockchains as one
of five converging innovation platforms; Coinbase calls crypto "the native execution
rail" for an agent-led economy. Stablecoins are the first and largest RWA category and
the proof of concept.

## Historical Context

Early RWA experiments appeared in 2022 (Goldfinch emerging-market credit, Centrifuge).
Messari's *Crypto Theses for 2024* flagged RWA diversification as a top DeFi trend and
stablecoins as "the new TINA trade." By 2026 Coinbase's earnings deck positioned
tokenization and stablecoins alongside trading as the three engines of the "onchain
economy," with AI agents as the accelerant.
