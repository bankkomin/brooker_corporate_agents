# Source Summary — NFT Market State / ENS, DeGods (Delphi, Jul 2022)

_As of 6 July 2022. Publisher: [[delphi-digital]] (Crypto Insiders Retail). Author: Teng Yan._

## Document Overview

A Delphi Digital retail NFT report covering the state of the NFT market and project
spotlights (ENS, Nina's Super Cool World, DeGods).

## Key Findings

- NFT trading volumes at a yearly low — OpenSea June 2022 volume was $829M, the lowest
  since July 2021 and less than a quarter of August 2021.
- Unique buyers fell for a second consecutive month — a significant number of
  participants had left the space.
- Average OpenSea transaction value fell sharply to ~$290 (~0.25 ETH) as activity
  centred on free mints and newer projects rather than "blue chip" collections.
- Over the long term, mainstream adoption is expected to bring larger volumes of
  lower-priced NFTs.
