# Source Summary — Goldman Sachs: Overview of Digital Assets and Blockchain (Asia)

_As of November 2021. Publisher: Goldman Sachs (Asia) L.L.C. — see [[goldman-sachs]]._

## Document Overview

A Goldman Sachs (Asia) primer on digital assets and blockchain — global engagement,
financial-infrastructure transformation, and opportunities in the Asian digital-asset
space.

## Key Findings

- Traces blockchain's evolution in four eras:
  - **1.0 (2008-13)** — Bitcoin; blockchain as cryptocurrency infrastructure.
  - **2.0 (2013-17)** — Ethereum and smart contracts; wider financial-services adoption.
  - **3.0 (2017-20)** — ICO boom; consortium/permissioned chains; B2B → B2C.
  - **4.0 (2020+)** — NFTs, play-to-earn, metaverse; governments consider national
    crypto adoption.
- Three-layer application roadmap: underlying technology → platform infrastructure
  (Ethereum, Hyperledger Fabric, R3 Corda) → commercial DApps.

## Relevance

A TradFi taxonomy of the blockchain landscape; useful framing for Asia-focused
digital-asset strategy and [[tokenization-real-world-assets]].
