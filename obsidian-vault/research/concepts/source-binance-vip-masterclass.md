# Source Summary — Binance VIP Retreat Masterclass: Earn, Futures, Spot & Margin (2025)

**As of: 9 June 2025** (three masterclass decks from the Binance VIP Retreat)

## Document Overview

Three presentation decks from Binance's **VIP Retreat Masterclass** series, covering
Binance's **Earn**, **Futures**, and **Spot & Margin** product lines for VIP /
institutional clients. See the entity [[binance]].

## Key Findings

**Earn — passive yield**
- Binance Simple Earn: Flexible products (instant subscribe/redeem, real-time APR,
  ~1–10% projected), Locked products (14–150 day terms, ~1–15% projected), and ETH/SOL
  Liquid Staking (WBETH / BNSOL, ~3–8% projected). LSTs auto-compound and are usable as
  collateral. VIP-exclusive higher yields and quotas.

**Futures — leverage & derivatives**
- Binance Futures launched September 2019; became the largest crypto derivatives exchange
  in September 2020; passed a $100T cumulative milestone in September 2024.
- Linear (USDⓈ-margined) and Inverse (coin-margined) contracts plus stablecoin Options;
  $78B+ average 24-hour volume, $30B+ open interest, 700+ contracts.

**Spot & Margin**
- Spot trading since July 2017; margin trading added December 2023; FIX API added.
- VIP fee tiers offer up to 77% discount at VIP 9 (71% at VIP 5); an additional 25%
  discount for paying fees in BNB.
- Margin offers up to 20x leverage; 1,400+ spot/margin pairs; a **Funding Rate Arbitrage
  bot**, grid/TWAP trading bots, and spot + futures testnet environments.

## Relevance

A counterparty-capability reference for Binance as a potential digital-asset treasury and
trading counterparty — its native-yield products, derivatives depth, fee structure, and
the Funding Rate Arbitrage tooling that supports the
[[market-neutral-funding-rate-arbitrage]] strategy.
