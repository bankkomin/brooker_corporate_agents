# Source Summary — CoinGecko Q2 2021 Cryptocurrency Report

_As of Q2 2021 (published 13 Sept 2021). Publisher: [[coingecko]]._

## Document Overview

CoinGecko's Q2 2021 quarterly. The total crypto market cap cooled after a May peak of
~$2.5T, with sharp 20-30% intraday drawdowns and cascading liquidations.

## Key Findings

- **Meme tokens** (Dogecoin and "doggo" coins) were the quarter's highlight, fuelled by
  Elon Musk.
- **DeFi expanded beyond Ethereum** — Polygon surged on TVL; Uniswap v3 launched
  concentrated liquidity; protocols began tokenizing future yield; Compound Treasury
  and Aave Pro signalled institutional entry.
- **Axie Infinity** pioneered play-to-earn — "a whole new economy."
- China banned crypto mining; El Salvador adopted Bitcoin as legal tender.

## Relevance

Documents the play-to-earn breakout ([[crypto-gaming-play-to-earn]]) and the
multichain-DeFi expansion.
