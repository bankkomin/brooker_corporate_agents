# Source Summary — Delphi: Nouns — Hyperscaling a Brand & Treasury from Zero

**As of: 17 August 2022** · Delphi Digital · Crypto Insiders "In Focus"

## Document Overview

A Delphi "Crypto Insiders In Focus" report on Nouns — an NFT project that bootstrapped a
brand and on-chain treasury from zero.

## Key Findings

- Nouns auctions exactly one NFT per day, perpetually; proceeds flow into an on-chain DAO
  treasury that funds brand-building and public goods.
- CC0 (public-domain) IP lets anyone build on the Nouns brand, accelerating reach.
- A model for "hyperscaling a brand and treasury from zero" — NFT as community-owned
  brand engine.

## Relevance

A flagship case study for [[nft-creator-economy]] (brand-from-zero) and
