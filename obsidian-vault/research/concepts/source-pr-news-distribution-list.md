# Source Summary — PR News-Site Distribution List (2023)

**As of: 4 January 2023**

## Document Overview

A single-sheet spreadsheet listing **news and finance websites** used as a press-release
distribution / syndication target list — an operational PR resource rather than a research
report.

## Key Findings

- A long list (several hundred entries) of news, finance, and crypto outlets and
  syndication networks — including major outlets (Yahoo Finance, Bloomberg, MarketWatch,
  Associated Press, Morningstar, Benzinga, Investing.com) and large numbers of regional
  US local-news domains and newswire syndication networks (EIN News, Global Newswire,
  Franklyinc / Newsnetmedia networks).
- Includes crypto-specific outlets (NewsBTC, Coin Journal, Bitcoin Insider, Blockster,
  NFTculture).

## Relevance

An operational reference for the reach of a press-release distribution effort — useful
context for how Brooker's PR / investor-communications activity is syndicated. Limited
analytical content; held mainly for completeness of the Counterparties collection.
