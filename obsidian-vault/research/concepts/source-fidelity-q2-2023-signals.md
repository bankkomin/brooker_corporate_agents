# Source Summary — Fidelity Digital Assets Q2 2023 Signals Report

**As of: 30 June 2023** · Fidelity Digital Assets Research · quarterly on-chain report

## Document Overview

A quarterly on-chain signals report scoring Bitcoin and Ethereum market conditions across
three time horizons (short <1yr, mid 1–5yr, long >5yr) using a curated metric toolkit.

## Key Findings

- **Bitcoin: Positive across all three horizons.** Up ~85% YTD; on-chain metrics
  normalising after the ordinals/BRC-20 experimentation. Long-term holders accumulating
  around $30,000; miners in a healthy profit margin (Puell Multiple).
- **Ethereum: Positive short and long, Neutral mid-term.** Up ~62% YTD; net-negative
  issuance since The Merge removed >700,000 ETH; validator count +15% in Q2; new-address
  momentum recovering.
- Halving flagged as the key BTC metric to watch (<9 months out at time of writing).
