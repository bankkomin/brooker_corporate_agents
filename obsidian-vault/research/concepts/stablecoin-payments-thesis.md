# Stablecoin Payments & Infrastructure Thesis

## Summary

A 2025-era thesis recorded most fully in the Kazea Stablecoin Velocity Fund pitchbook
([[source-kazea-stablecoin-velocity-fund]]): that stablecoins are a "once in a generation
opportunity" with the clearest product-market fit in crypto, set to disrupt global
payments and financial services.

## Core Argument

1. **Demonstrated traction.** Stablecoin supply has reached US$270B+; ~45M monthly active
   users; ~100M addresses hold stablecoins. Stablecoins are a top-20 holder of US
   Treasuries (ahead of Germany) and overtook Visa in transaction volume in 2024.
2. **Real-world frictions solved.** Near-instant, near-free cross-border remittances;
   24/7/365 settlement; transparency for regulators; a de-facto USD account in countries
   with volatile currencies.
3. **Where value accrues.** The investable stack concentrates at the Applications layer
   (stablecoin neobanks, RWA apps, on-chain lending/treasury) and Infra/Aggregation layer
   (on/off-ramp orchestration, B2B payouts).
4. **Emerging frontier — agentic payments.** Stablecoins are positioned as the
   machine-native payment rail for AI agents transacting online — the bridge to the AI
   convergence thesis ([[source-state-of-ai-messari-2025]]).
5. **Regulatory tailwind.** New regulations (and statements from Goldman Sachs and the
   US Treasury Secretary) are expected to drive a "stablecoin gold rush".

## Why It Matters

Stablecoin settlement is an explicit advisory line in Brooker's [[three-engine-model]]
(Engine 2). This thesis frames both the advisory opportunity and a VCC fund-of-funds
deal-flow theme (Kazea being a worked example of a single-theme stablecoin VC fund).
