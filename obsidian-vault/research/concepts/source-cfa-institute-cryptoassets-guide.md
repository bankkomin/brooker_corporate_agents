# Source Summary — CFA Institute: Cryptoassets Guide

_As of 2021. Publisher: [[cfa-institute]] Research Foundation. Authors: Matt Hougan & David Lawant (Bitwise)._

## Document Overview

A practitioner education brief — *Cryptoassets: The Guide to Bitcoin, Blockchain, and
Cryptocurrency for Investment Professionals* — aimed at financial advisers and
professional investors. Qualifies for 1.75 CFA Professional Learning credits.

## Key Findings

- Five parts: (I) how crypto works, (II) the crypto landscape, (III) crypto as an
  investment opportunity, (IV) crypto in a portfolio setting, (V) key risks.
- Frames Bitcoin from two perspectives: a solution to a technical problem (peer-to-peer
  value transfer without intermediaries) and an economic phenomenon.
- Notes the poor quality of crypto market information (even basic volume data) as a
  core challenge for due diligence.
- Total cryptoasset market cap cited at >$350B (Sept 2020).

## Relevance

A credentialed, balanced reference for the [[crypto-as-institutional-asset-class]]
thesis — a neutral counterweight to bull-case publisher research.
