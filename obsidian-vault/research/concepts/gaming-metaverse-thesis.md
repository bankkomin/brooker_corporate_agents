# Gaming, Metaverse & Play-to-Earn Thesis

## Summary

A 2022-era investment thesis recorded most fully in the Spartan Gaming & Metaverse Fund
pitchbook ([[source-spartan-fund-pitchbooks]]): that blockchain-based gaming and open
metaverses represent "the largest opportunity ever in crypto", spawning a digital
ecosystem where people earn, play, and socialize.

## Core Argument

1. **Natural progression.** Gaming/metaverse applications follow digital money (Bitcoin)
   and decentralized finance (DeFi) as the next wave of crypto adoption, attracting
   millions of new users.
2. **What changed.** Play-to-earn mechanics let players earn while playing; NFTs give
   true ownership of digital items (avatars, metaverse land); a talent influx and
   improved infrastructure (NFT marketplaces, scalable chains) enable richer experiences.
3. **Market scale.** ~2.7B gamers worldwide; the gaming sector valued at $300B+ across
   direct ($200B) and indirect ($100B) revenue — expected to migrate to Web3 platforms.
4. **The open metaverse** is expected to subsume much of today's Web2 gaming, e-commerce,
   and social-media activity.

## Caveats (from the same era's red-flags discipline)

The Pitchbook collection's own red-flags list flags P2E tokens for runaway supply
inflation (citing DragonLand, Tryhards). The thesis is preserved as a point-in-time view
from the 2022 market peak — read alongside the cautionary record.
