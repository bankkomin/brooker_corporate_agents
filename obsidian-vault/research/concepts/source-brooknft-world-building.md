# Source Summary — BrookNFT World-Building Lore (2023)

**As of: 19 October 2023**

## Document Overview

An internal **brainstorming / world-building document** for "BrookNFT" — a proposed NFT
project with an original fantasy lore universe called **Cryspira**.

## Key Findings

- **Premise.** Bitcoin mining's massive energy use (~200 TWh cited) triggers a quantum
  energy portal into a new world, "Cryspira", which is terraformed by the energy flow.
  Creatures pulled in from Earth absorb "cryptic energy" ("cryptomacy") and become magical
  monsters; some become humanoid.
- **Creative constraints.** Bull humanoid tribe, Bear humanoid tribe, Flame humanoid
  elders (the "Brooklingz"). A 7-state elemental magic system (prime, aqua, solid, hazel,
  tron, emerald, ignite). A lost "Sato" civilization with treasures/inscriptions that
  enable cryptomagic.
- **Structure.** The document sketches a wiki navigation menu — regions (Vereoran Plains,
  Wispweald Forests, etc.), a city ("Brookhammer Hold"), creatures ("Brooklingz"), and
  celestial-beast events.

## Relevance

A point-in-time record of Brooker's exploratory NFT / gaming-IP brainstorming — connects
to the [[gaming-metaverse-thesis]] and to the in-house hackathon concepts
([[source-brooker-hackathon-concepts]]). Preserved as an early-stage creative artifact;
not a developed product specification.
