# Source Summary — Coinbase Q4 & FY2025 Shareholder Letter

_As of 12 February 2026 (covering Q4 & FY2025). Publisher: [[coinbase]]._

## Document Overview

Coinbase's Q4 and full-year 2025 shareholder letter — a strong operational year with
guidance met or beaten every quarter.

## Key Findings

- Coinbase Total Trading Volume grew 156% to $5.2T in 2025; crypto trading volume market
  share doubled.
- >12% of all crypto worldwide resided on Coinbase in 2025.
- Record stablecoin revenue, driven by all-time highs in USDC held in Coinbase products.
- 12 products generate >$100M annualized revenue; two generate >$1B.
- $11.3B cash position; 8.2M shares repurchased since Nov 2025; +$2.0B buyback
  authorization (Jan 2026).
- 2026 priorities: grow the "Everything Exchange," scale stablecoins/payments, bring the
  world onchain (DeFi, Base App, Base Chain).
