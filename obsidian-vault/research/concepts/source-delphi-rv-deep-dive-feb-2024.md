# Source Summary — Macro Insiders Deep Dive with Raoul Pal (Feb 2024)

_As of February 2024. Publisher: [[real-vision]] (Macro Insiders, with Raoul Pal)._

## Document Overview

A Real Vision "Macro Insiders" deep dive applying Global Macro Investor's macro
framework — business cycle and seasonal effects — to crypto for the coming 12 months.

## Key Findings

- A "dynamically positive" outlook: BTC consolidating after the spot-ETF launch and the
  absorption of FTX/Celsius/GBTC selling overhang.
- The BTC ETF launch was a "defining moment" but was front-run; a retail wave is
  expected as platform/execution risk falls.
- Forecasts BTC moving toward the top of its valuation corridor (>$100k); higher-beta
  ETH and SOL to outperform; an ETH ETF likely before summer 2024.
- AltCoins added ~$250B of market cap in Q4 2023; a 5x expansion seen as plausible.

## Relevance

A current macro-overlay bull case; supports [[crypto-cycles-and-liquidity]] and the
post-ETF [[bitcoin-digital-gold-thesis]].
