# Source Summary — SkyBridge: Bitcoin Foundations

_As of January 2021. Publisher: [[skybridge-capital]] (SkyBridge Bitcoin Fund L.P.)._

## Document Overview

An investor-education deck accompanying the launch of the SkyBridge Bitcoin Fund L.P.,
making the institutional case for a Bitcoin allocation.

## Key Findings

- "Why Bitcoin Now": an emerging asset class de-risked over three years; attractive
  supply/demand dynamics (supply growing ~2.5%/yr, demand faster).
- Near-zero rates and money printing pose an existential risk to the 60/40 portfolio,
  placing a premium on scarce assets (gold, real estate, art, Bitcoin).
- "Bitcoin is digital gold — better at being gold than gold."
- Frames money as evolving technology: barter → shells → metals → paper → electronic →
  Bitcoin.
- SkyBridge funds held a ~$272M Bitcoin position as of January 2021.

## Relevance

An early, clean articulation of the [[bitcoin-digital-gold-thesis]] from an institutional
allocator.
