# Source Summary — CoinGecko 2021 Year End Cryptocurrency Report

_As of year-end 2021. Publisher: [[coingecko]]._

## Document Overview

CoinGecko's 2021 annual report — a full-year review of the crypto market's biggest year
to date.

## Key Findings

- Industry market cap grew >3x in 2021 to ~$2.4T (briefly $3T ATH).
- Bitcoin ended ~$48,000 (2x for the year) but dominance fell below 40% as attention
  shifted to Ethereum, L2s, and new L1s (Solana, Terra).
- DeFi TVL rose to $234B. **NFTs were the year's killer app** — total traded volume
  $23.9B — vaulting crypto into mainstream consciousness alongside the metaverse
  ("Meta" rebrand).
- US Bitcoin ETFs approved; El Salvador made Bitcoin legal tender; China mining ban.

## Relevance

The definitive full-year 2021 market record; anchors the NFT/metaverse and
multichain-L1 narratives.
