# Source Summary — JPMorgan: Opportunities in the Metaverse

**As of: February 2022** · JPMorgan Onyx · business strategy report

## Document Overview

A JPMorgan Onyx report on how businesses can explore the metaverse and navigate hype vs
reality.

## Key Findings

- The metaverse is "a seamless convergence of physical and digital lives" — not one world
  but many; market opportunity estimated at >$1 trillion in yearly revenue.
- Distinguishes a **Web2 metaverse** (Second Life, Roblox, Fortnite — closed) from a
  **Web3 metaverse** (Decentraland, The Sandbox — token-governed, user-owned).
- Brands of all sizes entering: Walmart, Nike, Gap, Verizon, Hulu, PWC, Adidas, Atari.
- Metaverse offers five activity types: Transact, Socialize, Create, Own, Experience.
