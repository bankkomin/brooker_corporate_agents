# Source Summary — Real World Crypto Loans / Goldfinch (Delphi)

_As of 30 May 2022. Publisher: [[delphi-digital]]._

## Document Overview

A Delphi Digital free report combining a "Chart of the Day" on Goldfinch Protocol with
a trading-psychology excerpt on navigating choppy selloffs.

## Key Findings

- Goldfinch is a credit protocol enabling uncollateralized loans to real-world
  businesses and trusts, flowing toward underbanked emerging-market borrowers.
- Borrowers are vetted by GFI-staking "Auditors" (incentivized via rewards/slashing).
- ~$100M served over the prior year at ~98% utilization. Two pools: Senior
  (lower-risk, diversified, first-loss priority) and Backer (higher-risk, ~20% of
  Senior yield).
- Caveat: these are effectively high-yield bonds with default risk; microloan TAM
  ~$343B by 2027.

## Relevance

An early concrete example of on-chain real-world credit — primary source for
[[defi-credit-uncollateralized-lending]] and an RWA case for [[tokenization-real-world-assets]].
