# Source Summary — ATOM 2.0: Building the Hub's Economy (Delphi)

_As of 13 October 2022. Publisher: [[delphi-digital]]. Author: "Ceteris."_

## Document Overview

A Delphi Digital deep dive on the ATOM 2.0 whitepaper and the plan to build out the
Cosmos Hub's economy.

## Key Findings

- **USDC Consumer Chain**: a generic asset-issuance chain (USDC first) secured by ATOM
  validators/stakers — every transaction pays fees to ATOM, addressing the Cosmos
  ecosystem's stablecoin-liquidity gap (~$85M total, only ~$16M native).
- Continued focus on Interchain Security (ICS) and liquid staking.
- ATOM's issuance schedule overhauled.
- **Interchain Allocator** — the Hub as a fund; **Interchain Scheduler** — tokenizing
  MEV.
- Three pillars of ATOM 2.0: Money, Productivity, Asset-Backing.
