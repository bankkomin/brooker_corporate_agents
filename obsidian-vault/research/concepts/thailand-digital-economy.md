# Thailand Digital Economy & Technology Outlook

## Summary

A concept drawn from the Thailand foresight study
([[source-thailand-digital-technology-foresight-2035]]): a structured view of which
technologies and megatrends will shape Thailand's digital economy through 2035 — the
country-level context for Brooker, a Thai-listed group.

## The Outlook

**Megatrends shaping Thailand (11):** explosive data generation, the Fourth Industrial
Revolution, smart ecosystems, urbanization/megacities, e-commerce growth, increasing
connectedness, workplace transformation, the shift in global economic power, aging
society, resource scarcity, and climate change.

**Seven shortlisted technologies** judged most material to Thailand:

| Technology | Role for Thailand |
|------------|-------------------|
| Internet of Things | Smart ecosystems, smart farms, connected infrastructure |
| Artificial Intelligence | Productivity, automation of cognitive tasks |
| Data Analytics | Business intelligence on an explosion of data |
| Next-Generation Telecom | Connectivity backbone |
| Distributed Ledger Technology | Financial and supply-chain infrastructure |
| Quantum Computing | Long-horizon compute capability |
| Automation | Robotics/RPA; a major GDP and productivity driver |

## Why It Matters

Brooker operates from Thailand; this outlook frames the country-level demand for the
technologies Brooker advises on (AI implementation, tokenization/DLT, automation) under
Engine 2 of the [[three-engine-model]]. It localizes the global
[[ark-disruptive-innovation-thesis]] to the Thai market.
