# Source Summary — CoinGecko Q3 2021 Cryptocurrency Report

_As of Q3 2021 (published 28 Oct 2021). Publisher: [[coingecko]]._

## Document Overview

CoinGecko's Q3 2021 quarterly. Total crypto market cap clawed back to ~$1.7T after the
May correction.

## Key Findings

- China's Q3 crackdown — a full ban on crypto mining and transactions — forced miners
  and exchanges out of the country (the "great mining migration").
- "NFTs are redefining value and culture" — profile-picture (PFP) collections led the
  JPEG trend; Loot (for Adventurers) launched.
- DeFi multichain dominance grew; Solana and Avalanche ecosystems profiled.

## Relevance

Documents the China mining ban — pivotal for [[bitcoin-mining-economics]] — and the PFP
NFT wave ([[nft-culture-asset-class]]).
