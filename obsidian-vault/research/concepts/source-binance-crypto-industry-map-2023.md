# Source Summary — Navigating Crypto: Industry Map (Binance, Mar 2023)

_As of March 2023. Publisher: [[binance-research]]._

## Document Overview

A Binance Research industry-mapping report segmenting the crypto landscape into core
ecosystems and topical sectors.

## Key Findings

- **Core ecosystems**: Infrastructure, DeFi, NFT, Gaming.
- **Topical sectors**: Real World Assets, Decentralized Social (DeSoc), Artificial
  Intelligence, Decentralized Science (DeSci), DAO Tooling.
- A reference taxonomy for understanding where crypto projects sit in the ecosystem.

## Relevance

A useful classification framework; the RWA and AI sectors foreshadow later theses
([[tokenization-real-world-assets]]).
