# Source Summary — The Block Research 2022 Digital Asset Outlook

**As of: December 2021 / January 2022** · The Block Research, commissioned by GSR

## Document Overview

The Block Research's annual outlook — a comprehensive look back at 2021 by the numbers and
forward to 2022. Covers state of the market, digital-asset venture investment, DeFi,
blockchain gaming/NFTs, multi-chain L1s, Web3 and crypto financial services.

## Key Findings

- **2021 by the numbers**: record market performance, on-chain volumes, stablecoin
  growth, mining state and miner revenue, spot volumes, Grayscale, BTC/ETH derivatives,
  and growing US regulatory effort.
- **Venture investment overview**: a surge in later-stage deals and growth capital; an
  "explosion of crypto unicorns"; later-stage rounds spread across DeFi, NFTs/Gaming, L1s,
  Web3, crypto financial services and trading/brokerage.
- Sectorisation of seed / pre-Series A activity by vertical.
- The "rise of a multi-chain future and L1s" as a structural 2022 theme.

## Relevance

A data-anchored snapshot of the 2021 peak in crypto venture funding and market activity —
useful as a quantitative baseline against which the 2022 downturn (Terra, credit crisis)
can be measured. Cross-links to [[institutional-crypto-adoption]], [[web3-ownership-thesis]].
