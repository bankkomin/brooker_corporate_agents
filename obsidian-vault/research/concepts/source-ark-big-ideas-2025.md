# Source Summary — ARK Invest Big Ideas 2025

_As of 4 February 2025. Publisher: [[ark-invest]]. Data as of 31 December 2024._

## Document Overview

ARK's flagship annual research report. 11 "Big Ideas" across five converging innovation
platforms: AI, Robotics, Energy Storage, Public Blockchains, Multiomic Sequencing. The
crypto-relevant chapters are Convergence, AI Agents, Bitcoin, Stablecoins and Scaling
Blockchains. ARK forecasts disruptive innovation could command >two-thirds of global
equity market cap by 2030 (38% CAGR), and global real GDP growth accelerating to ~7.3%
by 2030.

## Key Findings

- **Public blockchains** are one of five innovation platforms: at scale, "all money and
  contracts will likely migrate onto Public Blockchains."
- **Bitcoin 2024 milestones**: spot Bitcoin ETF launch (most successful ETF launch in
  history — >$4B first-day inflows); fourth halving cut issuance to ~0.9%/yr; hashrate
  and price hit all-time highs; market-cap dominance surged past 55%.
- **Bitcoin risk-adjusted returns** remained superior to major asset classes — 2024
  return 122.2%, Sharpe 1.4, 5-year CAGR 67.2%; annualized volatility fell to an
  all-time low.
- **AI Agents**: agents could add $50-200/user enterprise value to digital wallets;
  crypto positioned as a settlement rail for agent-led commerce.

## Relevance

ARK's most current full innovation thesis in this set. Directly supports the
[[bitcoin-digital-gold-thesis]] (post-ETF, post-halving) and [[tokenization-real-world-assets]].
