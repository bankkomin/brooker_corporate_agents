# Source Summary — Spartan Group Fund Pitchbooks (2022)

**As of: February 2022 (Global Blockchain Opportunities Fund) and August 2022 (Gaming & Metaverse Fund)**

## Document Overview

Two investor decks for funds managed by **Spartan Capital**, the digital-asset management
unit of The Spartan Group: the **Spartan Global Blockchain Opportunities Fund** (an
open-ended liquid hedge fund) and the **Spartan Gaming & Metaverse Fund** (a closed-end
venture fund, the marketing name for Spartan Venture Fund II — see
[[source-spartan-venture-fund-ii-legal-docs]]). See the firm entity [[spartan-group]].

## Key Findings

**The Spartan Group**
- Founded 2017; a leading crypto-native asset-management and advisory group; founders are
  ex-Goldman Sachs bankers and VCs. As of 31 July 2022, managing 3 funds + 1 managed
  account with combined AUM of ~US$350M.

**Spartan Global Blockchain Opportunities Fund** (open-ended)
- A liquid hedge fund with a fundamental discretionary long-short strategy; launched
  August 2018; AUM grew from $9M to ~$100–154M.
- Net return since inception: **+521%** vs the Crypto Currencies Index 30 at +53% (as of
  31 July 2022).

**Spartan Gaming & Metaverse Fund** (Spartan Venture Fund II)
- Thesis: gaming and metaverse applications are "the largest opportunity ever in crypto",
  driven by play-to-earn, NFT true-ownership, talent influx, and improved infrastructure.
- Expected fund size $200M; GP commitment $25M; targeting 100–150 projects.
- Track-record MOICs cited: Axie Infinity 70x, Merit Circle 28x, Genopets 24x, LayerZero
  20x, Project Galaxy 11x.

## Relevance

Worked examples of a crypto hedge fund and a thematic (gaming/metaverse) venture fund —
relevant to Brooker's VCC fund-of-funds (Engine 1 of the [[three-engine-model]]) as both
deal-flow reference and a model for fund construction. Also the primary record of the
[[gaming-metaverse-thesis]].
