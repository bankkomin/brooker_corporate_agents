# Source Summary — Digital Asset Insights H1 2024 (CME x Glassnode)

_As of H1 2024 (data through 31 May 2024). Publishers: CME Group & [[glassnode]]._

## Document Overview

A joint CME Group / Glassnode report providing institutional investors a structured
guide to the digital-asset landscape — market overview, capital flows, market cycles,
derivatives fundamentals, and institutional adoption.

## Key Findings

- Digital assets have "transitioned from a niche, exotic investment to an established
  asset class."
- The approval of spot Bitcoin ETFs and their record-breaking inflows is cited as
  evidence of maturation.
- Emphasises that investors need to understand crypto's idiosyncratic risks — asset
  fundamentals, market cycles, and on-chain behavioural data.
- CME positioned as the institutional derivatives venue (Bitcoin futures since 2017).

## Relevance

A credentialed, institution-facing affirmation of the
[[crypto-as-institutional-asset-class]] thesis, post-spot-ETF.
