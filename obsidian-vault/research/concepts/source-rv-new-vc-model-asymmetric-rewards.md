# Source Summary — Real Vision: The Future Is Happening Fast

**As of: 12 April 2022** · Anthony Pompliano & Raoul Pal · Real Vision transcript

## Document Overview

A Real Vision "Adventures in Crypto" interview — Anthony Pompliano ("Pomp") and Raoul Pal
discuss the digital-asset space and a new venture-capital model based on asymmetric
rewards.

## Key Findings

- Pomp's evolution from Bitcoin proponent to a multi-chain, multi-application investor;
  having invested in ~150 companies, he pivoted toward managing his own capital via a
  family office.
- Discusses a venture model built around asymmetric (long-tail) reward profiles in crypto
  investing.
