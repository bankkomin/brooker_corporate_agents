# Source Summary — Real Vision Pro Crypto: Bitcoin — Absorbing Supply for the Next Leg Higher

**As of: 16 July 2024** · Author: Jamie Coutts (Chief Crypto Analyst, Real Vision)

## Document Overview

A Real Vision Pro Crypto "Deep Dive" — Jamie Coutts argues mid-2024 Bitcoin weakness is an
absorbable, liquidity-cycle buying opportunity.

## Key Findings

- Government BTC sales (German, US) and the Mt. Gox estate distribution weighed on price
  but represent a small, absorbable share of trading volume / realized cap.
- **Supply overhang**: Bitcoin's ~6% overhang is far below the ~29% average for altcoins
  (which carry pre-mine / insider-vesting overhangs); a structural advantage.
- PoW vs PoS nuance: Bitcoin reaches full dilution in a century (inflation only); PoS
  fully-diluted figures understate future variable emissions — so most altcoins' supply
  keeps expanding beyond listed FDMC.
- Introduces a supply-scoring model for asset selection (higher score = less overhang).
- Forward view: as sales are absorbed and catalysts (FTX claims) approach, the setup
  favours strong 12-month returns.
