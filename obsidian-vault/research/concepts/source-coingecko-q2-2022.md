# Source Summary — CoinGecko Q2 2022 Cryptocurrency Report

_As of Q2 2022 (published 26 Sept 2022). Publisher: [[coingecko]]._

## Document Overview

CoinGecko's Q2 2022 quarterly, dominated by the Terra/LUNA collapse and the contagion
that followed.

## Key Findings

- Dedicated "The Fall of Terra & Contagion Effects" section: the UST de-peg ("downwards
  algo un-stablecoin"), the destruction of LUNA, and the chain reaction from Terra/UST
  to Three Arrows Capital (3AC) and CeFi firms "on the brink."
- Ethereum's stETH:ETH price deviation flagged as a contagion vector.
- Broad market drawdown across all top-30 coins.
