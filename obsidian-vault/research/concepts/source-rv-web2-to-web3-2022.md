# Source Summary — From Web 2.0 to Web 3.0 (Real Vision, Mike Dudas)

_As of 14 January 2022. Publisher: [[real-vision]]. Interview: Mike Dudas (6th Man Ventures) & Raoul Pal._

## Document Overview

A Real Vision interview transcript with Mike Dudas — founder of The Block, former Paxos
stablecoin VP, and co-founder of 6th Man Ventures and LinksDAO.

## Key Findings

- Wide-ranging discussion of stablecoins, crypto venture capital, and DAO governance.
- 6th Man Ventures: a Web 3.0 operator fund — Fund 1 ($7.4M, 60+ investments at ~$100k
  checks); Fund 2 raised $125M for larger-scale investing.
- LinksDAO described as building "the world's greatest golf and leisure community" — a
  mix of online membership and a real-world facility.

## Relevance

An early practitioner view of Web 3.0 venture investing and DAO communities — context
for [[web3-ownership-thesis]] and [[dao-governance-and-treasuries]].
