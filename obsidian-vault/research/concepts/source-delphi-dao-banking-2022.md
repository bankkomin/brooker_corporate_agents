# Source Summary — DAO Banking: Beyond Tokens (Delphi)

_As of 30 June 2022. Publisher: [[delphi-digital]] (Crypto Insiders In Focus)._

## Document Overview

A Delphi Digital report on DAO service providers and funding structures — how DAOs can
raise capital beyond simply selling tokens.

## Key Findings

- Tokens are a powerful incentive tool but have been overused by DAOs.
- The first major DAO financing attempt was SUSHI's July 2021 strategic raise
  (unsuccessful); two UMA-proposed options remain worth reconsidering.
- **Porter Finance** expands the capital stack with debt — DAO convertible bonds sold
  via public auction (e.g. Ribbon raised $3M at ~7.1% YTM).
- **Atlendis** offers order-book lending markets where lenders bid rates and DAOs draw
  fixed-rate loans as needed, issued as NFTs.
