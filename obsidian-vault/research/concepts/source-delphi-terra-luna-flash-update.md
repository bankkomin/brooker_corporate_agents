# Source Summary — Delphi/Real Vision: Terra LUNA Flash Update

**As of: 31 May 2022** · Jose Maria Macedo (Delphi Ventures / Delphi Labs) · Real Vision transcript

## Document Overview

A Real Vision flash-update transcript: Delphi Labs head Jose Maria Macedo reflects on the
Terra collapse two weeks on, and looks forward.

## Key Findings

- Delphi has "no strong conviction" on Terra 2.0 — UST was the real differentiator and
  the trust is "completely broken"; any new decentralised stablecoin would have to be
  overcollateralised (capital-inefficient, like DAI).
- **The death spiral is specific** to algorithmic / endogenous-collateral stablecoins —
  not a risk for BTC/ETH. Macedo "personally stays away from algorithmic stablecoins"
  going forward; overcollateralised stablecoins (DAI) proved resilient.
- Crypto proved "anti-fragile" — the $40B unwind was absorbed without systemic contagion;
  institutions with exposure had de-risking checks that accelerated the de-peg.
- **Forward view**: Delphi Labs exploring its own app-chain with cross-chain "outposts"
  (Mars money-market on Osmosis first); very bullish Ethereum (L2s, TVL, developer
  mindshare); the future of DeFi is an integrated single-platform experience.
- Analyst conflicts handled by full transparency; Delphi never accepts payment to write
  research.
