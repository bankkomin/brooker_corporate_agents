# Source Summary — CoinGecko Q1 2021 Cryptocurrency Report

_As of Q1 2021 (published 24 May 2021). Publisher: [[coingecko]]._

## Document Overview

CoinGecko's quarterly crypto industry report covering Q1 2021 — the opening leg of the
2021 bull market. Sections: Market Landscape, Bitcoin, DeFi, NFTs, Exchanges.

## Key Findings

- "NFT hype is real" — first quarter NFTs went mainstream with big-brand entrants and
  high-profile sales; CoinGecko released a "How to Bitcoin" book.
- DeFi ecosystem expanded; primer sections on "Ethereum Killers," Binance Smart Chain,
  Terra, and the rise and fall of algorithmic stablecoins.
- Ethereum scaling solutions introduced as a major theme.

## Relevance

Earliest CoinGecko quarterly in the set; baseline for the 2021 bull cycle and the NFT
mainstreaming documented in [[nft-culture-asset-class]].
