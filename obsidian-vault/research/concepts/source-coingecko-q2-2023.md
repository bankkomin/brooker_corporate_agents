# Source Summary — CoinGecko Q2 2023 Crypto Industry Report

_As of Q2 2023 (published 19 July 2023). Publisher: [[coingecko]]._

## Document Overview

CoinGecko's Q2 2023 quarterly, covering the SEC enforcement wave and the BlackRock spot
Bitcoin ETF filing.

## Key Findings

- The SEC filed lawsuits against Binance and Coinbase (June 2023), naming numerous
  altcoins as securities; markets fell sharply, BTC briefly under $25,000.
- Sentiment reversed when BlackRock — the world's largest asset manager — filed for a
  spot Bitcoin ETF.
- Ethereum's Shapella upgrade turbocharged liquid staking — >17% of ETH supply staked
  by quarter-end; LSDfi emerged as a core DeFi primitive.
- May 2023 memecoin season; MEV and perpetuals analysis.

## Relevance

Documents the regulatory low and the institutional turning point (BlackRock ETF filing)
that fed the [[crypto-as-institutional-asset-class]] thesis.
