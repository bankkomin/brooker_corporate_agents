# Source Summary — SkyBridge Bitcoin Fund LP Presentation

**As of: December 2020 / January 2021** · SkyBridge Capital · fund informational deck

## Document Overview

A marketing/informational presentation for the SkyBridge Bitcoin Fund L.P., launched by
Anthony Scaramucci's SkyBridge Capital. Lays out the firm's case for Bitcoin as an
institutional allocation.

## Key Findings

- **"Why Bitcoin Now"**: an emerging asset class de-risked over three years; growing
  retail + institutional adoption (network effect); near-zero rates and money printing as
  an existential risk to the 60/40 portfolio, placing a premium on scarce assets.
- **"Bitcoin is digital gold — better at being gold than gold"** (echoes the
  [[bitcoin-digital-gold-thesis]]).
- **Money is technology**: barter → beads → metal coins → gold → paper → electronic →
  Bitcoin; good money is scarce, durable, portable, verifiable, divisible.
- Bitcoin described as the most powerful computing network, never hacked; fixed 21M
  supply; ownership on a public, transparent, decentralised ledger (contrasted with DTC).

## Relevance

Evidence of the institutional adoption wave ([[institutional-crypto-adoption]]) — a
traditional alternatives manager launching a dedicated Bitcoin vehicle. Supports
[[bitcoin-digital-gold-thesis]] and [[crypto-portfolio-allocation]].

## Extracted Data

- SkyBridge investment funds held a $272M Bitcoin position at the time of the deck.
- Quotes mutual-fund legend Bill Miller on Bitcoin's supply/demand dynamics.
