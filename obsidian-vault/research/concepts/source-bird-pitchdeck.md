# Source Summary — Bird Investor Relations Pitchdeck (2022)

**As of: 25 April 2022**

## Document Overview

An investor-relations pitchdeck for **Bird**, an early-stage company applying machine
learning to on-chain blockchain data to build predictive-analytics products for Web3.

## Key Findings

- **Premise.** Advancements in machine learning and Web3 will converge, but few
  organizations are combining the two — Bird positions itself as first to bring advanced
  user targeting to the blockchain.
- **Product.** Bird data-mines on-chain behavior to produce predictive-analytics products.
  The flagship "Bird Launchpad Score" classifies investors (e.g. "paper hands" vs
  "diamond hands") so launchpad administrators can offer differentiated IDO terms.
- **Roadmap.** Phase 1 — investor scores for launchpads/IDOs and gaming-platform
  analytics; Phase 2 — credit scores enabling undercollateralized DeFi lending; Phase 3 —
  consumer scores for next-gen targeted marketing.
- **Ethical-data positioning.** Bird proposes an "inclusive, not extractive" model in
  which governance rights and profits are shared back to the users who generate the data
  — contrasted with Web2 data-monetization business models (Google, Facebook).
- **Market signals cited.** TheGraph query volume rose over 800x from Jan 2020 to May
  2021; 86% of businesses report better customer experience through AI.

## Relevance

A worked example of an early-stage ML + Web3 startup pitch — illustrative of the
AI/blockchain convergence thesis ([[ark-disruptive-innovation-thesis]]) and useful as a
deal-flow / due-diligence reference for the [[source-vc-due-diligence-checklist]].
