# Source Summary — Soul Searching: Soulbound Tokens

**As of: October 2022** · Author: Stefan Piech · research report

## Document Overview

A research report on Soulbound Tokens (SBTs) — non-transferable tokens proposed as an
identity and reputation layer for "Decentralised Society."

## Key Findings

- SBTs are non-transferable tokens bound to a wallet ("Soul") — representing credentials,
  affiliations and reputation rather than tradable value.
- Covers design (including "Guardians" for social recovery), technical aspects, and the
  competitive landscape: POAPs, Project Galaxy, RabbitHole, Noox World, DeQuest, and
  Binance Account Bound (BAB).
- Use cases include sybil-resistant governance, undercollateralised lending and verified
  NFT ownership.
