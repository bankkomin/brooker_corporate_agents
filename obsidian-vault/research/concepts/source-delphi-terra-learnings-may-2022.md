# Source Summary — Delphi Digital: Learnings From Last Week (Terra Post-Mortem)

**As of: 18 May 2022** · Delphi Digital · public post-mortem

## Document Overview

Delphi Digital's transparency post and post-mortem following the May 2022 collapse of
Terra's UST stablecoin. Delphi (Ventures, Research, Labs) had significant exposure; the
post discloses involvement and dissects where the thesis went wrong.

## Key Findings

- The UST de-peg wiped out ~$40B across UST and LUNA — "the most catastrophic event since
  Mt Gox." Delphi calls its risk miscalculation "fair" criticism it accepts.
- **Anchor and thesis drift**: Anchor's ~20% fixed APY (reasonable when 3-digit yield
  farms were common) stayed fixed as yields compressed, ballooning UST deposits and the
  reserve deficit. The rebalancing mechanism was abandoned for direct reserve donations —
  "the classic algo stable mistake."
- LFG/BTC reserves were a step toward exogenous collateralisation but did not grow fast
  enough; the callable liability overhang was too large to defend.
- Delphi Labs (most exposed) contributed to Astroport and Mars; never sold tokens it
  held. Astroport became the only functioning exit venue when Terraswap went down.
