# DAO Governance and Treasuries

## Summary

DAOs (Decentralised Autonomous Organisations) feature as the organisational pillar of the
Web3 thesis: token-governed entities that coordinate communities and manage on-chain
treasuries. By 2022 DAOs collectively held >$10B in treasury AUM (a16z).

## Themes

- **Bottom-up governance** of "any kind of organization" — protocols, brands, funds,
  collectives.
- **Treasury composition risk** — most DAO treasury value is held in the protocol's own
  native governance token, concentrating risk (a16z).
- **Brand DAOs** — Nouns demonstrated bootstrapping a brand and treasury from zero via a
  daily NFT auction and CC0 IP.
- **Service providers & funding structures** emerged to support DAO operations (legal
  wrappers, contributor payments, grants).
- **Soulbound tokens (SBTs)** — non-transferable tokens proposed (Vitalik et al.) as a
  reputation/identity layer to enable richer, sybil-resistant governance and
  "Decentralised Society."
