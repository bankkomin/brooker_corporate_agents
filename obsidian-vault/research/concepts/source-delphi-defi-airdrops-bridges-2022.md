# Source Summary — Airdrops, Cross-Chain Bridges & Lending Markets (Delphi)

_As of 25 May 2022. Publisher: [[delphi-digital]] (Crypto Insiders Retail)._

## Document Overview

A Delphi Digital retail-oriented guide to airdrop farming, cross-chain bridges and
lending markets — subtitled "...& the Marshmallow Test."

## Key Findings

- Surveys live and rumoured airdrops: Lens Protocol, Hop Protocol, Optimism's OP token,
  and protocol-specific distributions (Synthetix, Perpetual Protocol, Lyra, Kwenta).
- Covers cross-chain bridging (Celer cBridge) and the mechanics of farming
  opportunities.
- Heavy caution language — airdrop farming carries significant risk.

## Relevance

A practical record of the 2022 airdrop-farming meta; touches token distribution and
value-accrual mechanics ([[token-value-accrual]]).
