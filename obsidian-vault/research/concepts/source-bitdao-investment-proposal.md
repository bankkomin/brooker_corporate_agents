# Source Summary — BitDAO Investment Proposal (2021)

**As of: 18 June 2021** (proposal date; file modified 25 June 2021)

## Document Overview

An internal **investment proposal** recommending a token purchase of BitDAO — a DeFi
treasury/ecosystem DAO seeded by the Bybit crypto derivatives exchange. A worked example
of in-house token-investment analysis and valuation.

## Key Findings

- **Recommendation.** Buy ~1,250,000 BitDAO tokens for US$1,500,000 (≈THB 31,000,000).
- **Project background.** Bybit, a top-5 crypto derivatives exchange (founded 2018; 1M+
  users; Q1 2021 trading volume $1,032B), allocates 2.5 bps of its fee income to BitDAO
  to fund and build DeFi projects. Bybit retains 55% of tokens.
- **Investment highlights.** BitDAO would receive ~$50–100M/month from Bybit fees;
  valuation framed at 8x expected cashflow (P/E ~8.1); lead investors cited as Peter
  Thiel and Pantera, adding PR value; Bybit fees ~4x FTX's burn rate.
- **Restrictions.** 1-year lockup; gradual token distribution linearly over 12 months.
- **Risks identified.** Trading-fee decline (bear market / competition), Bybit changing
  its 2.5 bps contribution, regulatory injunction, pump-and-dump, treasury-asset decline.
- **Valuation conclusion.** Comparing to FTX Token's then ~$8.6B FDV, BitDAO's expected
  ~$1B burn-equivalent implied a $20–30B potential market cap — a posited 2–3x return on
  listing within a year.

## Relevance

A point-in-time example of how a token investment is sized, valued, and risk-assessed —
useful as a template and as a cautionary record (the valuation reasoning is highly
optimistic and pre-dates the 2022 crypto downturn). Pairs with the
[[source-vc-due-diligence-checklist]] red-flags discipline.
