# Source Summary — RV/Delphi: Attention Is All You Need

**As of: 13 May 2024** · Author: Michael Rinko (Delphi) · Real Vision Pro Crypto / Crypto Insiders Deep Dive

## Document Overview

A Delphi "Crypto Insiders" deep dive (with Real Vision) arguing that **attention** is the
most valuable yet misunderstood economic resource, and examining its role in markets.

## Key Findings

- Three dimensions of the attention economy:
  1. How Big Tech **monetized** attention (the Web2 advertising model).
  2. How crypto **tokenizes** attention (tokens/memecoins as attention markets).
  3. Why AI will **disintermediate** attention.
- Frames memecoins and token speculation as markets for attention rather than
  fundamentals — a lens on crypto market behaviour.

## Relevance

A more speculative, narrative-economics deep dive; relevant to [[web3-ownership-thesis]]
and the limits of fundamental [[token-value-accrual]] (attention vs cash flow).
