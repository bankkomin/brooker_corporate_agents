# Source Summary — ARK Invest Big Ideas 2022

**As of: 25–26 January 2022**

## Document Overview

ARK Invest's annual research report for 2022, themed **"Change & Convergence"**. It
identifies 14 transformative technologies underlying five innovation platforms and argues
that historians will look back on this era as one of "unprecedented technological foment".
Held in the wiki as the earlier counterpart to [[source-ark-big-ideas-2026]], showing how
ARK's framing evolved over four years.

## Key Findings

- **Five innovation platforms (2022 framing):** Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, Energy
  Storage, DNA Sequencing, and Blockchain Technology — evolving and converging
  simultaneously.
- **Equity-market scale.** ARK projected the five platforms' attributable market
  capitalization growing from ~$3.2T in 2020 toward a much larger 2030 base, with AI
  capitalization rising from $1.4T (26% CAGR) and Blockchain from $0.17T (35% CAGR).
- **2022 report sections** included Artificial Intelligence, Digital Wallets, Public
  Blockchains, Bitcoin, Ethereum & DeFi, Web3, plus EVs, gene editing, and orbital
  aerospace.
- **Convergence thesis** — costs dropping across 14 technologies unleash demand across
  sectors and geographies, spawning further innovation.

## Relevance

A point-in-time anchor for ARK's disruptive-innovation framework at the 2021–2022 market
peak. Useful as a contrast against the 2026 edition: the 2026 report folds DNA Sequencing
into "Multiomics", elevates Robotics as a catalyst, and adds space-based compute. See
[[ark-disruptive-innovation-thesis]].
