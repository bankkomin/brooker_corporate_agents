# Source Summary — The Hitchhiker's Guide to Ethereum (Delphi)

_As of 26 May 2022. Publisher: [[delphi-digital]]. Author: Jon Charbonneau._

## Document Overview

A Delphi Digital technical primer on Ethereum's scaling roadmap and the path to
danksharding.

## Key Findings

- Ethereum is the only major protocol building a scalable unified settlement and
  data-availability (DA) layer.
- Rollups scale computation while leveraging Ethereum's security.
- The "endgame": centralized block production, decentralized trustless block
  validation, censorship resistance.
- Proposer-builder separation (PBS) and weak statelessness separate building from
  validating to achieve scale without sacrificing decentralization.
- MEV is "front and center"; danksharding combines data availability sampling and KZG
  commitments to provide a scalable base layer for the rollup-centric roadmap.
