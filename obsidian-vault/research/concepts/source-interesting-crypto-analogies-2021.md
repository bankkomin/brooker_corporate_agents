# Source Summary — Interesting Crypto Analogies (Notes Deck)

_As of September 2021. Format: informal slide-deck notes._

## Document Overview

A short, informal slide deck of crypto analogies and talking points — apparently
personal notes rather than a published report.

## Key Findings

- "Store of wealth" analysis: stocks, USD, real estate, gold, oil all have drawbacks as
  long-horizon stores of value (taxes, money printing, ~2% supply growth, OPEC-style
  over-production).
- Argues commodities make poor money because supply expands when prices rise — framing
  Bitcoin's fixed supply as the differentiator.

## Relevance

A condensed restatement of the scarcity / store-of-value argument behind the
[[bitcoin-digital-gold-thesis]]; low standalone depth.
