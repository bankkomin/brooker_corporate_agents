# Source Summary — Real Vision: The Sandbox — How to Distinguish the Metaverse from Gaming

**As of: 20 January 2022** · Sebastien Borget (The Sandbox COO) & Ash Bennington · Real Vision transcript

## Document Overview

A Real Vision interview with The Sandbox co-founder/COO Sebastien Borget on what
distinguishes a true metaverse from online gaming.

## Key Findings

- The metaverse looks like online gaming but aims to be a "parallel universe."
- **Interoperability** — avatars, items and currency transferable between worlds — is the
  defining property, alongside **user ownership** and DAO governance over game rules.
- "The essence of the metaverse can't be captured in a simple rebranding campaign."

## Relevance

A builder's-eye source for [[metaverse-thesis]] — defines the Web3 metaverse by ownership
and interoperability rather than graphics. Touches [[nft-creator-economy]].
