# Source Summary — Research x Labs: DeFi on BNB Chain (Binance)

_As of October 2022. Publisher: [[binance-research]] (Research x Labs series)._

## Document Overview

A Binance Research / Binance Labs joint report profiling the DeFi ecosystem on BNB
Chain, with project spotlights and interview highlights.

## Key Findings

- BNB Chain offers high throughput and relatively low fees, and held the third-largest
  DeFi ecosystem by TVL as of end-August 2022.
- Four core verticals profiled with a flagship project each:
  - **DEXes** — PancakeSwap
  - **Lending & Borrowing** — Venus
  - **Asset Management** — Tranchess
  - **Liquid Staking** — pSTAKE

## Relevance

An ecosystem snapshot of BNB Chain DeFi; supports [[defi-credit-uncollateralized-lending]]
(Venus money market) and protocol value-accrual analysis.
