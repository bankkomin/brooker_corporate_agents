# Source Summary — Interview with Sam Bankman-Fried (Real Vision, Jul 2022)

_As of 8 July 2022. Publisher: [[real-vision]]. Interview: Sam Bankman-Fried (FTX) & Raoul Pal._

## Document Overview

A Real Vision interview transcript with Sam Bankman-Fried, then co-founder and CEO of
FTX — recorded ~4 months before FTX's collapse. "What the Current Mess Means for the
Future of Crypto."

## Key Findings

- Macro discussion of higher rates and liquidity, with parallels drawn to the 1998
  Long-Term Capital Management blow-up.
- Debates whether ETH 2.0 could establish a "risk-free rate" for Web 3.0.
- SBF discusses tokenizing traditional markets and onboarding the masses via mobile and
  tickets.

## Relevance

A historically notable pre-collapse interview; the tokenization discussion presages the
[[tokenization-real-world-assets]] thesis. Read with awareness of the November 2022 FTX
collapse — see [[source-delphi-ftx-downfall-2022]].
