# Source Summary — Kazea Stablecoin Velocity Fund Pitchbook (2025)

**As of: 24–26 August 2025**

## Document Overview

A confidential private-placement pitchbook for **Kazea Stablecoin Velocity Fund LP**, a
highly focused venture fund investing in early-stage technology companies building
stablecoin applications or infrastructure. See the fund entity
[[kazea-stablecoin-velocity-fund]].

## Key Findings

- **Thesis.** Stablecoins are a "once in a generation opportunity" with the clearest
  product-market fit in crypto; the fund builds a global portfolio of ~10 stablecoin
  application/infrastructure companies. Three warehoused investments completed by the
  principals.
- **Market momentum.** US$270B+ stablecoin supply, ~45M monthly active users, ~100M
  addresses holding stablecoins; stablecoins are a top-20 holder of US Treasuries (ahead
  of Germany) and overtook Visa in transaction volume in 2024.
- **Focus areas.** Two stack levels where the fund sees value accruing — Applications
  (stablecoin neobanks, RWA apps, on-chain lending/treasury) and Infra/Aggregation
  (on/off-ramp orchestration, B2B payouts, agentic payments).
- **Partners.** Daniel Bertoli (Co-Founder KAST; ex-Quona Capital, ex-Telenor, ex-Xiaomi)
  and Antonio Echavarria (Founder SSV; ex-PE in LatAm) — a TradFi/fintech + crypto blend
  with strong LATAM connections; the two co-invested for five years.
- **Fund terms** — see Extracted Data.

## Relevance

A worked example of a small, concentrated thematic VC fund and a primary reference for the
[[stablecoin-payments-thesis]]. Relevant to Brooker's VCC fund-of-funds deal flow and to
the stablecoin-settlement advisory line of the [[three-engine-model]].
