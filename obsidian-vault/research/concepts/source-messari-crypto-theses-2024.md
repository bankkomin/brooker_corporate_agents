# Source Summary — Messari Crypto Theses for 2024

_As of 27 December 2023. Author: Ryan Selkis, [[messari]]._

## Document Overview

Messari's flagship annual report — key trends, people, companies and projects to watch,
with 2024 predictions. Subtitled "We're So Back" after 2023's "It's So Over."

## Key Findings

- After a brutal 2022-23 credit crisis (FTX, SBF jailed), the surviving operators
  (Coinbase, Kraken, Circle) anchor a renewed cycle; "Wall Street's embrace is imminent."
- Top investment trends: BTC & digital gold, ETH & the world computer, the liquid field,
  resurgence of private crypto markets, IPOs/M&A, policy, "AI & Crypto: Money for the
  Machines," and three new De's — DePIN, DeSoc, DeSci.
- "Bitcoin is the Godzilla of Finance"; stablecoins framed as "the new TINA trade."
- DeFi trends include RWA diversification and DeFi lending; consumer crypto covers
  DeSoc, NFT market models, gaming, and network states.

## Relevance

A comprehensive bull-turn thesis for 2024; supports [[tokenization-real-world-assets]],
[[defi-credit-uncollateralized-lending]] and [[bitcoin-digital-gold-thesis]].
