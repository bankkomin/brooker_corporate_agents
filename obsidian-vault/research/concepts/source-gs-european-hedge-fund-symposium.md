# Source Summary — Goldman Sachs 24th Annual European Hedge Fund Symposium (2026)

**As of: 29 April 2026**

## Document Overview

A presenting-managers directory for **Goldman Sachs' 24th Annual European Hedge Fund
Symposium** — a Goldman Sachs Capital Introduction event listing hedge-fund managers
investors can request to meet, with strategy attributes, AUM ranges, inception dates, and
presenter biographies. Distributed via Goldman's "Marquee Connect" digital platform.

## Key Findings

- A capital-introduction directory rather than a research report — it profiles
  market-neutral, relative-value, quant, macro, and event-driven hedge-fund managers
  presenting to investors.
- Example managers profiled: 111 Capital (quant/statistical arbitrage, market-neutral,
  ~$500–750M), ADAPT Investment Managers (relative value / volatility arbitrage,
  $1,000–2,500M), Alphadyne Asset Management (discretionary global macro, >$5,000M),
  Amundsen Investment Management (event-driven, ESG, $250–500M).
- Presenter biographies trace pedigrees through Société Générale, Goldman Sachs, JP
  Morgan, Deutsche Bank, BTG Pactual, and sovereign wealth funds (Norges Bank).

## Relevance

A point-in-time snapshot of the institutional hedge-fund landscape and Goldman Sachs'
capital-introduction franchise — useful as context on market-neutral / relative-value
manager universe relevant to a fund-of-funds platform, and on how placement and
cap-intro events surface managers.
