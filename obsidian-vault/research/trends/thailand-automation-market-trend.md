# Thailand Robotics & Automation Market (2020-2035 projection)

## Overview

A projected time series for Thailand's robotics & automation market value, from the
Thailand Digital Technology Foresight 2035 study
([[source-thailand-digital-technology-foresight-2035]]). The projection (citing Frost &
Sullivan data) tracks the market across four CAGR phases to 2035.

## The Time Series

| Year | Market value (THB) | Phase CAGR |
|------|--------------------|------------|
| 2020 | 200 billion | (base year) |
| 2025 | 438 billion | 13.98% (2020–2025) |
| 2030 | 739 billion | ~9% (2026–2030) |
| 2035 | >1 trillion | 6% (2031–2035) |

## Drivers

- Thailand's position as a regional automotive hub, electronics & electrical appliances
  hub, food-processing center, and medical-services center.
- The International Federation of Robotics regards automotive plus electrical/electronics
  industries as the highest contributors to global automation growth (~64% market share).
- Ambition to become a world exporter of robots and automation systems.

## Adoption Notes

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) is early-stage: fewer than 35% of Thai companies are
expected to adopt RPA within five years, with banking/finance and retail leading. RPA is
expected to evolve toward Smart Process Automation (AI/ML-integrated) and cognitive
automation over a 10-year horizon.
