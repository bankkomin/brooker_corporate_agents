# Tiang & Partners

## Overview

Tiang & Partners is an independent Hong Kong law firm that collaborates closely with
PricewaterhouseCoopers Limited (PwC). Its Funds and Regulatory practice covers the full
spectrum of investment funds (private equity, hedge, hybrid, retail/authorised) and
non-contentious regulatory matters, including crypto-fund structuring and SFC licensing.
