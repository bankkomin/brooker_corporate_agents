# MicroStrategy Incorporated

## Overview

MicroStrategy Incorporated is a US-listed (NASDAQ) enterprise-software company that became
the highest-profile corporate Bitcoin holder after announcing, in 2020, that Bitcoin was
its primary treasury reserve asset. Its 2020 program documents are the defining reference
for the [[corporate-bitcoin-treasury-playbook]].

| HQ | Tysons Corner, Virginia, USA |
| Program documents | Treasury Reserve Policy, Bitcoin Trading Policy, Project Roadmap, Key Considerations, Accounting memo, Custodian-contracting checklist |

## History

In December 2020 MicroStrategy disclosed it had made more than $1B in total Bitcoin
purchases that year, framing it as an investment to "provide the opportunity for better
returns and preserve the value of capital over time compared to holding cash". The company
published a full suite of governance, accounting, and custody documents (some co-authored
with Deloitte) that have served as a template for other corporates.
