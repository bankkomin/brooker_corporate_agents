# Goldman Sachs — Digital Assets Research

## Overview

Goldman Sachs is a global investment bank whose research on digital assets is a useful
"TradFi consensus" marker. Two 2021 publications appear in this vault: the *Top of Mind*
"Crypto: A New Asset Class?" report and an Asia-focused "Overview of Digital Assets and
Blockchain."

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Type | Global investment bank |
| Digital-assets lead | Mathew McDermott (Global Head of Digital Assets) |
| 2021 stance | Launching crypto products; clients "increasingly say yes" |
| Research framing | Crypto as a candidate institutional asset class; blockchain 1.0-4.0 evolution |

## History

The May 2021 *Top of Mind* report staged the institutional debate (Novogratz "yes" vs.
Roubini "no income or utility"). The November 2021 Asia overview traced blockchain's
evolution from Bitcoin (1.0) through smart contracts (2.0), the ICO boom (3.0), and
NFTs/metaverse (4.0).
