# SkyBridge Capital

## Overview

SkyBridge Capital is a US alternative-investment firm founded by Anthony Scaramucci. In
late 2020 it launched the SkyBridge Bitcoin Fund L.P., taking a direct institutional
Bitcoin allocation and publishing the "Bitcoin Foundations" investor education deck.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |

| Founder | Anthony Scaramucci |

| Bitcoin position (Jan 2021) | ~$272M across SkyBridge funds |
| Thesis | Bitcoin as "digital gold" — better at being gold than gold |

## History

SkyBridge's "Bitcoin Foundations" deck (Jan 2021) framed Bitcoin as a scarce monetary
technology and an existential hedge against the 60/40 portfolio amid near-zero rates
and money printing — an early institutional articulation of the digital-gold thesis.
