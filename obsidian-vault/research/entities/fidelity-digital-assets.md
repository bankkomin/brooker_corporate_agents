# Fidelity Digital Assets

## Overview

Fidelity Digital Assets is the institutional crypto custody, trading and research arm of
Fidelity Investments. Its research group publishes the quarterly **Signals Report**, a
structured digest of on-chain and market metrics organised by investment time horizon.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Parent | Fidelity Investments |
| Services | Institutional custody, trade execution, research |
| Flagship research | Quarterly Signals Report (Bitcoin and Ethereum outlooks) |
| Method | Signals grouped by short- (<1y), mid- (1-5y), long-term (>5y) horizons |

## History

Fidelity was one of the earliest large traditional asset managers to build crypto
infrastructure. Its Q2 2023 Signals Report rated Bitcoin positive across all three
horizons and Ethereum positive/neutral/positive, using metrics such as NUPL, MVRV
Z-Score, Reserve Risk and the Puell Multiple — an applied use of the
[[on-chain-analysis-framework]].
