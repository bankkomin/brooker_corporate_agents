# CFA Institute Research Foundation

## Overview

The CFA Institute Research Foundation is the not-for-profit research arm of the CFA
Institute, the global association of investment professionals. It publishes practitioner
education material, including the 2021 brief *Cryptoassets: The Guide to Bitcoin,
Blockchain, and Cryptocurrency for Investment Professionals*.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Type | Not-for-profit investment-research foundation |
| Audience | CFA charterholders and investment professionals |
| Notable work | Cryptoassets (2021), authored by Matt Hougan & David Lawant of Bitwise |
| Status | Qualified for CFA Professional Learning (PL) credit |

## History

The *Cryptoassets* brief was an early mainstream-credentialed guide aimed at financial
advisers and professional investors, covering crypto basics, the landscape, crypto as
an investment opportunity, portfolio construction, and key risks.
