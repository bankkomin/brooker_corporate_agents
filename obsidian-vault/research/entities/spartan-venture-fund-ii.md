# Spartan Venture Fund II LP (Gaming & Metaverse Fund)

## Overview

Spartan Venture Fund II LP — marketed as the **Gaming & Metaverse Fund** — is a closed-end
venture fund managed by Spartan Capital ([[spartan-group]]), focused on play-to-earn
games, metaverses, gaming guilds/studios, GameFi infrastructure, NFT platforms, and
gamified DeFi.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Type | Closed-end venture capital fund |
| Vehicle | Cayman Islands exempted limited partnership |
| General Partner | Spartan Capital GP |
| Expected fund size | US$200M (GP commitment $25M) |
| Committed (first + second close) | ~US$117M; final close targeted Nov–Dec 2022 |
| Minimum Commitment | US$200,000 |
| Term | 6 years (+ 2-year extension option) |
| Management Fee | 2% p.a. of committed capital |
| Carried Interest | 20% up to 40% IRR, 30% thereafter |
| Target | 100–150 projects; seed $300K–1M, strategic rounds $1–10M |
