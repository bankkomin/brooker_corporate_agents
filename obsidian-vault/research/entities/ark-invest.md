# ARK Investment Management LLC

## Overview

ARK Investment Management LLC ("ARK Invest") is a US-based investment management firm
focused on disruptive innovation. It is best known for its flagship annual research
report, **Big Ideas**, published since 2017 — the 2026 edition is the 10th.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Type | Investment manager / research house |
| Flagship publication | Big Ideas (annual, since 2017) |
| Chief Futurist / Director of Research | Brett Winton |
| Research focus | AI, robotics, energy storage, public blockchains, multiomics, space |
| Method | Cross-sector top-down + bottom-up research; convergence scoring |

## History

ARK began publishing Big Ideas in 2017 to highlight breakthrough-technology research
conclusions. The 2022 edition ([[source-ark-big-ideas-2022]]) framed five platforms around
"Change & Convergence"; the 2026 edition ([[source-ark-big-ideas-2026]]) reframed them
around "The Great Acceleration" with AI as the central dynamo.
