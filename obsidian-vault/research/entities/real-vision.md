# Real Vision

## Overview

Real Vision is a financial media and research platform co-founded by Raoul Pal (former
GLG / Goldman Sachs macro investor). It produces long-form interviews and macro/crypto
research, including the "Macro Insiders" and "Pro Crypto" / "Crypto Insiders" products
(the latter in partnership with Delphi Digital).

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Type | Financial media + research platform |
| Co-founder / lead voice | Raoul Pal (also founder of Global Macro Investor) |
| Products | Real Vision interviews, Macro Insiders Deep Dives, Pro Crypto / Crypto Insiders |
| Crypto framing | Macro business-cycle + global-liquidity overlay on crypto ("Exponential Age") |

## History

Real Vision's crypto interview library in this vault spans 2021-2024: Yat Siu on NFTs
and culture, Tom Bilyeu on NFT impact, Mike Dudas on Web 3.0, Sam Bankman-Fried
(July 2022, pre-collapse), the Tally Labs founders on community-owned IP, and Raoul
Pal's own 2024 macro deep dive forecasting a post-ETF retail wave.
