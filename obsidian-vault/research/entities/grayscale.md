# Grayscale Investments

## Overview

Grayscale Investments is a US digital-asset manager best known for the Grayscale Bitcoin
Trust (GBTC), historically the largest publicly traded Bitcoin vehicle. Its research
arm publishes investment theses arguing for institutional digital-asset allocation.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Type | Digital-asset manager |
| Flagship product | Grayscale Bitcoin Trust (GBTC) — converted to a spot ETF in 2024 |
| Research voice | "Grayscale Insights" thesis series |
| Notable thesis | "The Modern Portfolio" (2019) — digital assets as a new asset class |

## History

Grayscale's 2019 "Modern Portfolio" paper made an early Modern Portfolio Theory case
for digital assets as the "missing piece" of the efficient frontier. GBTC's persistent
discount-to-NAV and its 2024 ETF conversion were recurring themes in later market
research (Messari, ARK).
