# Delphi Digital

## Overview

Delphi Digital is a crypto research and venture firm. It publishes deep-dive protocol
research (often via the "Crypto Insiders" series in partnership with Real Vision) and
operates an affiliated venture arm, Delphi Ventures. Its analysts — including Jon
Charbonneau — produced widely cited primers on Ethereum scaling, rollups, gaming and
DeFi credit during 2022.

## History

Delphi's 2022 output is a strong record of bear-market protocol research: technical
primers on Ethereum's rollup roadmap and danksharding, the ATOM 2.0 thesis, RWA credit
(Goldfinch), crypto gaming, and a same-day post-mortem of the FTX collapse. Disclosures
note Delphi Ventures positions in many covered tokens.
