# Stone Ridge Asset Management

## Overview

Stone Ridge is a US alternative asset manager founded by Ross L. Stevens. It is the parent
of NYDIG (New York Digital Investment Group), a major institutional Bitcoin custodian and
products business. Stone Ridge is known for its diversified, uncorrelated-risk-premium
"10/10" portfolio philosophy and an essay-style annual shareholder letter.

## History

- 2013–2017: built out an uncorrelated-alternatives fund platform.
- 2017: began holding Bitcoin as the principal component of treasury reserves.
- 2020: NYDIG affiliate custodied >$350M of Bitcoin for insurer clients.
