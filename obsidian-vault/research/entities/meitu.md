# Meitu, Inc.

## Overview

Meitu, Inc. (美图公司) is a Cayman-incorporated, Hong Kong-listed (Stock Code 1357)
technology company providing online advertising and internet value-added services through
a portfolio of photo and community apps. In 2021 it ran a board-approved digital-asset
treasury program, becoming a notable Hong Kong-listed corporate crypto holder.

## History

In March–April 2021 Meitu executed a board-approved Cryptocurrency Investment Plan,
purchasing Ether and Bitcoin in open-market transactions to a cumulative net purchase of
~US$100M — disclosed across three HKEX announcements (see
[[source-meitu-crypto-purchases]]). The Board framed the allocation as diversification
away from cash and as preparation to enter the blockchain industry.
