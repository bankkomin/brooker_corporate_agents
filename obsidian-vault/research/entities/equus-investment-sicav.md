# EQUUS Investment SICAV

## Overview

EQUUS Investment SICAV is an open-ended investment company ("Société d'Investissement à
Capital Variable") organized under the laws of the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg as a UCITS
umbrella fund.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Type | Luxembourg UCITS umbrella fund (SICAV) |
| Regulatory status | Part I, Luxembourg Law of 2010; UCITS (EU Directive EC/2009/65) |
| Structure | Single SICAV with multiple separate Sub-Funds |
| Sub-Funds (Feb 2026) | Ascender Asia Value Fund; Momentum Opportunities Fund; IMP Emerging Equity Trends |
| Share mechanics | Issue/redeem/convert at Net Asset Value |
