# Binance Research

## Overview

Binance Research is the in-house research arm of Binance, the world's largest crypto
exchange by volume. It publishes industry maps, ecosystem reports, and the
"Research x Labs" series profiling projects on BNB Chain.

| Focus | Crypto ecosystem mapping, BNB Chain DeFi/NFT/gaming |

## History

The March 2023 "Industry Map" segmented crypto into Infrastructure, DeFi, NFT, Gaming
plus topical sectors (RWA, DeSoc, AI, DeSci, DAO tooling). The "DeFi on BNB Chain" study
profiled DEXes (PancakeSwap), lending (Venus), asset management (Tranchess) and liquid
staking (pSTAKE). Binance Research also publishes the "State of BNB Chain" quarterly
series.
