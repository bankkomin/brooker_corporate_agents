# BitOoda

## Overview

BitOoda is a digital-asset financial-services and research firm specialising in Bitcoin
mining, energy, and derivatives. Its research focuses on quantitative analysis of mining
power capacity, hashrate, and miner economics.

## Key Facts

| Notable work | Bitcoin Power Analysis (2020) |

## History

BitOoda's July 2020 "Bitcoin Power Analysis" was an early systematic attempt to measure
and geolocate global Bitcoin mining power capacity (~9.6 GW) and model the capex and
price thresholds for the S19 upgrade cycle — research that preceded the 2021 China
mining ban.
