# BNB Chain

## Overview

BNB Chain is a public, open-source, EVM-compatible blockchain ecosystem — a multichain
framework spanning the BNB Smart Chain, Beacon Chain, sidechains, and planned ZK/optimistic
rollups plus the BNB Greenfield storage chain. It is closely associated with, but
formally separate from, the Binance group of exchange entities.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Type | Public EVM-compatible blockchain ecosystem |
| Native token | BNB (200M total supply; deflationary burn since 2017) |
| Consensus | Proof-of-Staked-Authority (PoSA) |
| BNB burned by Q1 2023 | >44M (~22% of total supply) |
| Relationship to Binance | Separate entity; Binance/Binance Labs/Launchpad support the ecosystem |

## History

- 2017: BNB token and burn program launched.
- Q1 2023: validator decentralisation (BEP-131/153/159); Uniswap launched on BNB Chain;
  BUSD wound down after Paxos was forced to cease issuance; CFTC sued Binance/CZ.
- 2023 roadmap: modular rollup L2s, BNB Greenfield storage, account abstraction,
  validators 29 → 100.
