# CoinGecko

## Overview

CoinGecko is a Singapore-based cryptocurrency data aggregator, founded 2014. It tracks
prices, volumes, market capitalization and on-chain metrics across thousands of coins
and hundreds of exchanges. Its free **Quarterly Crypto Industry Reports** are a primary
point-in-time record of market structure used throughout this knowledge base.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Type | Crypto data aggregator / research publisher |

| Coverage | >12,000 coins, 500+ exchanges (as of 2021) |
| Flagship research | Quarterly + annual Crypto Industry Reports |
| Report structure | Market Landscape, Bitcoin, Ethereum, DeFi, NFT, Exchanges |

## History

CoinGecko's quarterly reports in this vault span Q1 2021 through Q2 2023 — covering the
2021 bull market, the 2022 Terra/LUNA collapse and contagion, and the 2023
SEC-enforcement and spot-ETF-anticipation period. The reports also produced consumer
guides ("How to Bitcoin," "How to DeFi," "How to NFT").
