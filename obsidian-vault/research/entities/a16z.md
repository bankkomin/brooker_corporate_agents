# Andreessen Horowitz (a16z crypto)

## Overview

Andreessen Horowitz ("a16z") is a major US venture capital firm; **a16z crypto** is its
dedicated crypto/Web3 investment unit. a16z crypto publishes the annual "State of Crypto"
report and is one of the most influential proponents of the Web3 / internet-of-ownership
thesis.
