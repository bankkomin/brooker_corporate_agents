# Coinbase Global, Inc.

## Overview

Coinbase (NASDAQ: COIN) is the largest US-listed cryptocurrency exchange and custodian.
It listed via direct listing in April 2021 — the first pure-play crypto platform to do
so. Coinbase publishes institutional market reviews and quarterly shareholder letters
that are key point-in-time records of crypto's institutionalization.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|

## History

Coinbase's 2020 review declared crypto "an institutional asset class." Its H1 2021
review documented the launch of Coinbase Prime Broker and the COIN direct listing. By
FY2025-Q1 2026 it framed crypto as "updating all financial services" with AI as the
accelerant, and tokenization/stablecoins as core growth engines.
