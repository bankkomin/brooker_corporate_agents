# The Spartan Group

## Overview

The Spartan Group is a leading crypto-native asset-management and advisory group founded
in 2017, with a global presence. Its founders are former Goldman Sachs bankers, venture
capitalists, and early crypto advocates. **Spartan Capital** is its digital-asset
management unit; **Spartan Advisory** is its advisory arm.
