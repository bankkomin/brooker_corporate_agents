# Kazea Stablecoin Velocity Fund LP

## Overview

Kazea Stablecoin Velocity Fund LP is a BVI-domiciled, highly concentrated venture fund
investing primarily in early-stage equity of technology companies that build stablecoin
applications or infrastructure.
