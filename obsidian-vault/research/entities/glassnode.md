# Glassnode

## Overview

Glassnode (Glassnode Services AG, Baar, Switzerland) is a blockchain data and
intelligence provider founded in 2017, focused on institutional clients. It delivers
on-chain analytics and proprietary metrics for Bitcoin and DeFi, and co-publishes
research with ARK Invest, CME Group and Coinbase.

| Specialty | On-chain metrics, market-cycle and holder-behaviour analytics |

## History

Glassnode metrics underpin much of the modern [[on-chain-analysis-framework]]. Its 2023
collaboration with ARK introduced "Cointime Economics," and its institutional reports
with CME (2024) and Coinbase (2025) document the post-spot-ETF market.
