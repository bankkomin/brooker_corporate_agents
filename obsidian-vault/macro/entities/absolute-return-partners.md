# Absolute Return Partners (Niels C. Jensen)

## Overview

Absolute Return Partners LLP (ARP) is a London-based alternative-investment boutique
providing independent asset management and investment advisory to institutional
investors. It is authorised and regulated by the UK Financial Conduct Authority and
registered in England (No. OC303480). Its research — written principally by founder
**Niels C. Jensen** — is organised around a structured set of long-term "megatrends"
published in the *Absolute Return Letter* and the *ARP+* research series. ARP is a
member of UN PRI (Principles for Responsible Investment).

## Signature Framework — The Megatrends

ARP frames all of its research around **[[the-seven-megatrends]]**, a structured
long-horizon model. The list was revised in early 2020 from eight trends to six (plus
wealth-to-GDP as an aggregate result), then a seventh — Globalisation 2.0 — was added
in May 2022:

1. The End of the Debt Supercycle — see [[debt-supercycle]]
2. Changing Demographics — see [[demographics-as-destiny]]
3. The Rising Gap between Rich and Poor — see [[rising-inequality]]
4. Rise of the East — see [[rise-of-the-east]]
5. The Age / Era of Disruption
6. Climate Change
7. Globalisation 2.0
- Mean-Reversion of Wealth-to-GDP — see [[wealth-to-gdp-mean-reversion]] (the aggregate result)

## Agent Notes

ARP's megatrend papers are explicitly long-horizon ("megatrends are, by definition,
not short-term in nature") and were each promised a refresh every ~3 years. Each paper
also lists associated *investment themes*. Jensen openly flags his own forecast
errors (e.g. the 2019 call that rates would stay low) — treat the analysis as a
disciplined framework, not point forecasts.
