# Rosenberg Research (David Rosenberg)

## Overview

Rosenberg Research is a global independent economic research and markets-strategy
firm founded and led by **David A. Rosenberg**. It provides clients with macro
insights and investment guidance and positions itself as an unbiased, independent
voice. In the macro knowledge area it appears as the author of the June 2022 note
*Inflation Still Transitory!* — a contrarian disinflation call made at the peak of the
2022 inflation scare.

## Notable Idea

Rosenberg's 2022 note leans on **Bob Farrell's Rule #9** — "when all the experts and
forecasts agree, something else is going to happen" — to argue against the
then-consensus that high inflation was here to stay. See [[inflation-regime-debate]].
