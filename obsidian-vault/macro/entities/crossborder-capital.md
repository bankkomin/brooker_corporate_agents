# CrossBorder Capital (Michael Howell)

## Overview

CrossBorder Capital Limited is a London-based independent macro research and advisory
firm specialising in the measurement and forecasting of **Global Liquidity**. It is
authorised and regulated by the UK Financial Conduct Authority (registered in England,
company No. 2687676). Its flagship outputs are the *Global View* thematic notes and the
*Weekly Global Liquidity Update*, addressed to professional institutional advisors. The
firm is closely associated with Michael Howell's work on the global liquidity cycle.

## Signature Framework — The Global Liquidity Cycle

CrossBorder's house thesis is **[[global-liquidity-cycle]]**: changes in Global
Liquidity — defined as the capacity of financial-sector balance sheets — are the
dominant driver of asset prices, more important than corporate profits or policy
interest rates. Key elements of the framework:

- A four-phase cycle: **Rebound → Calm → Speculation → Turbulence**.
- Three factors matter most: the **US Federal Reserve**, the **People's Bank of China**,
  and the **MOVE index** of US Treasury volatility (which sets collateral haircuts).
- Markets have become "vast debt-refinancing mechanisms" — ~3 of every 4 capital-market
  deals are refinancings, not new capital raises.
- A long-run **[[monetary-inflation]]** trend: liquidity expands ~6-8% p.a., doubling
  every decade, driven by mandatory public-spending commitments and sovereign-debt
  rollover needs.
