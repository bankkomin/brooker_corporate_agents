# Global Macro Investor / Real Vision (Raoul Pal)

## Overview

Global Macro Investor (GMI) is an institutional macro research service founded by
Raoul Pal in January 2005. Real Vision is the affiliated financial and crypto media
platform Pal co-founded; Macro Insiders is the Real Vision Pro research tier
(GMI-quality research for experienced retail investors), co-produced with
Julian Brigden of MI2 Partners. Pal also co-founded Exponential Age Asset Management
(EXPAAM), a digital-asset fund of funds. This research house is the single largest
source in the macro knowledge area — roughly half the ingested reports originate from
GMI / Real Vision / Macro Insiders.

## Signature Frameworks

- **[[the-everything-code]]** — Pal's all-encompassing thesis (developed 2023): the
  "Magic Formula" of GDP growth, the clockwork debt-refinancing cycle, and
  central-bank debasement projected forward.
- **[[currency-debasement]]** — QE as monetisation of government interest payments
  ("MOAR COWBELL"); the denominator devaluation that optically lifts asset prices.
- **[[the-exponential-age]]** — technology and crypto as the only assets that
  outrun debasement.
- **[[demographics-as-destiny]]** — debt, deflation and demographics as the three
  secular drivers (Pal's original 2005 GMI thesis).
- The "Banana Zone" and "Macro Summer" — Pal's labels for the liquidity-driven risk-on
  phase of the business cycle.

## Agent Notes

GMI/Real Vision content is opinionated, point-in-time, and written in a deliberately
provocative register. Treat price targets and "crystal ball" odds as the author's
probabilistic views, not forecasts. The durable, recurring ideas are promoted to the
`concept` articles linked above.
