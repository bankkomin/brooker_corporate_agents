# Source: Bonds / The Convexity Maven — Real Vision (Apr 2022)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **14 April 2022** (extract `_Modified:` 2022-04-26). A Real Vision
interview presentation featuring **Harley S. Bassman** ("The Convexity Maven,"
Managing Partner, Simplify ETFs), hosted by Evan Lorenz of Grant's Interest Rate
Observer.

## Key Findings

The extract is largely **slide titles only** (image-based deck). Identifiable content:

- The **MOVE Implied Volatility Index** — "the VIX for bonds" — is a featured chart.
- The **2-year vs 10-year yield spread** with recessions shaded — the classic
  curve-inversion recession signal.

## Relevance

Adds a bond-volatility / convexity voice. The MOVE index later becomes central to the
[[global-liquidity-cycle]] framework (it sets repo collateral haircuts).
