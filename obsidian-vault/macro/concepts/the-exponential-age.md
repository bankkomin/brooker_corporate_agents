# The Exponential Age

## Summary

"The Exponential Age" is the [[global-macro-investor]] / Real Vision thesis that
technology — AI, robotics, blockchain/crypto, gene editing, green energy, space — is
advancing at an exponential pace that will transform the economy and society. It is the
*escape route* from the debt and demographic trap of [[the-everything-code]]: as
population growth goes negative, exponential productivity is the only way to revive
GDP and GDP per capita. Real Vision's *The Exponentialist* (David Mattin) is the
dedicated channel for this theme.

## Why It Matters for Investing

Within [[currency-debasement]], only two assets rise *beyond* the central-bank balance
sheet — **technology equities and crypto**. They combine genuine adoption growth with
scarcity, making them the scarcest, highest-return assets in a 1.75%-growth world. Pal
has based his entire investing framework on this "mega-secular trend," focusing on
crypto from 2020 and technology equities from 2022.

## The "$85 Trillion" Infrastructure Build

The AI arms race implies an enormous physical build-out — compute, semiconductors,
energy, rare earths. AI inferencing workloads are ~100-1,000x more compute-intensive
than simple online services, driving a coming surge in energy demand (next-generation
nuclear, a solar/wind boom, fusion funding going exponential). Near-zero-cost energy
plus exponential compute is framed as the productivity miracle that ends the trap.

## AI as Electricity, AI as Magic

A recurring sub-thesis (see [[ai-as-electricity]]): large language models will be
**commodified** like electricity — a race to the bottom on price. The durable value
sits with **human context and distribution** (the "magic") — owned by the tech giants
(Apple, Google, Meta) — and with the "shovels" (Nvidia, TSMC). This is the macro area's
clearest investment lens on the AI cycle.

## Society and Politics

The technology revolution is also a social and political force — see
[[creatures-and-machines]] (the coming "accelerate vs decelerate" / augmented-vs-organic
divide) and the broader question of AI consciousness and a post-2030 "economic
singularity." Real Vision frames a coming "Renaissance" / new Enlightenment driven by
AI-fuelled scientific discovery.
