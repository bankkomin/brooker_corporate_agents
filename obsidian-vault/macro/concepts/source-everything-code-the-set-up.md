# Source: The Everything Code Part 2 — The Set-Up (GMI, 2023)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **2023** (two ingested copies: `Everything code part 2.docx`, `_Modified:`
2024-09-10; and `The_Everything_Code-The_Set-Up_V2_-_Final.pdf`, same period). Author:
Raoul Pal, [[global-macro-investor]]. **Part 2** — how the system got into this mess.

## Key Findings

- GMI's 2005 thesis: the world is driven by **debt, deflation, and demographics**.
- The narrative trap — **liquidity vs debasement**: QE is not just liquidity; it is
  the purposeful monetisation of debt and a debasement of the reserve currency. Once
  you see the denominator being debased, asset prices "make sense."
- The **Magic Formula**: GDP Growth = Population + Productivity + Debt Growth — the
  demographic driver is "dead, forever."
- The **clockwork economy**: post-2008 debt refinanced into the 3-5 year curve created
  a near-perfect ~3½-year cycle. Rates *must* be ≤ GDP growth.
- The **Central Bank Accord**: the Fed, BoJ and ECB monetise interest payments in
  concert; the balance sheet "is basically explained by the interest payments 3½
  years later." ~97% of S&P 500 moves explained by G5 balance sheets.
- Only **tech and crypto** escape the trap — see [[the-exponential-age]]. P/E ratios
  are now monetary indicators; the median American is poorer in asset terms.
