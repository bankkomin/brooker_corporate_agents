## Key Findings

- **Economy**: the Philly Fed Index and ISM New Orders less Inventories point to the
  ISM falling toward ~40; the GMI Financial Conditions Index suggests the ISM is near
  a cycle bottom — equities and crypto have been discounting that low in advance.
- **China Credit Impulse** leads the ISM by ~12-18 months and signals an upturn soon;
  a China recovery would help the rest of the world.
- **Inflation**: "yesterday's news" — the business cycle drives inflation (CPI lags
  the ISM by ~7 months); commodity prices briefly went negative, suggesting CPI could
  follow.
