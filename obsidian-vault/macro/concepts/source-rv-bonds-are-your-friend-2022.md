# Source: Bonds Are Your Friend — RV Macro Insiders (Feb 2022)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **28 February 2022** (extract `_Modified:` 2022-03-01). Author: Raoul Pal,
Macro Insiders In Focus ([[global-macro-investor]] / Real Vision).

## Key Findings

- Opens with geopolitical context on the Russian invasion of Ukraine — Russia's
  history of being invaded, the Crimea annexation (2014), and the buffer-zone rationale.
- Investment thesis: in times of change and uncertainty, **bonds are a friend** — Pal
  expects yields to fall as a slowdown materialises.
- Consistent with the [[business-cycle-cowbell]] framework: a tightening-driven
  slowdown forces the Fed to pause; bonds rally ahead of it.

## Relevance

A point-in-time bond call written in the opening days of the Russia-Ukraine war;
supports the [[business-cycle-cowbell]] thesis.
