# Source: GMI January 2024 Think Piece

## Document Overview

As-of date: **8 January 2024** (extract `_Modified:` 2024-02-16). Author: Raoul Pal and
contributors, the annual Global Macro Investor "Think Piece" ([[global-macro-investor]]).
A large multi-essay edition.

## Key Findings

- **2023 review**: GMI claims a banner year (>+100%), led by switching most ETH to SOL.
  The acknowledged miss: bonds, which decoupled from the usual business cycle.
- **2008 — The Year the World Broke**: builds on Jordi Visser's "Peak Fiat" idea — 2008
  was the peak of the fiat-based system. Most non-US asset markets (Europe, UK, EM,
  China, commodities) peaked in 2008 *in USD terms*; only India broke higher (the GMI
  "Monsoon" thesis).
- The dollar lags the Fed balance sheet by ~18 months and *rises* with debasement —
  the "de-dollarization weakens the dollar" myth is "180 degrees wrong." See
  [[dollar-dynamics]].
- GMI's secular themes: Debt & Demographics, the Retirement Crisis, the Crypto Super
  Massive Black Hole, [[the-exponential-age]], and [[the-everything-code]].
- A "crystal ball": ~60% chance of a usual cycle with BTC reaching ~$150-200k;
  scenarios for a front-loaded or larger-bubble cycle.
- Warns the 2024 US election will be the first AI-saturated election — "verify
  everything, believe nothing."
