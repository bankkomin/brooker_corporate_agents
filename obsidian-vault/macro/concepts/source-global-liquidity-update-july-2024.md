# Source: Global Liquidity Update — CrossBorder Capital (July 2024)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **July 2024** (extract `_Modified:` 2024-08-15). Publisher:
[[crossborder-capital]] — a monthly *Global Liquidity Update*.

## Key Findings

- A detailed monthly read on the [[global-liquidity-cycle]] across the ~90-economy
  CrossBorder universe.
- The cycle bottomed October 2022 and is roughly a third of the way through a typical
  5-6 year trough-to-trough cycle; projected peak late 2025.
- Q2 2024 was a liquidity "air-pocket" — Fed and PBoC tightening plus ECB/BoE QT. The
  PBoC is the swing factor; the Fed has been "dragging its feet."
- The framework details Central Bank liquidity, the Shadow Monetary Base, collateral
  values, and the four-phase cycle.

## Relevance

A monthly data installment supporting [[global-liquidity-cycle]] and the long-run
[[monetary-inflation]] trend.
