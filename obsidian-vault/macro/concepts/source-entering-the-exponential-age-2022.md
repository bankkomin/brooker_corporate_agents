# Source: Entering the Exponential Age — RV Macro Insiders (Apr 2022)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **21 April 2022** (extract `_Modified:` 2022-04-26). Author: Raoul Pal,
Macro Insiders In Focus ([[global-macro-investor]] / Real Vision).

## Key Findings

- Pal moves to **buy beaten-up Exponential Age (growth/technology) names** after waiting
  ~18 months — many down ~70% on the rotation into cyclicals, commodities and value.
- Growth-stock sentiment is the worst he has seen since ~2002.
- On inflation: Pal expects this cycle marks the *high* in inflation; future
  downcycles will not see new yield lows (due to fiscal stimulus). Frames the regime
  as "more the 1940s than the 1970s" — high debt eroded by some inflation, stable
  range-bound yields under yield curve control, rapid technology growth, fiscal
  stimulus.

## Relevance

An early, explicit statement of [[the-exponential-age]] as an investable entry point;
also an early articulation of the 1940s-analogue / yield-curve-control idea later
central to [[us-sovereign-debt-risk]].
