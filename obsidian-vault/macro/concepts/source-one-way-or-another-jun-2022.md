# Source: One Way or Another — Macro Insiders (Raoul Pal, Jun 2022)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **27 June 2022** (extract `_Modified:` 2022-06-28). Author: Raoul Pal,
Macro Insiders Deep Dive ([[global-macro-investor]] / Real Vision).

## Key Findings

- A trading "roadmap": monetary tightening is unprecedented in speed; personal incomes,
  wealth and confidence have been hit and the housing market has turned.
- Commodity markets are signalling that **global inflation is about to evaporate** — a
  setup reminiscent of 2008, 2012 and 2018. When the ISM falls, oil follows; even
  elevated prices flip from an inflation boost to a brake.
- The game is now about how fast the Fed pivots — a late pivot means a deeper recession
  and bear market; an early pivot a quicker return to bull markets. Since 1974, every
  Fed cut or pause came as the ISM reached 50.
- "For bonds, one way or another, all roads lead to lower yields."

## Relevance

A core statement of the [[business-cycle-cowbell]] framework — ISM, oil, the Fed
reaction function — and the [[inflation-regime-debate]] (disinflation side).
