# Source: Top 5 — Macro Insiders (Raoul Pal, Aug 2022)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **22 August 2022** (extract `_Modified:` 2022-08-26). Author: Raoul Pal,
Macro Insiders In Focus ([[global-macro-investor]] / Real Vision) — the first GMI
"Top 5" chart pack.

## Key Findings

- GMI's view: the economy is "about to fall off a cliff" then rebound in early 2023.
- "The scariest chart in macro": US 30-year mortgage rates up ~90% YoY — pointing to a
  plunging ISM.
- With under ~15% of global central banks easing, this is one of the broadest
  tightening cycles in history, consistent with the ISM falling toward ~35.
- The GMI Financial Conditions Index is among the lead indicators highlighted.
