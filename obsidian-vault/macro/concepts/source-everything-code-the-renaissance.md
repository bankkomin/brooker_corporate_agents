# Source: The Everything Code Part 1 — The Renaissance (GMI, 2023)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **2023** (extract `_Modified:` 2024-09-10; The Everything Code was
published to GMI clients in July 2023, then released to Real Vision Pro in three
parts). Author: Raoul Pal, [[global-macro-investor]]. This is **Part 1** — the
end-state of the framework.

## Key Findings

- Everything is driven by **the debasement of fiat** — see [[currency-debasement]].
- The "Renaissance": the answer is not a "Great Reset" but a technology-driven
  renaissance. The 2008 GFC was the Fourth Turning moment when past models broke.
- Exponential trends — crypto, AI, gene editing, self-driving, robots, the metaverse,
  new energy, space — build network effects on each other (invokes Reed's Law).
- **Yield Curve Control** is the resolution: like Japan (10yr pegged 0.5% vs 1.25%
  GDP) and the post-WWII US (rates capped 2.5%, GDP 5%, equities +750% in a decade).
  Once GDP exceeds interest rates by a margin, the debt problem is "solved."
- Near-zero-cost energy plus exponential compute is the productivity miracle; ~10-15
  years to play out. CBDCs are the trap; digital-asset ownership is the escape.
