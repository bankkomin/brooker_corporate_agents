# Source: Liquidity, Economic Momentum and Investor Positioning — CrossBorder Capital (Jul 2024)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **31 July 2024** (extract `_Modified:` 2024-08-15). Publisher:
[[crossborder-capital]] — a *Liquidity, Economic Momentum and Risk Exposure* update.

## Key Findings

- Central Bank liquidity growth has been edging higher since mid-July — CrossBorder is
  "hopeful" the Q2 2024 air-pocket is over.
- 3-month annualised monetary-base growth (their preferred net-liquidity measure):
  latest **-2.8%** vs the Q2 average of **-8.6%**; in US-dollar terms latest **-3.1%**
  vs Q2 average **-13.3%**.
- The PBoC is the main positive driver; other improvements are base effects. Fed/PBoC
  tightening plus ECB/BoE QT had kept growth in check.
