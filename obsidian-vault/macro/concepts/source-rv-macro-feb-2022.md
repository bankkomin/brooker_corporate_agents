# Source: The Macro Setup — RV Macro Insiders (Feb 2022)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **21 February 2022** (extract `_Modified:` 2022-02-24). Author: Raoul Pal,
Macro Insiders Deep Dive ([[global-macro-investor]] / Real Vision).

## Key Findings

- Pal makes a contrarian case for a US (and global) economic slowdown as the Fed enters
  its three-step tightening (stop QE → hike → QT).
- Tightening cycles are similar in shape: the NDX typically corrects ~16-17% on the
  news, then rallies through the hiking cycle.
- A rapidly flattening yield curve points to a future recession; COVID supply-chain
  disruption is a distortion to the secular disinflation/deflation trend driven by
  [[demographics-as-destiny]].
- As yield curves flatten and bonds rally, the Fed pauses, triggering the next wave of
  [[the-exponential-age]] themes.
- Game plan: add crypto, buy innovation (ARKK, SMT), buy the next NDX dip, buy 10-year
  US bonds, buy USD.

## Relevance

An early-2022 statement of the [[business-cycle-cowbell]] framework — slowdown → Fed
pause → risk-on.
