# Source: Weekly Global Liquidity Update — CrossBorder Capital (2 Aug 2024)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **2 August 2024** (extract `_Modified:` 2024-08-15). Publisher:
[[crossborder-capital]] — the *Weekly Global Liquidity Update*.

## Key Findings

- Global liquidity growth is accelerating and **liquidity hit a new all-time high**.
  Rising collateral values were the key driver.
- Liquidity rose ~US$1.98tn in the week to **US$174.94tr** — surpassing the late-2023
  peak of US$171.16tr.
- Growth rates: ~14.3% (3-month annualised), ~5.0% (YoY).
- Central banks present a mixed picture: a big jump in PBoC liquidity, but the Fed
  "dragging its feet"; ECB/BoE QT continues. The Fed hinted at a September rate cut.
- Roughly a third of the way through a typical 5-6 year liquidity cycle (Oct-2022 low).
