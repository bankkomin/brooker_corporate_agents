# Source: Crypto Update — Macro Insiders In Focus (Apr 2024)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **25 April 2024** (extract `_Modified:` 2024-04-25). Author: Raoul Pal,
Macro Insiders In Focus ([[global-macro-investor]] / Real Vision).

## Key Findings

- A crypto cycle update around the 2024 Bitcoin halving. The recent selling pressure is
  "fairly typical" halving behaviour, similar to 2016: an explosive run-up, sideways
  chop into the halving, then a corrective shake-out before prices rise again.
- SOL's ~30% drop from its early-April top is read as a retest of the breakout of a
  large inverse head-and-shoulders pattern; strong buying appeared at the ~$122
  support level.
- 30% corrections are described as typical of crypto bull markets — to be expected,
  not feared.

## Relevance

A point-in-time crypto cycle call; crypto as the leading expression of
[[the-exponential-age]] within the [[business-cycle-cowbell]] framework.
