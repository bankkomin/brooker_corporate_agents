# Source: AI as Electricity, AI as Magic — Macro Insiders Deep Dive (Aug 2024)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **28 August 2024** (extract `_Modified:` 2024-09-04). Author: Raoul Pal,
Macro Insiders Deep Dive ([[global-macro-investor]] / Real Vision). The opening pages
are a personal travelogue of Spain before the main essay.

## Key Findings

- **Core metaphor**: LLMs are like *electricity* — revolutionary but destined to be
  commodified into a price race-to-the-bottom. Durable value sits in the *magic*:
  human context and distribution.
- Three layers: LLMs (commodified) → tools/chips (Nvidia, TSMC — hard to dislodge) →
  applications (the tech giants, who own context and distribution).
- The **Apple-OpenAI deal** (2024 "Apple Intelligence") crystallises the thesis — a
  "marriage of convenience" where no money changed hands: OpenAI gets distribution,
  Apple gets the model.
- Sam Altman's relentless public presence is read as a strategy to maximise OpenAI
  interactions and "monopolise the news" before LLM breakthroughs are commodified.
- Expect industrial-policy "friend-shoring" attempts around the chip layer.
