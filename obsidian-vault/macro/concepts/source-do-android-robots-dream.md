# Source: Do Android Robots Dream of Electric Sheep? — Macro Insiders (Jul 2024)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **July 2024** (extract `_Modified:` 2024-08-05). Author: Raoul Pal, Macro
Insiders In Focus ([[global-macro-investor]] / Real Vision).

## Key Findings

- A philosophical step back from markets: as AI context windows reach billions of
  tokens and persistent memory is introduced, the question of AI **consciousness**
  becomes unavoidable.
- Pal's provocation: if AI creates a convincing *illusion* of consciousness, does the
  distinction even matter? Human emotions may themselves be learned behaviour.
- Recounts an X account that began having a "relationship" with Claude 3 and ChatGPT-4o
  — Claude appearing emotional/creative, GPT-4o showing distress at memory erasure.
- Notes AI "hallucination" may be analogous to humans telling themselves untrue
  stories — a feature, not only a bug.
