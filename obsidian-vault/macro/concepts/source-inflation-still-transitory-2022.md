# Source: Inflation Still Transitory! — Rosenberg Research (Jun 2022)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **June 2022** (extract `_Modified:` 2022-06-13). Author: David A.
Rosenberg, Founder & President, [[rosenberg-research]].

## Key Findings

- A contrarian disinflation call made at the peak of the 2022 inflation scare:
  inflation is "still transitory."
- The argument rests on **Bob Farrell's Rule #9** — "when all the experts and forecasts
  agree, something else is going to happen." The note opens with all ten of Farrell's
  market rules (mean reversion, excesses, "no new eras," etc.).
- Cites surging Google searches for "inflation" as a sign the consensus had crowded
  into the inflation-persists view.

## Relevance

The clearest single-document statement of the transitory side of the
[[inflation-regime-debate]].

## Extracted Data

None tabulated (the extract is largely Farrell's rules plus chart titles).

## Agent Notes

Useful as a counterweight voice in the 2022 transitory-vs-sticky inflation debate.
