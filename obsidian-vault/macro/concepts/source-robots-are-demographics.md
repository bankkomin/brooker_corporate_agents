# Source: Robots Are Demographics — Macro Insiders Deep Dive (Oct 2022)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **28 October 2022** (extract `_Modified:` 2022-11-01). Author: Raoul Pal,
Macro Insiders Deep Dive ([[global-macro-investor]] / Real Vision).

## Key Findings

- Global population growth is collapsing (~1% now, heading to zero by 2100) — driven by
  lower child mortality, longevity, rising real costs, globalisation and technology.
- Reframing (with Balaji Srinivasan): **robots and AI are demographics** — they will
  replace the missing population over time. Elon Musk's warnings about population
  collapse are read as economically substantive, not alarmist.
- First-order thinking ("GDP per capita rises") is incomplete; the deeper consequence
  is that robots/AI become the synthetic productive labour force.
- Investing angle: the ROBO index (down ~45% from its late-2021 high) plus single-stock
  and PE exposure. The "Catch 22": invest and you accelerate the trend; don't and it
  happens anyway without you.
