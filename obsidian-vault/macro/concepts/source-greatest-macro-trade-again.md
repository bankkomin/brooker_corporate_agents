# Source: The Greatest Macro Trade of All Time (Again) — Macro Insiders (Jun 2024)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **28 June 2024** (extract `_Modified:` 2024-06-30). Author: Raoul Pal,
Macro Insiders Deep Dive ([[global-macro-investor]] / Real Vision).

## Key Findings

- **The retirement crisis**: the median Baby Boomer has ~US$150k saved; Gen X (now in
  their 50s) a median of just ~US$40k. The purchasing power of wages in equities has
  fallen ~50% since 2008 — under-saved cohorts cannot catch up. See
  [[the-retirement-crisis]] and [[currency-debasement]].
- **Crypto as the life raft**: crypto is framed as the only asset class offering an
  escape — "the greatest macro trade of all time." The liquid-token crypto market is
  projected to go from ~US$2.7tn to US$100tn+ within a decade, reaching ~1 billion
  active wallets this cycle and half the world by 2030.
- The wealth created will be the fastest, largest accumulation in history — and
  relatively concentrated.
- **NFTs / digital art**: new wealth historically buys art; the thesis is that a
  staggering sum will flow into high-end digital art (NFTs), with XCOPY and Beeple as
  the culturally-relevant "OG" artists, and Autoglyphs / Fidenza / Ringers as the
  generative-art OGs. Pal's strategy: scrape SOL profits into these artists.
