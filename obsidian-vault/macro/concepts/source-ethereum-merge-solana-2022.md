# Source: Ethereum Merge & Solana — Macro Insiders Deep Dive (Aug 2022)

## Document Overview

As-of date: **26 August 2022** (extract `_Modified:` 2022-08-29; original GMI piece
4 July 2022). Authors: Raoul Pal and Remi, Macro Insiders Deep Dive
([[global-macro-investor]] / Real Vision).

## Key Findings

- Written in the aftermath of the CeFi / Luna / Three Arrows Capital collapse — the
  thesis is that good projects were "thrown out with the bathwater."
- The imminent Ethereum **Merge** to Proof-of-Stake lets ETH generate a benchmark
  yield, enabling institutional return streams and structured products — accelerating
  adoption. Pal: "ETH is going to have the upside of a fast-growing tech stock with
  buybacks, plus the elements of a sovereign bond market."
- Solana is highlighted as #1 in active addresses, total transactions, and average
  block time — "an insane amount of talent and capital being deployed."
- Pal recommended averaging into long ETH and SOL positions.

## Relevance

A crypto-specific expression of [[the-exponential-age]] thesis — crypto as the highest
expected-return asset.
