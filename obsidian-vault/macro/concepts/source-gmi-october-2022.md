# Source: Global Macro Investor — October 2022

## Document Overview

As-of date: **3 October 2022** (extract `_Modified:` 2022-10-25). Author: Raoul Pal,
Global Macro Investor monthly ([[global-macro-investor]]) — the free/edited edition.

## Key Findings

- **The Magic Formula**: Long-term GDP growth = population growth + productivity
  growth; debt grows to offset the fall in both — the origin of the [[debt-supercycle]]
  / [[currency-debasement]] framework. (Hat-tip to Arthur Hayes.)
- **The debt trap**: rates *must* sit below GDP growth; positive real rates "bankrupt
  the system." The system runs on stimulus.
- **"More Cowbell"**: every slowdown forces QE + rate cuts + fiscal stimulus. Lists the
  triggers (inflation lower, unemployment up, ISM <47, dollar spiking, yields
  decoupling, equities falling, systemic distress).
- The bond market is "broken" — the biggest yield-vs-ISM divergence in history.
- **The dollar wrecking ball / Doom Loop**: the DXY heading to 117-120; "the dollar
  dies of its own strength." See [[dollar-dynamics]].
- Record-bearish sentiment as a contrarian signal; inflation-driven bear markets
  average ~28-34% drawdowns, not 50%.
