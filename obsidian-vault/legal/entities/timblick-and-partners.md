# Timblick & Partners Limited

## Overview

Timblick & Partners Limited is the **Thai law firm** that issued the
[[2025-12-29-thai-regulatory-tax-opinion|legal opinion of 29 December 2025]] on Thai
regulatory and tax advice to [[brooker-group|Brooker Group PCL]]. The opinion analyses
whether the presence and activities of BROOK personnel in Thailand expose FOF to the Thai
SEC Act and Thai taxation.

## Scope of Its Opinion

The firm's opinion is **limited** to assessing whether the presence/activities of relevant
BROOK personnel in Thailand could result in FOF being regarded as carrying on restricted
business activities in Thailand. It expressly does **not** cover the overall fund structure
or any other legal/regulatory/tax issues, does not extend to non-Thailand matters, and
involved **no due diligence** on the entities in the fund structure. The opinion is given
under the laws of Thailand in force at its date.

## Reliance Terms

The opinion is **solely for the addressee's benefit** and may be relied upon only by the
addressee. A copy may be provided for information only to the addressee's professional
advisers, auditors and regulators — but on the basis that no such person may rely on it or
pass it on. The firm accepts **no responsibility or legal liability** to any person other
than the addressee.
