# Law Studios Thailand

## Overview

Law Studios Thailand is the **Thai law firm** engaged by [[brooker-group|The Brooker Group
PCL]] under an engagement letter dated **23 March 2026** to provide legal due-diligence
services on the [[obsidian-creek-capital|Obsidian Creek Capital]] fund and its lending /
gap-financing structure. The engagement is led by **Mr. Rachapoll Phromyarat**,
Attorney-at-Law.

## Engagement Terms

- **Governing law:** the laws of the Kingdom of Thailand; disputes resolved first by
  good-faith negotiation, then by the competent Thai courts.
- **Fees payable in full upon authorization to proceed**; work commences on receipt of
  payment.
- **Confidentiality:** strict mutual confidentiality; no disclosure without the Client's
  written consent except where required by law or court order.
- **Conflict of interest:** the firm represents that no known conflict exists at engagement
  date and will promptly notify the Client of any conflict that arises.
- **No guaranteed outcome:** the firm cannot guarantee any specific legal outcome.
- **Termination:** either party may terminate by written notice; the Client remains
  responsible for all fees.
