# Law Studios Engagement — Obsidian Creek Capital Legal DD (23 Mar 2026)

## Summary

[[brooker-group|The Brooker Group PCL]] engaged [[law-studios-thailand|Law Studios
Thailand]] (Mr. Rachapoll Phromyarat, Attorney-at-Law) to perform **legal due diligence**
on the [[obsidian-creek-capital|Obsidian Creek Capital]] fund and its lending /
gap-financing structure. The engagement letter is **dated 23 March 2026** and was
**accepted and signed by Mr. Varut Bulakul (CEO) on 23 April 2026**.

## Scope of Engagement (Section 1.1)

The attorney's services are **limited to**:

- Reviewing the **fund term sheet**, private placement memorandums / offering documents,
  limited partnership agreement (or equivalent constitutional documents), investment
  management agreement, and **subscription / side-letter documentation** relating to the
  Transaction.
- Reviewing **template loan, security, intercreditor/interparty, and collection account
  management (CAM)** documents used by Obsidian Creek Capital (or its affiliates) for
  lending and gap-financing to producers and entertainment businesses.
- **Legal due diligence** on: (i) the fund structure, governing law, and basic regulatory
  status of Obsidian Creek Capital and its affiliated manager / GP, based on publicly
  available information; and (ii) the legal framework and main risk points of the proposed
  lending / gap-financing structure — security/collateral, **priority in the recoupment
  waterfall**, and enforcement mechanics, at a high level.
- Providing **written/email summaries** of key legal issues and red-flags, with high-level
  recommendations for protective provisions or investor side-letter terms.

## Matters Specifically Excluded (Section 1.2)

Unless separately agreed in writing, the engagement does **not** include:

- **Non-Thai law opinions / regulatory advice** (U.S., Cayman Islands, or any non-Thai law
  — including securities, investment management, tax, sanctions, licensing) beyond
  high-level discussion comments.
- Background checks; financial, tax, accounting, valuation or business due diligence on
  Obsidian Creek Capital, its principals, any fund vehicle, or any borrower/producer.
- **Drafting or negotiating** the core fund documents beyond comments/markups limited to
  investor protections.
- Litigation, arbitration, enforcement actions, or regulatory-proceeding representation.
- **Ongoing post-closing monitoring** of the fund, portfolio loans or productions, or any
  continuous compliance / surveillance services.
- Thai or foreign **tax planning, transfer-pricing, withholding tax, or double-tax treaty
  analysis**.
- Any other legal services not in Section 1.1 — requiring a separate written agreement or
  amendment.

## Commercial Terms

| Term | Detail |
|------|--------|

## Rationale

The engagement provides Brooker with **Thai-law legal due diligence** on the Obsidian
Creek Capital fund and its gap-financing/lending documentation before participating in the
Transaction, with red-flag identification and investor-protection recommendations.
