# Source: MT & RPT 2026 Notifications (ทจ. 45/2568, ทจ. 46/2568)

## Document Overview

The primary-document bundle behind the 1 July 2026 MT/RPT reform — see
[[material-transaction-related-party-2026]] for the substantive analysis. The folder
contains the two notifications, their appendices, an explanatory briefing deck, the
revised Form 56-1 One Report extract, and SEC News No. 16/2569. Source files modified
through 11 March 2026.

## Key Findings

- **ทจ. 45/2568** — *Rules on Material Transactions* — 19 December 2025. Defines MT
  size bands (≥10% for distressed companies, ≥25% otherwise), the highest-of methods in
  Appendix 1, 12-month aggregation of related transactions, the 3/4 shareholder-approval
  rule, and IFA-opinion requirements for larger transactions. Five appendices cover
  calculation methods and disclosure detail.
- **ทจ. 46/2568** — *Rules on Related Party Transactions* — 19 December 2025. Parallel
  notification for connected transactions, with six appendices.
- **Form 56-1 One Report (Part 1, item 1.1.5)** — amended to capture additional
  disclosure on the company's investments.
- **บรรยาย_การปรับปรุงเกณฑ์ MT RPT** — an SEC explanatory briefing deck summarising
  the calculation, aggregation, exemption and investor-protection changes.

## Relevance

These notifications are the operative law from 1 July 2026 for any Brooker significant
asset acquisition/disposal or connected transaction. The numbered appendices (01/02
ภาคผนวก 1–6) are calculation and form annexes consumed via the parent concept article.
