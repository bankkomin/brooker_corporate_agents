# Source: Takeover & Acquisition/Disposal Disclosure Notifications

## Document Overview

A set of Capital Market Supervisory Board notifications collected in the
`การเข้าถือหลักทรัพย์เพื่อครอบงำกิจการ` folder, supporting the
[[takeover-and-tender-offer-regime]]. Source files modified 2015-05-20; underlying
notifications date 2009–2011.

## Key Findings

- **ทจ. 28/2554** — sets the requirements for reporting acquisition or disposal of
  securities, operationalising the Section 246 obligation to report each 5% change in
  voting rights to the SEC Office.
- **สจ. 37/2554** — prescribes **Form 246-2** (แบบรายงานการได้มาหรือจำหน่าย
  หลักทรัพย์ของกิจการ), the standard report filed when an investor's stake crosses a
  reportable threshold.
- **ทจ. 40/2552** — prescribes the form and the timeline within which a target
  company's board must prepare and disclose its opinion on a tender offer received.

## Relevance

These are the procedural/forms layer beneath the takeover regime. The folder also holds
pure form templates (Form 246-2 .doc) and a stray Office lock file (`~$...`); these carry
no substantive content beyond the notifications above.
