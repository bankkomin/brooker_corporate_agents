# SET Listed-Company Guidance Manuals

## Summary

The Stock Exchange of Thailand publishes a series of **practical guidance manuals**
(คู่มือ) for listed companies, their boards and officers. This folder (`Manual_SET`)
collects the SET reference library — guidance, not binding rules, but the canonical
"how to comply" companions to SET and SEC regulations. The manuals support corporate
governance: SET's working definition is that good corporate governance is the system of
relationships among shareholders, the board, management and all stakeholders, built to
create competitiveness and long-term shareholder value.

## Audit Committee Handbook — Highlight

The Audit Committee handbook treats the audit committee as a key corporate-governance
mechanism: an **independent sub-committee** of the board. Its remit has broadened over
time from reviewing financial reports and internal controls to also covering **risk
management oversight** and review of information beyond the financial statements. The
handbook covers committee composition, member qualifications, appointment, term of
office, removal, the committee secretary, meeting conduct, reporting and performance
evaluation, and provides a sample charter and pre-appointment checklists for independent
directors / audit committee members.

## Why It Matters

These manuals are the operational reference layer between high-level law (the Securities
and Exchange Act, the Public Limited Companies Act) and day-to-day compliance. They are
the standard sources for board, audit-committee and company-secretary practice for a
Thai listed company.
