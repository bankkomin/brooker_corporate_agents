# Source — Acquisition/Disposal of Assets Guide (January 2020)

## Document Overview

- **As-of date:** January 2020 consolidated guide; file modified 4 April 2023
  (download year 2566 / 2023).
- **Folder:** `กฎ การได้มาหรือจำหน่ายไปซึ่งสินทรัพย์ _Download ณ ปี2566`.
- **Subject:** explanatory guide to the rules on material acquisition or disposal of
  assets (MT) for Thai listed companies.

## Key Findings

- Legal basis: Section 89/29 of the Securities and Exchange Act B.E. 2535 (as amended
  by Act No. 4, effective 31 Aug 2008) → Capital Market Supervisory Board notification
  ทจ. 20/2551 → SET implementing criteria.
- Defines "asset" and "acquisition/disposal", lists exclusions, and sets out the four
  transaction-size tests and the procedure for board/shareholder approval and
  disclosure.
