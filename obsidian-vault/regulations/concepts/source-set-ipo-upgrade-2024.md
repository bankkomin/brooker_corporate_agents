# Source — SET Circular บจ. (ว) 2/2567 Listing-Criteria Upgrade Package

## Document Overview

- **As-of date:** circular dated 19 February 2024; file modified 27 February 2024.
- **Issuer:** The Stock Exchange of Thailand (ตลาดหลักทรัพย์ฯ), Listed Companies
  Supervision Department; signed by Assistant Manager Mrs. Duangrat Samutwanich.
- **Addressees:** managing directors of listed companies, financial advisers, member
  and non-member securities companies.
- **Folder:** `2025 SET (กฎ IPO เข้าใหม่) New Regulations Effective Year 2024 and 2025`.

This circular announced SET's program to raise listed-company governance across the
whole process — from selecting companies that will enter the market through to ongoing
supervision. It revised or repealed **23 SET notifications and guidelines**.

## Key Findings

1. **Admission-criteria upgrade** — added financial-position and operating-result
   qualifications; **effective 1 January 2025**.
2. **Risk-company supervision upgrade** — for companies with weak finances, losses,
   low liquidity or non-conforming qualifications, including wider warning signs;
   **effective 25 March 2024**.
