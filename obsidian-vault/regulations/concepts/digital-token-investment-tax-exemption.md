# Tax Exemption for Investment Token Transfers & Carbon Credit Sales

## Summary

Two Thai **Royal Decrees** issued under the Revenue Code in B.E. 2566 (2023) grant
corporate tax relief relevant to Brooker's digital-asset and ESG activity: Decree **No.
779** exempts the transfer of **investment tokens** from income tax and VAT, and Decree
**No. 760** exempts profit from domestic **carbon credit** sales from corporate income
tax.

## Royal Decree No. 779 — Investment Token Transfers

Issued 13 August 2023, published in the Royal Gazette 15 August 2023, effective the
following day. It defines an **"investment token" (โทเคนดิจิทัลเพื่อการลงทุน)** as a
digital token whose purpose is to confer a right to **co-invest** in a project or venture
under the Digital Asset Business law.

- **Section 4:** companies and juristic partnerships are **exempt from income tax and
  VAT** on income or tax-base value arising from the transfer of investment tokens
  **offered to the public** under the Digital Asset Business law, for transfers made
  **from 14 May 2018 onward** (retroactive to the law's commencement).
- Where a publicly offered token has **mixed purposes**, the exemption applies **only**
  to the separable investment-token portion.
- **Section 6** independently exempts VAT on investment-token transfers from 14 May 2018.
- The rationale: investment tokens are securities-like fund-raising instruments, so
  their transfer should bear the **same tax burden as a securities transfer**.

Companies must follow conditions set by the Director-General of the Revenue Department;
non-compliance terminates the exemption from the date the benefit was first used.

## Royal Decree No. 760 — Carbon Credit Sales

Issued 18 March 2023, published 19 March 2023. Section 3 exempts corporate **income tax
on net profit from selling carbon credits in Thailand** under voluntary greenhouse-gas
reduction projects **registered with the Thailand Greenhouse Gas Management Organization
(TGO)**, for **three consecutive accounting periods** from the decree's effect through
**31 December 2027**. The first qualifying period begins when TGO issues the carbon-credit
sale certificate.

## Why It Matters

Decree 779 means a Brooker entity transferring publicly offered investment tokens faces
**no income tax or VAT** on that transfer — aligning token economics with securities and
materially affecting after-tax return modelling. Decree 760 supports any carbon-credit
revenue line.

## Agent Notes

Decree 779 exemption is retroactive to 14 May 2018 and applies only to **publicly
offered** investment tokens (separable portion if mixed-purpose). Decree 760 carbon-credit
exemption runs for 3 accounting periods, ending no later than 31 Dec 2027.
