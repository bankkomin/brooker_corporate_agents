# China Investment & Legal Reference (Yunnan Focus)

## Summary

The `จีน` (China) folder is a set of **foreign-jurisdiction reference material**, not
Thai SEC regulation. It collects Thai-language background on **investing in Yunnan
Province, PRC**, plus Thai translations of core Chinese statutes (civil, tax and labour
law). The newest file timestamp is **9 August 2013**, so the material is dated and should
be treated as historical orientation rather than current law.

## What the Folder Contains

- **Yunnan investment guide** (`ลู่ทางการลงทุน...`) and **China economic structure /
  Yunnan economic policy** documents — covering Yunnan as a tourism- and
  resource-rich western province, China's "Go West" development policy, the **Kunming
  Economic & Technology Development Zone**, wage levels and the requirement for
  employers to pay social-security contributions, and the strict **foreign-exchange
  controls** governing profit repatriation (approval by China's foreign-exchange
  control department, clean and transparent accounts, no outstanding tax/wage/utility
  arrears).
- **Yunnan province profile** (`มณฑลหยูนาน.doc`).
- Thai translations of the **PRC Civil Law**, **PRC Tax Law** and **PRC Labour Law**.
- A saved web-page fragment on a Chinese law firm (`.mht`) — unsupported format, no
  substantive content; noted only as a contact pointer.

## Why It Matters

This is a low-priority, point-in-time reference set kept for context on potential China /
Yunnan investment routes. Its key practical caution is that **profit repatriation from
the PRC is legally permissible but operationally constrained** by foreign-exchange
approval and clean-accounts requirements. Any live China investment analysis must use
**current** Chinese law, not these 2013 documents.

## Related Concepts

None — this material sits outside the Thai SEC regulatory framework.
