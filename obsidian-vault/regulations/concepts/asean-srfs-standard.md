# ASEAN Sustainable & Responsible Fund Standard (ASEAN SRFS)

## Summary

The **ASEAN Sustainable & Responsible Fund Standard (ASEAN SRFS)** is a regional fund
standard issued jointly by ASEAN capital-market regulators — including the Thai SEC
([[thai-sec]]) — under the **ASEAN Capital Markets Forum (ACMF)**. A Thai SRI Fund that
discloses fully in line with ASEAN SRFS requirements can be **offered for sale across
ASEAN markets more readily**, lowering the cross-border distribution barrier for
sustainable funds.

## Key Facts

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Issuing body | ASEAN capital-market regulators via the ASEAN Capital Markets Forum (ACMF) |
| Programme context | Part of the ACMF 5-year roadmap (**2021–2025**) |
| Purpose | A common sustainability-disclosure standard enabling cross-border ASEAN offering of sustainable/responsible funds |
| Relationship to Thai SRI Fund | A Thai [[sri-fund-regime|SRI Fund]] meeting ASEAN SRFS disclosure can passport more easily into other ASEAN markets |

## Related Cross-Border Mechanism

Separate from ASEAN SRFS, the Thai SEC was negotiating with **Hong Kong's Securities and
Futures Commission** to allow Thai AMCs to distribute SRI Funds in Hong Kong under the
**HK-TH MRF** (Hong Kong–Thailand Mutual Recognition of Funds) scheme.
