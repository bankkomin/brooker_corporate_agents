# Source — FAP Explanatory Manual to TFRS 2

## Document Overview

- **As-of date:** published by the Federation of Accounting Professions (FAP, สภา
  วิชาชีพบัญชี) on 4 February 2019; file modified 9 March 2023.
- **Folder:** `TFRS 02 share base payment (กฎ การจ่ายโดยใช้หุ้นเป็นเกณฑ์)`.
- The manual is explicitly **not** part of the standard itself; it explains
  application of TFRS 2.

## Key Findings

- Restates the recognition principles for share-based payment to employees and others.
- Provides worked examples distinguishing share-based payment from (a) shares issued
  to people in their shareholder capacity, (b) shares issued as part of a business
  combination, and (c) a major shareholder selling shares below fair value with
  service/supply conditions attached.
