# Delisting of Listed Securities — SET Regulation B.E. 2564

## Summary

The SET Regulation on **Delisting of Listed Securities B.E. 2564 (2021)** sets the
rules, conditions and procedures by which listed securities are removed from the
exchange. It is issued by the SET Board under **Section 170, paragraph two (1) and
(17) of the Securities and Exchange Act B.E. 2535**, with the approval of the Thai
SEC. **Effective 1 November 2021.** This is the formal SET regulation (ข้อบังคับ); the
operational grounds and SP/NC procedure are summarised in [[delisting-criteria]].

## Key Points

- **Two delisting cases:**
  1. **Delisting of shares** — follows the rules, conditions and procedures in this
     regulation.
  2. **Delisting of listed securities other than shares** — the SET Board has
     discretion to decide and may set conditions as it sees fit (except where specific
     delisting rules already exist for that security type).
- **Voluntary delisting** (การเพิกถอนหุ้นโดยสมัครใจ): a listed company that wishes to
  have its own shares delisted must follow the rules, conditions and procedures set by
  the SET Board; once it has fully complied, the Board considers the request.
- The regulation also covers involuntary delisting where a company enters and fails to
  cure a possible-delisting cause.

## Key Definitions

- **Listed company** — a company whose ordinary shares are listed on SET.
- **Convertible security** — a security whose rights may convert into shares.
- **Foreign listed company (Secondary Listing)** and **Home Exchange** — as defined in
  the SET listing-of-shares regulation.

## Historical Context

The delisting rules interlock with the [[set-listed-company-supervision-upgrade-2024]]
warning-sign regime — a company that enters a possible-delisting cause and fails to
cure it within the prescribed period may face delisting; if it cures the cause and
resumes trading (Resume Trading) it is reassessed like a new listing. This article
reflects the copy filed alongside the [[acquisition-disposal-of-assets]] materials in
the project archive.
