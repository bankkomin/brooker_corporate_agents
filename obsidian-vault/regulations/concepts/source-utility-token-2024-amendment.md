# Source: SEC News Release 167/2567 — Utility Token Supervision Amendment

## Document Overview

**As of 14 August 2024.** Thai SEC press release No. 167/2567 announcing the
amended supervisory framework for ready-to-use utility tokens, plus two
technical appendices. Folder `กฎ SEC_TOKEN`. Effective 13 August 2024.

## Key Findings

- The reform is delivered through **7 Capital Market Supervisory Board
  notifications** (กจ. 16/2567, กจ. 17/2567, กธ. 18–22/2567), all published in
  the Royal Gazette.
- **Primary market:** Group 1 ready-to-use utility tokens (consumption /
  certificate purpose) are exempt from offering approval; Group 2 tokens
  intended for listing need SEC approval, a filing, a prospectus, and sale via
  an approved ICO portal.
- **No staking, no Means-of-Payment** function for any utility token, except
  permitted staking forms.
- Appendix to กธ. 18/2567 defines three categories: MOP tokens, staking, and
  project-exchange tokens — and lists which usages (gas fee, cross-chain
  oracle, proof-of-stake, governance voting, fee discount, GameFi/metaverse)
  do **not** make a token a MOP.
- Appendix 3 to กธ. 22/2567 sets exchange **listing criteria** (exclude meme
  and fan tokens, exclude issuer-related-party tokens), a **6-month silent
  period** for tokens received below offering price, **delisting triggers**,
  and ongoing issuer **disclosure** duties.

## Relevance

Defines the Thai regulatory boundary for digital-token products and exchange
listings. Cross-referenced from the master concept [[utility-token-regulation]].
