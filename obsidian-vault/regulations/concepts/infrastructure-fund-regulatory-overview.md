# Infrastructure Fund — Regulatory Overview (2024 Seminar)

## Summary

The single document in this batch's `2024 Update Regulations by Seminar` folder is a
2024 seminar deck (FA 103/1, dated 27 May 2024) introducing the **Infrastructure Fund**
(กองทุนรวมโครงสร้างพื้นฐาน, "infra fund") as a capital-raising vehicle in Thailand.

## Rationale

Good-quality, broadly accessible infrastructure is a primary driver of economic potential
and quality of life, while the State faces budget and public-debt constraints. The
infrastructure fund is the market mechanism that lets infrastructure assets be financed
by private capital rather than the State balance sheet.

## State Tax Support

The deck highlights the government tax incentives that make the infra-fund structure
attractive:

- **Dividend-income exemption** for individual unitholders for **10 years** from the
  fund's registration date (with 10% withholding tax).
- Reduced transfer and mortgage-registration fees.
- Exemption from **VAT / Specific Business Tax / stamp duty** on transactions transferring
  assets into the infrastructure fund.

The deck also covers the general features and rules of infrastructure funds and gives
examples of capital-raising through the structure.

## Relevance

Background on a fund structure relevant to large-scale asset financing. This batch holds
only the introductory seminar deck; the detailed infrastructure-fund rules are primary
material handled in other regulation batches. Treat this article as a high-level
orientation, not the operative rulebook.
