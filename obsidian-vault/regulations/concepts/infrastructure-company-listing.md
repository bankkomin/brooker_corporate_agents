# Infrastructure Company — SET Listing Characteristics

## Summary

This article covers the SET reference material on **Infrastructure Companies**
(บริษัทที่ประกอบธุรกิจโครงสร้างพื้นฐาน) and the characteristics required for an
infrastructure operating company to list on the Stock Exchange of Thailand. The folder
is dated as-of **24 January 2023**.

## Scope of the Source Set

The material is a set of SET informational pages and brochures, not a single
notification:

- *รู้จัก Infrastructure Company* — an introduction to what qualifies as an
  infrastructure company.
- *ลักษณะของ Infrastructure ที่จะเข้าจดทะเบียนใน SET* — the characteristics an
  infrastructure business must meet to list on the SET.
- *Infrastructure Business* and *Companies with investment in infrastructure businesses*
  — SET listing-service pages on equity listing for infrastructure businesses.
- A saved-webpage bundle (`สินค้าและบริการ - ... การเข้าจดทะเบียนตราสารทุน.html`) plus
  its asset folder — saved-webpage clutter, no substantive content beyond the above.

## Key Points

- Infrastructure businesses span transport, utilities, energy, telecommunications, and
  related systems — broadly the same asset categories eligible for an
  [[infrastructure-trust-listing]].
- The SET provides a dedicated equity-listing pathway and informational guidance for
  companies operating or investing in infrastructure businesses.
- Detailed financial and operational admission criteria follow the general SET
  listing rules (tightened by the 2024 governance uplift).

## Why It Matters

For Brooker, the infrastructure-company and Infra Trust routes are alternative
structures for holding or listing infrastructure exposure with public capital.
