# Source — TorChor. 45/2568: Rules on Material Transactions

## Document Overview

Notification of the Capital Market Supervisory Board No. **TorChor. 45/2568** — "Rules on
Material Transactions" (หลักเกณฑ์ในการทำรายการที่มีนัยสำคัญ), dated **19 December 2025**,
issued under Sections 16/6(2)(1), 89/27 and 89/29 of the Securities and Exchange Act
B.E. 2535 (as amended No. 4, B.E. 2551). Effective **1 July 2026** (1 July B.E. 2569).
It repeals TorChor. 20/2551 and its amendment TorChor. 21/2553.

## Key Findings

- **Scope (Clause 4):** listed companies and Section-56-reporting public companies.
- **Definition of MT (Clause 6):** entering, agreeing or reaching any understanding,
  directly or indirectly, that creates a transaction of the size in Part 2 — including
  conduct whose **true substance** is an MT. Four MT categories: (1) acquisition/disposal
  of assets; (2) transfer/waiver of rights and benefits, including waiving claims against
  parties who harmed the company; (3) entering/amending/terminating leases of business or
  assets; (4) financial assistance — loans, credit, guarantees, obligation-creating
  juristic acts where a third party lacks liquidity.
- **Carve-outs (Clause 7):** transactions between the company and its subsidiary, between
  subsidiaries, formation of a new subsidiary, liquidity-management transactions, current
  assets used in normal business, and lease/financial-assistance items that are normal
  business.
- **Size bands (Clause 8):** ≥ 10% for companies with negative NAV or operating loss
  where the deal could worsen finances; ≥ 25% for all other companies. Use the highest of
  the Appendix 1 methods, on the latest audited/reviewed financial statements.
- **Aggregation (Clause 9):** related or same-project transactions in the prior 12 months
  count as one; shareholder-approved transactions are excluded.
- **Approval (Clause 10):** EGM approval at **≥ 3/4** of votes of shareholders present and
  eligible, excluding interested shareholders. Where AC or IFA opposes, holders of
  **≥ 10%** of votes present can veto.
- **IFA opinion required (Clause 12):** for ≥ 25% deals in the Clause 8(1) (adverse) case,
  and ≥ 50% deals in the Clause 8(2) case.
- **Progress reporting (Clause 17):** 6-monthly progress reports (by 31 Jul / 31 Jan)
  until completion, plus immediate report of material failure or cancellation; also
  carried in Form 56-1 One Report.
- **Exemptions:** Clause 14 (competitively sensitive deals), Clause 15 (subsidiary that is
  itself listed and obtained its own approval), Clause 16 (financial institutions under
  Bank of Thailand-approved merger/transfer plans, or voluntary delisting).
- **Transitional (Clause 18):** MTs whose board resolution to table the agenda predates
  1 July 2026 remain under the old TorChor. 20/2551.

## Relevance

This is one of the two operative notifications behind the
[[material-transactions-rpt-2026-reform]] concept. Brooker (BTC) as a listed company is
bound by it from 1 July 2026.
