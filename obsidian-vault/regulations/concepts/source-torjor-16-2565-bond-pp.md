# Source: Notification TorJor. 16/2565 — Private Placement of Debt Instruments

## Document Overview

**As-of:** Capital Market Supervisory Board **Notification No. TorJor. 16/2565** (ประกาศ
คณะกรรมการกำกับตลาดทุน ที่ ทจ. 16/2565), "Application and Approval for the Offering of
Newly Issued Debt Instruments to Limited Groups of Persons, and the Offering of
Convertible Debentures to Persons of Specific Characteristics." **Effective 1 October
2022.** Extract from a Feb 2025 regulatory pack.

## Key Findings

- Establishes the **private-placement (PP) regime** for newly issued debt instruments,
  defined as **debentures, bonds and bills/notes** (หุ้นกู้, พันธบัตร, ตั๋วเงิน).
- Defines key sub-types: **convertible debenture** (หุ้นกู้แปลงสภาพ), **reserved shares**
  (หุ้นรองรับ) to support conversion, and **subordinated debenture** (หุ้นกู้ด้อยสิทธิ),
  ranking below ordinary creditors.
- Issued under Sections 16/6, 89/27, 35, 40(11), 41(3)–(4), 42(10) and 43(3) of the
  Securities and Exchange Act B.E. 2535 (amended by Act No. 4, B.E. 2551).
- Must be read with the SEC notification defining standard terms for all debt-instrument
  offerings, and with **Appendix 3** post-issuance terms.

## Relevance

This is the operative notification a Brooker-type issuer relies on to raise debt via
private placement — the lower-cost, faster alternative to a public debt offering. It is
the primary document behind the [[bond-private-placement]] concept.

## Extracted Data

- Effective date: 1 October 2022.
- Eligible instruments: debentures, bonds, bills/notes (newly issued).
