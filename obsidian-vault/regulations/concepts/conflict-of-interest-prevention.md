# Conflict-of-Interest Prevention and Management Rules

## Summary

The SEC maintains a consolidated notification setting out detailed requirements on the
**prevention and management of conflicts of interest**
(ข้อกำหนดในรายละเอียดเกี่ยวกับการป้องกันและจัดการความขัดแย้งทางผลประโยชน์). It obliges
regulated entities to identify, prevent and manage situations where the interests of the
firm, its personnel or related parties could conflict with the interests of clients or
investors.

## Relevance

Conflict-of-interest management is closely tied to the connected-transaction regime — see
[[material-transactions-rpt-2026-reform]], whose RPT half (TorChor. 46/2568) governs
transactions with directors, major shareholders and their affiliates. For day-to-day
operations, this notification is the firm-level conduct counterpart to the deal-level RPT
rules.
