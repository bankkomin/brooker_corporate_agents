# Source: GULF Tender Offer for ADVANC Securities (Form 247-4, 2021)

## Document Overview

**Offer dated 28 June 2021** (extract file modified 2023-05-18). The Form 247-4
tender offer by **Gulf Energy Development PCL** for all securities of **Advanced
Info Service PCL (ADVANC)**, prepared by Bualuang Securities. Held in folder
`2025 To Regulation_ Tender Offer ... (as of 4-Aug-2025)` as a reference example.

## Key Findings

- GULF intended to acquire **INTUCH Holdings**; INTUCH held **40.45%** of ADVANC.
  Reaching ≥50% of INTUCH voting rights triggered a **Chain Principle** obligation
  to tender for ADVANC under Notification TorChor. 12/2554.
- GULF launched the ADVANC offer on the **same business day** as the INTUCH offer
  to satisfy the chain obligation and limit market disruption.
- The Takeover Panel / SEC granted **waivers** of the obligation to re-tender for
  ADVANC under the chain rule and of the obligation to pre-fund the ADVANC offer.
- Offer: **2,973,925,791 ordinary shares = 100%** of ADVANC's issued and paid-up
  shares; ESOP warrants were excluded as non-exercisable by GULF.
- Offer price: **THB 120.93/share** (final, non-amendable subject to conditions);
  net to offeree **THB 120.606512** after 0.25% brokerage fee + 7% VAT.

## Relevance

A concrete worked example of the Chain Principle and the indirect-acquisition
offer-price method. Cross-referenced from the master concept
