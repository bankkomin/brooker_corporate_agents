# Source — Approved SRI Fund List (early 2024)

## Document Overview

- **As-of date:** Fund data shown "as of 08 March 2024" (ข้อมูล ณ 08-Mar-24); source files
  modified 2024-03-12.
- **Type:** Tabular registers of SEC-approved SRI Funds — one Excel master list
  (`รายชื่อกองทุนรวมเพื่อความยั่งยืน SRI Fund.xlsx`) and a PDF extract covering the SCB and
  Bualuang asset managers.
- **Coverage:** ~47 fund / share-class entries; this archive's "16 funds" label refers to
  an earlier cut and the folder grew as more funds were approved.

## Key Findings

- Each entry records: fund name, fund type (ตราสารทุน equity / ผสม mixed / ตราสารหนี้
  fixed-income), tax-privilege status (most carry **Thai ESG**), investment policy summary,
  unit NAV, fund NAV (THB millions), data date, and fund registration date.
- The majority of listed SRI Funds were **registered 18–19 December 2023**, coinciding with
  the launch of the Thai ESG tax-incentive scheme — confirming that the SRI Fund population
  expanded sharply alongside Thai ESG.
- **Investment policies are standardised** around: ≥80% of NAV (annual average) in
  sustainable/ESG-distinguished SET- or mai-listed equities; ESG Integration analysis or
  ESG-index tracking (SET ESG Total Return Index / SETTHSI); up to 20% in other in-house
  funds; derivatives permitted only for efficient portfolio management or hedging.
- **Asset managers represented** include Krung Thai (KTAM), Krungsri, Kasikorn (K),
  KKP, KWI, TISCO, SCB Asset Management, Bualuang (BBLAM), Principal, UOB Asset
  Management, LH Fund, ONE/UNNA (วรรณ), Eastspring, MFC, and Asset Plus — see
  [[thai-esg-fund]] for the AMC landscape.
- Example data points: B-TOP-THAIESG (Bualuang) NAV THB 1,061.57M; SCBTM(ThaiESG) THB
  448.29M; SCBTP(ThaiESG) THB 230.77M — all as of 08-Mar-24.

## Relevance

This is the registry behind the [[sri-fund-regime]] concept and the data source for the
[[sri-fund-approvals-2023-2024]] trend. It demonstrates how SRI Fund labelling and the
Thai ESG tax scheme combined to produce a wave of new sustainable funds in late 2023.
