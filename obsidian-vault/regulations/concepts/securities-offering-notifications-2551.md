# SEC Securities Offering Notifications B.E. 2551 (Issuance Suite)

## Summary

This folder ("การเสนอขายหุ้น" — securities offering) collects the **B.E. 2551 suite of
SEC / Capital Market Supervisory Board notifications** that together form the core
framework for issuing and offering securities in Thailand. They are the consolidated
(ฉบับประมวล) texts. An index sheet (สารบัญ) in the folder confirms the suite is curated as
"rules concerning securities offering."

## The Notifications

| Notification | Subject |
|--------------|---------|
| **กจ.17/2551** | Definitions used across the securities offering and issuance notifications — the shared glossary for the whole suite |
| **กจ.18/2551** | Exemptions from filing a securities-offering registration statement (filing) |
| **ทจ.28/2551** | Application for, and grant of, approval to offer **newly issued shares** — the primary equity-offering approval rule |
| **ทจ.29/2551** | Subscription, distribution, and allotment of securities |
| **ทจ.30/2551** | Filing of the securities-offering registration statement (filing) |
| **ทจ.32/2551** | Offering of newly issued securities to **directors or employees** (ESOP) |
| **ทจ.9/2552** | Application for, and grant of, approval to offer **newly issued debt securities** (bonds) |

## How the Suite Fits Together

A securities offering in Thailand runs through three layers, each governed by part of this
suite:

1. **Definitions (กจ.17/2551)** — fixes the meaning of terms ("subsidiary," "associate,"
   "private placement," etc.) used by every other notification.
2. **Approval to offer** — equity via ทจ.28/2551, debt via ทจ.9/2552, ESOP via ทจ.32/2551.
3. **Disclosure and distribution** — filing the registration statement (ทจ.30/2551), filing
   exemptions (กจ.18/2551), and the subscription/allotment process (ทจ.29/2551).

## Why It Matters

These B.E. 2551 notifications are the statutory base for every Brooker-relevant equity or
debt issuance, public offering, private placement, or ESOP grant. Later reforms — including
the new-share offering approval updates and the 2566 PP revision — amend rather than replace
this suite. ทจ.28/2551 in particular is the workhorse equity-offering approval rule
(superseded for some purposes by ทจ.39/2559, referenced in the SET listing criteria).
