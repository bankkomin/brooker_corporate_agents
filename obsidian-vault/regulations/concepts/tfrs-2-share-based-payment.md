# TFRS 2 — Share-Based Payment

## Summary

Thai Financial Reporting Standard No. 2 (TFRS 2), **Share-Based Payment** (มาตรฐานการ
รายงานทางการเงิน ฉบับที่ 2 เรื่อง การจ่ายโดยใช้หุ้นเป็นเกณฑ์), governs how a company
recognises in its financial statements transactions in which it receives goods or
services and pays for them with shares, share options or cash whose amount is based on
the company's share price. It is the Thai adoption of **IFRS 2**, issued by the
Federation of Accounting Professions (สภาวิชาชีพบัญชี). The project archive holds the
**TFRS 2 revised B.E. 2565 (2022)** text.

## Key Definition

**Share-based payment** is a transaction in which the entity (1) receives goods or
services from a counterparty (including employees) under a share-based-payment
arrangement, or (2) incurs an obligation to settle such a transaction when another
group entity receives the goods or services.

## Key Points

- **Scope** — applies to all share-based-payment transactions, whether or not the
  entity can identify the goods/services received:
  - **Equity-settled** — settled with the entity's own equity instruments (e.g. ESOP
    shares/options);
  - **Cash-settled** — settled with cash (or other assets) for an amount based on
    share price; and
  - **Choice-of-settlement** transactions where either party may choose cash or
    equity.
- A transaction may be settled by another group entity (or the group's shareholders)
  on behalf of the entity receiving the goods/services; TFRS 2 still applies.
- **Fair value** under TFRS 2 has a meaning **different from** the fair-value
  definition in TFRS 13; entities must measure under TFRS 2's own requirements.

### What is *not* share-based payment

- Issuing shares to employees/others in their **capacity as shareholders** (e.g. a
  rights-style discount offered to all shareholders of a group) — not TFRS 2.
- Shares issued as **part of a business combination** — accounted for under TFRS 3,
  not TFRS 2.
- A major shareholder selling shares to employees/suppliers below fair value, with
  service or supply conditions attached, **is** within TFRS 2.

## Changes in the Revised Standard

TFRS 2 (revised 2018 / ปรับปรุง 2561) made substantive changes versus the 2017
version, adding accounting treatments for:

- vesting and non-vesting conditions of **cash-settled** share-based payments;
- share-based-payment transactions with a **net-settlement feature** for an employee's
  withholding-tax obligation; and
- modifications that **reclassify** an award from cash-settled to equity-settled.
