# SET Listed-Company Supervision Upgrade — 25 March 2024

## Summary

Part 2 of SET circular **บจ. (ว) 2/2567** tightened supervision of **listed companies
that present risk** — weak financial position, ongoing losses, low financial liquidity,
or qualifications that do not meet exchange criteria. It introduced wider, clearer
warning signs so investors are informed of a company's status. **Effective 25 March
2024.**

## Key Points

- New action is taken against: companies **with no business**, companies with
  **continuous losses for 3 years**, and companies that have **defaulted on financial-
  institution debt or debt instruments** (ตราสารหนี้).
- Revised treatment where the **auditor disclaims an opinion** (ไม่แสดงความเห็น) on the
  financial statements.
- Revised treatment where a listed company fails qualification criteria: being a
  **Cash Company**, lacking a proper **Audit Committee**, failing the **Free Float**
  requirement, or not holding an **Opportunity Day** as required.
- Greater scrutiny of companies entering by **Backdoor Listing** and companies in
  **Resume Trading** after curing a possible-delisting cause — treated like a New
  Listing.
- Criteria aligned with Thai SEC rules; outdated rules repealed or revised.

## Warning Signs / Conditions

The circular maps risk conditions to trading signs and remedial conditions. Notable
mechanics referenced:

- A "**C**" (Caution) sign continues to mark companies whose financial position,
  results, liquidity or qualifications fall short.
- When a company carries the C sign, investors must trade its securities on a **Cash
  Balance** basis, and the company must hold a **Public Presentation** for investors.
- Indicative thresholds cited: shareholders' equity < 50% of paid-up capital; net
  profit from operations < 100M baht (SET) / 50M baht (mai); equity < 100% of paid-up
  capital after 3 consecutive years of losses; possible referral to a **Special
  Audit** (special-purpose audit, CS sign) where the auditor disclaims an opinion or
  the SEC orders one.

## Historical Context

This is the front-line supervision counterpart to the [[set-ipo-listing-criteria-2025]]
admission upgrade — together they form SET's "raise governance across the whole
process" program announced in February 2024.
