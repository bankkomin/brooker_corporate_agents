# Source: Disclosure Guideline — Financial Assistance & Default

## Document Overview

**As-of:** modified 2018-09-04. A disclosure guideline (แนวทางการเปิดเผยสารสนเทศ) for
listed companies covering two situations: **giving financial assistance** (การให้ความ
ช่วยเหลือทางการเงิน) and a counterparty's **default on payment** (การผิดนัดชำระ). Held in
the financial-assistance folder of the Thai SEC regulations archive.

## Key Findings

- The guideline addresses **what a listed company must disclose** when it provides
  financial assistance (loans, guarantees, similar support) and when a borrower or
  counterparty **defaults**.
- It is the disclosure companion to the substantive financial-assistance rules
  (see [[financial-assistance-rules]]).

## Relevance

For a holding company that lends to subsidiaries, associates, or related parties, this
guideline defines the trigger and content of market disclosures — both on entering the
arrangement and on a subsequent default. Default disclosure is a material-event obligation.
