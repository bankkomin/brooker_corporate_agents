# Source: Public Limited Companies Act B.E. 2535 (Consolidated)

## Document Overview

**As-of:** latest consolidated version (รวมล่าสุด) of the **Public Limited Companies Act
B.E. 2535** (พระราชบัญญัติบริษัทมหาชนจำกัด พ.ศ. 2535), the Thai statute governing the
formation, structure, and operation of public limited companies. Held in the
`Collection of Laws` folder of the Thai SEC regulations archive.

## Key Findings

- The Act governs **public limited company** matters: incorporation, share capital, board
  of directors, shareholder meetings, dividends, capital increases/reductions, mergers,
  and dissolution.
- It is the **corporate-law counterpart** to the Securities and Exchange Act B.E. 2535:
  the Securities Act regulates a company's *securities and market conduct*, while this Act
  regulates its *corporate existence and internal governance*.
- A Brooker-type entity ("PCL" / "PLC") is incorporated and operates under this Act.

## Relevance

This statute underpins board composition, shareholder-meeting procedure, and
capital-structure actions (capital increase, reduction, par change) for any Thai listed
company. SEC/SET listing and disclosure rules sit on top of it.
