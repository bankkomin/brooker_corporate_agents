# Reporting of Securities Acquisition or Disposal and Tender-Offer Opinions

## Summary

This topic gathers three SEC/CMSB notifications on the **reporting of acquisition or
disposal of securities** and on **the form and timeframe for preparing an opinion on a
tender offer** (คำเสนอซื้อหลักทรัพย์):

- **TorChor. 28/2554** — requirements on **reporting the acquisition or disposal of
  securities** (the substantial-shareholding / Section 246 reporting line).
- **TorChor. 40/2552** — the **form and timeframe** for a target company to prepare its
  opinion on a tender offer.
- **TorKor. 54/2559 (No. 2)** — an amendment to the form-and-timeframe rules for the
  tender-offer opinion.

## Relevance

These rules sit alongside the tender-offer and substantial-acquisition regime. They are
relevant to Brooker where the group crosses a substantial-shareholding reporting
threshold, or where a Brooker-affiliated company is the target of a tender offer and must
issue a target-board opinion within the prescribed window.
