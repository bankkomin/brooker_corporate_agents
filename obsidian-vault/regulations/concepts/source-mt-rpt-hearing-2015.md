# Source: MT & RPT Reform — 2015 Public Hearing (อพษ. 23/2558)

## Document Overview

SEC public-hearing paper No. **อพษ. 23/2558**, *Re: Improvement of rules on Material
Transactions (MT) and Related Party Transactions (RPT) of listed companies*, published
**5 November 2015** with a comment deadline of **3 February 2016**. This is the earlier
consultation that began the long arc of MT/RPT reform later completed by the
[[material-transaction-related-party-2026]] notifications.

## Key Findings

- The 2015 consultation aimed to align the MT/RPT rules with the increasingly diverse
  and complex transactions of listed companies as they moved into new businesses.
- The proposal had four parts of rules and good-practice guidance, opening with
  improvements to the conduct of Material Transactions.
- The folder also holds a "PP Tool Kit" (private-placement reference) and a
  comment-deadline notice — supporting material, not substantive new rules.

## Relevance

Useful as historical context: it documents the regulatory intent (transaction
diversity, investor protection) that ultimately produced the 1 July 2026 regime. Treat
as superseded background — the operative law is the 2025/2026 notifications.
