# Securities and Exchange Act B.E. 2535 — English Reference Translations

## Summary

This article covers the **English-language reference translations** of Thailand's core
capital-markets statutes, collected in the `English` source folder. The headline statute
is the **Securities and Exchange Act B.E. 2535 (1992)** (SEA), the legislative basis for
the SEC, the SET, securities offerings, and the takeover regime. English texts are
provided strictly for reference — **only the original Thai text has legal force**.

## Documents in This Set

| Statute / Notification | Note |
|------------------------|------|
| **Securities and Exchange Act B.E. 2535 (As Amended)** | Core capital-markets law. Given 12 March 1992; in force 60 days after publication (with listed exceptions effective the next day). Repealed the SET Acts B.E. 2517 and B.E. 2527 (No. 2). |
| **Securities and Exchange Act B.E. 2551 (No. 4) — unofficial translation** | The 2008 amendment, which substantially restructured SEC/Capital Market Supervisory Board governance and added Sections such as 89/27 (later cited by debt-instrument notifications). |
| **Public Limited Companies Act B.E. 2535** | English translation of the corporate-law statute — see [[public-limited-companies-act-2535]]. |
| **BorJorPor 22-01 (Bor.Jor./Por.22-01)** | SET Board of Governors Notification, *Disclosure of Information and Other Acts of Listed Companies Concerning Connected Transactions, 2003* — in force 15 December 2003 (Clause 15 effective six months later). The original connected-transaction disclosure rule predating the [[material-transaction-related-party-2026]] reform. |

## Why It Matters

These English texts let Brooker's English-working advisors and counterparties read the
primary law directly. The SEA underpins the [[takeover-and-tender-offer-regime]]
(Chapter 8 Part 2) and the SEC's authority over MT/RPT and debt-instrument rules.
BorJorPor 22-01 is the historical SET connected-transaction baseline.
