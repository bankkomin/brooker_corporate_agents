# SET / mai Common-Share Listing Criteria (Aug 2563)

## Summary

A SET reference summary (August 2020) of the criteria for listing common shares of a
**general-business company** on the Stock Exchange of Thailand (SET) or the Market for
Alternative Investment (mai). Listing requires meeting both **share qualifications** and
**company qualifications**, then a two-track approval (SEC offering approval, then SET
admission).

## Share Qualifications

- Par value (Par) **≥ THB 0.50** per share, fully paid.
- Registered holders; freely transferable except restrictions required by law (which must
  be in the articles of association).

## Company Qualifications

| Criterion | SET | mai |
|-----------|-----|-----|
| Status | Public limited company or special-law entity | Same |
| Paid-up capital (common shares, post-IPO) | > THB 300M | > THB 50M |
| Shareholders' equity | > THB 300M (and > 0 pre-IPO) | > THB 50M (and > 0 pre-IPO) |
| Profit test — track record | ≥ 3 years operations, ≥ 1 year same management | ≥ 2 years operations, ≥ 1 year same management |
| Profit test — net profit | Net profit ≥ THB 50M over last 2–3 years, with ≥ THB 30M in the latest year + positive cumulative interim period | Net profit ≥ THB 10M in the latest year + positive cumulative interim period |
| Minority shareholding | ≥ 1,000 minority holders | ≥ 300 minority holders |
| Free-float ratio | ≥ 25% of paid-up capital (≥ 20% if paid-up > THB 3,000M) | Same |

The SET also offers a **Market Cap test** (alternative to the profit test): market
capitalisation > THB 7,500M plus EBIT-positive latest year and cumulative interim period,
with the same 3-year/1-year track-record condition.

## Public Offering of Shares

- SEC offering approval required (except special-law entities); offered through a securities
  underwriter.
- Number of shares offered: SET — > 15% of paid-up capital (> 10% if paid-up > THB 500M and
  par value of common shares > THB 75M, whichever higher); mai — > 15% of paid-up capital.

## Other Conditions

- **Silent Period** — Strategic Shareholders are barred from selling shares totalling 55%
  of post-IPO paid-up capital for **1 year** from first trading; 25% may be released after
  each 6 months.
- Board, executives, controlling persons must have no prohibited characteristics; CFO and
  accounting supervisor qualifications per Capital Market Supervisory Board notification
  ทจ.39/2559; chairman and managing director must be different persons.
- Audit Committee, good corporate governance, adequate internal control, no conflict of
  interest, provident fund established, TSD (or SET-approved person) as registrar.
- **Opportunity Day** required within 1 year of first trading.

## Strategic Shareholders Defined

Directors, managers, executives and related persons; shareholders holding > 5% of paid-up
capital and related persons; controlling persons. Minority shareholders are everyone else.

## Process Timeline

File offering application with the SEC (file Filing); SEC counts day 1 and decides within
120 days of complete documents; offer IPO shares within 6 months of SEC approval; file SET
listing application; shares begin trading at T+1 after SET admission announcement.

## Spin-off

A listed company may list a subsidiary or associate with potential ("Spin off") by IPO; the
parent must still satisfy maintenance-of-listed-status criteria afterwards.

## Why It Matters

Defines the eligibility bar a Brooker portfolio company (or a backdoor-listing combined
entity) must clear. SET imposes materially higher capital, profit, and float thresholds
than mai.
