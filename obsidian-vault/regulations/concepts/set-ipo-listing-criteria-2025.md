# SET IPO Listing Criteria — 2024/2025 Upgrade

## Summary

The Stock Exchange of Thailand (ตลาดหลักทรัพย์ฯ) raised its securities-admission
(listing) criteria for new companies via circular **บจ. (ว) 2/2567** (19 February
2024). The objective is to strengthen the financial position, operating results and
secondary-market liquidity of newly listed companies on both the main board (SET) and
the Market for Alternative Investment (mai). The package revised or repealed 23
exchange notifications/guidelines in total. The two parts have different effective
dates:

- **Admission-criteria upgrade** (เกณฑ์รับหลักทรัพย์) — effective **1 January 2025**.
- **Risk-company supervision upgrade** (companies with weak finances, losses, low
  liquidity or non-conforming qualifications, including a wider set of warning signs)
  — effective **25 March 2024**. See [[set-listed-company-supervision-upgrade-2024]].

## Key Metrics — New Admission Thresholds (effective 1 Jan 2025)

Companies that file a listing application **before 1 January 2025** are assessed under
the old thresholds; applications from that date use the new ones. Figures below show
old → new.

| Criterion | SET (old → new) | mai (old → new) |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Net profit — latest year | 30M → **75M baht** | 10M → **25M baht** |
| Net profit — combined 2–3 years | 50M → **125M baht** | — → **40M baht** |
| Shareholders' equity (Equity, post-IPO) | 300M → **800M baht** | 50M → **100M baht** |
| Paid-up capital (common shares) | 300M → **100M baht** | 50M → 50M baht |
| Minimum free-float holders | 1,000 holders | 300 holders |

Notes: "Profit" is net profit from operations, which must persist continuously up to
the listing date. Equity must be > 0 before IPO. Paid-up capital for SET was *reduced*
(to support sound businesses that do not need large capital) but kept above the mai
minimum.

### Free Float and IPO proportion (by paid-up size, on par value)

| Paid-up size (X) | SET — % Free Float (old) | mai — % IPO (old) |
|------------------|--------------------------|-------------------|
| 50M < X < 300M baht | 30% (was 25%) | 20% (was 15%) |
| 300M < X < 3,000M (SET) / 300M < X < 500M (mai) | 25% | 15% or 60M baht, whichever higher |
| X > 3,000M (SET) / X > 500M (mai) | 20% | 10% or 75M baht, whichever higher |

## Other Admission Requirements (Profit Test track)

- Company must be a public limited company (or a juristic person established under a
  specific Thai law).
- Common shares: par value (Par) **≥ 0.50 baht/share**, fully paid, registered, with no
  transfer restrictions other than those required by law and stated in the articles.
- Operating track record under substantially the same board/management group: **SET ≥
  3 years**, **mai ≥ 2 years** (with ≥ 1 year continuous before filing).
- Demonstrable stable financial position and sufficient working capital.

## Historical Context

The 2024/2025 upgrade is part of a broader SET program to raise listed-company
governance "across the whole process" — from selection of companies entering the
market to ongoing supervision. It also aligned exchange criteria with Thai SEC rules
and tightened scrutiny of backdoor-listing-style indirect listings and Resume
Trading (treating them like a New Listing). For the supervision/warning-sign side, see
the companion concept [[set-listed-company-supervision-upgrade-2024]].
