# Source — Principles of Good Corporate Governance for Listed Companies (2006)

## Document Overview

- **As-of date:** 2006 (B.E. 2549) edition; file modified 21 June 2006.
- **Issuer:** Corporate Governance development centre, The Stock Exchange of Thailand.
- **Folder:** `CG Thailand's Publications` → `หลักการกำกับดูแลกิจการที่ดีสำหรับบริษัท
  จดทะเบียน ปี 2549 CG_Principle_2005`.

## Key Findings

- Defines corporate governance as the system of structures and processes governing the
  relationship among board, management and shareholders.
- Frames good CG as a value-creation tool consistent with the Sufficiency Economy
  philosophy.
- Organises listed-company governance into the five standard categories (shareholder
  rights; equitable treatment; stakeholders; disclosure; board responsibility).
