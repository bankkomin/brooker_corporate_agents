# Source: ThorJor.16/2565 — PP Offer of Debt Securities

## Document Overview

**As of:** notification text dated 2022; document set received/timestamped 16 February
2023. Capital Market Supervisory Board Notification **ThorJor.16/2565**, *Application and
approval for the offer of newly-issued debt securities to persons in a limited circle
(private placement) and the offer of convertible bonds to persons with specific
characteristics.* Effective **1 October 2022**. Authority: ss.16/6, 89/27, 35, 40(11),
41, 42(10), 43(3) of the Securities and Exchange Act B.E. 2535 (as amended by Act No. 4
B.E. 2551).

## Key Findings

- Defines the regulated **debt securities** as newly-issued bonds, debentures and bills,
  and defines **convertible bonds**, **reserved shares**, **subordinated bonds**, the
  **rights statement** (ข้อกำหนดสิทธิ), the **financial advisor** and the **Thai Bond
  Market Association**.
- Distinguishes offers permitted under a **general approval** from those requiring a
  **case-by-case application** to the SEC Office.
- Carries three appendices: Appendix 1 — an **exemption schedule** listing rule clauses
  waived for specific cases (Thai government-agency debt, foreign-entity debt,
  special-purpose-vehicle bonds, foreign-currency bonds sold and traded entirely abroad,
  bonds to non-Thai institutional investors); Appendix 2 — **additional approval criteria
  and post-approval conditions**; Appendix 3 — **post-approval criteria for convertible
  bond offerings**.

## Relevance

This is the primary SEC instrument for any non-public debt raise by a Thai issuer. It is
read together with ThorJor.19/2565 (registration-statement filing). See the
[[private-placement-debt-securities]] concept for the consolidated view.

## Extracted Data

- Effective date: 1 October 2022.
- Definition of "manager" (ผู้บริหาร): the top executive, the next four executive ranks
  and equivalents, and accounting/finance executives at department-manager level or above.
