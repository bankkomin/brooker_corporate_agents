# Source — Heavy-Metal Groundwater Contamination Explainer (2025)

## Document Overview

- **As-of date:** explanatory note modified 23 July 2025; folder labelled
  "update 4-Aug-2025".
- **Folder:** `2025 กฎ การปนเปื้อนโลหะในดินและน้ำใต้ดิน (update 4-Aug-2025)`.

## Key Findings

- Plain-language explainer of which factory categories must test for heavy-metal and
  other contamination in soil and groundwater under the two B.E. 2559 instruments.
- Lists the higher-risk factory categories and the two contaminant groups (VOCs and
  heavy metals) that must be tested.

## Relevance

Supports the [[soil-groundwater-contamination-control]] concept, which carries the
full analysis.
