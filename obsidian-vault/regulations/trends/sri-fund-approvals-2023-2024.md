# SRI Fund Approvals — Growth 2022 to 2024

## Summary

The population of SEC-approved [[sri-fund-regime|SRI Funds]] grew rapidly after the April
2022 disclosure guideline, accelerating sharply once the **Thai ESG tax incentive**
([[thai-esg-fund]]) launched in December 2023.

## Time Series

| Date | Approved SRI Funds | Note |
|------|-------------------|------|
| April 2022 | — | SEC disclosure guideline issued |
| 31 Dec 2022 | 4 | Per the SEC explainer paper |
| 18–19 Dec 2023 | (wave) | Bulk of Thai ESG-linked SRI Funds registered |
| 08 Mar 2024 | ~47 fund / share-class entries | Per the approved-fund list |

## Drivers

- **April 2022 disclosure guideline** established the SRI Fund label.
- **2023 application-fee waiver** ("2023 New Year Gift", Cabinet resolution 20 Dec 2022)
  removed the cost of establishing or converting to an SRI Fund for 2023 filings.
- **Thai ESG tax incentive** (launched Dec 2023) gave AMCs strong demand-side reason to
  register sustainable funds; most archive entries were registered 18–19 Dec 2023.
