# Federation of Accounting Professions (สภาวิชาชีพบัญชี)

## Overview

The **Federation of Accounting Professions under the Royal Patronage of His Majesty the
King** (สภาวิชาชีพบัญชี ในพระบรมราชูปถัมภ์) is the Thai professional body that issues and
maintains **Thai Financial Reporting Standards (TFRS)** and Thai Accounting Standards. It
is the Thai counterpart of an IFRS-adopting national standard-setter.

## Key Facts

- **Role:** sets and publishes TFRS, the accounting standards Thai listed companies must
  apply in their financial statements.
- **TFRS basis:** TFRS standards are the Thai adoption of the corresponding **IFRS**
  standards (e.g. TFRS 2 corresponds to IFRS 2 — *Share-Based Payment*).
- **Publications:** issues both the standard text and explanatory manuals (คู่มืออธิบาย);
  the explanatory manuals are explicitly **not part of the standard** itself.

## History

The Federation published the TFRS 2 explanatory manual on 4 February 2562 (2019); the
revised TFRS 2 standard text in the regulations archive is the 2565 (2022) revision.
