# SET — The Stock Exchange of Thailand

## Overview

The Stock Exchange of Thailand (ตลาดหลักทรัพย์แห่งประเทศไทย — "ตลท." / "SET") is
Thailand's principal securities exchange. It issues and enforces listing rules, disclosure
requirements, the acquisition/disposal-of-assets (MT) and connected-transaction (RPT)
regulations for listed companies, trading signs (e.g. the "C" sign), and delisting
criteria. The Market for Alternative Investment (MAI) is its secondary board.
