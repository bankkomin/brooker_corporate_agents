# Thai SEC — Securities and Exchange Commission, Thailand

## Overview

The Securities and Exchange Commission, Thailand (สำนักงานคณะกรรมการกำกับหลักทรัพย์
และตลาดหลักทรัพย์ — "ก.ล.ต." / "กลต.") is Thailand's capital-market regulator. It
supervises securities offerings, listed companies, digital-asset businesses, mutual and
infrastructure funds, takeovers, and market conduct. It is the issuing authority behind
most rules summarised in the `regulations` knowledge area.

## Key Attributes

| Attribute | Value |
