# Crypto Insights Group — Research Platform Subscription

## Summary

[[brooker-international-company]] executed a Customer Agreement with
[[crypto-insights-group]] on **23 March 2026** for firm-wide access to CIG's crypto
research-data Platform. The contract was signed via DocuSign by Varut Bulakul (Director,
BICL) and Vincent Molino (COO, CIG).

## Change Details

| Term | Value |
|------|-------|
| Agreement | Customer Agreement |
| Effective Date | When Customer executes and Company issues an invoice |
| Signature date | 23 March 2026 (both parties) |
| Scope | Firm-wide access — **5 seats** |
| Fee | US$18,000 — annual commitment |
| Payment | Billed annually at the outset; due within 14 days of invoicing |
| Access term | 13 months from the Effective Date |
| Forms of payment | ACH/wire, credit card, Stripe options; crypto at CIG's discretion |
| IP | Platform rights remain with CIG; Customer gets a limited, non-transferable license |
| Confidentiality | Mutual non-disclosure of terms and shared confidential information |
| Governing law | State of California |

## Rationale

The CIG Platform supplies crypto-industry research, financial models, expert opinions,
data/insights, databases, sentiment reports and allocator contact data — supporting the
VCC's manager-research and due-diligence workflows for
[[crypto-insights-group|CIG]]-sourced intelligence.

## Risk & Compliance Notes

- **All purchases are final and non-refundable** under any circumstance, including CIG
  discontinuing the service.
- Disputes are resolved by **binding arbitration** (AAA Commercial Rules, seat in
  California) with a **class-action waiver**; a 30-day opt-out from arbitration is
  available.
- Liability is capped at amounts paid in the 3 months prior to a cause of action; the
  Platform is provided "as is" with broad disclaimers.
- Content **may not** be used in any SEC/regulatory filing, nor to train/improve AI
  models, nor systematically scraped to build a competing database.
- Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled; CIG may change prices on notice.

## Impact

Provides the VCC team a sanctioned research-data source. The non-refundable, auto-renew
and AI-training-prohibition terms should be tracked at renewal (≈13 months from the
Effective Date).
