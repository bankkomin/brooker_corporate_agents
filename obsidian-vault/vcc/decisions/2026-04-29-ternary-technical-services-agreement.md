# Ternary x Brooker — Technical Services Agreement

## Summary

[[ternary-fund-management]] (the "Manager") and [[brooker-international-company]] (the
"Technical Service Consultant") entered into a **Technical Services Agreement (TSA)**
dated **29 April 2026**. The TSA appoints Brooker International to provide technical,
brand and ecosystem support services for [[brook-limited-partners-fof]], and sets the fee
mechanism by which Brooker shares in the fund's management fees.

> Filename note: the source file is named "TSA - Jan2026 v2" but the executed document is
> dated 29 April 2026 — this article uses the document date.

## Technical Services Provided

Brooker International, as an **independent contractor** (not a fiduciary, not an agent),
provides:

- Brand licensing, education and ecosystem-development support under the Brooker / Brook
  Technology Capital brands.
- Organising/supporting industry events, conferences and investor-education activities.
- Attending events and updating the Manager on relevant information.
- Technical analysis of blockchain architecture, wallet management, smart contracts,
  security, on-chain analytics, counterparty and integrations.
- Informing the Manager of important developments affecting the Fund.

All recommendations are **non-binding**; all investment decisions remain solely the
Manager's responsibility.

## Fee Mechanism — Technical Services Fee

- The Technical Services Fee is **residual Management Fees**: the Management Fee received
  from [[brook-technology-capital-vcc]] – Brook LP FoF I, **less** (1) Ternary Fees
  (per the engagement letter dated 19 June 2025), (2) portfolio-manager salary, and (3)
  expenses incurred by the Manager.
- Payable **quarterly in arrears**, within 30 days of each quarter-end.
- The same fee structure/deduction methodology applies to any future sub-funds launched
  under the VCC, unless agreed otherwise in writing.
- The fee is consideration for technical/brand/ecosystem services — explicitly **not** an
  investment-advisory, placement or management fee. Taxes are the Consultant's expense.

## Key Provisions

- **Brand & IP** — all IP in "The Brooker Group" and "Brook Technology Capital" brands
  remains with Brooker International; the Manager gets a non-exclusive, non-transferable
  license for use in connection with the Fund during the Term.
- **Not a fiduciary / non-exclusive** — Brooker may provide similar services to others.
- **Liability** — Consultant not liable for indirect/consequential loss; no
  responsibility for the Manager's investment decisions or fundraising outcomes;
  indemnification capped at total Technical Services Fee received.
- Brooker International or affiliates may separately act as an investor or strategic
  capital partner in the Fund on separately negotiated terms.

## Impact

The TSA is the contractual route by which the [[brooker-group]] monetises its brand and
technical input on the VCC platform — a residual share of the Management Fee paid to
[[ternary-fund-management]]. It survives independently of the Investment Management
Agreement.
