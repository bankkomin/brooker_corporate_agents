# Crypto Insights Group Corp.

## Overview

Crypto Insights Group Corp. ("CIG") is a US research-data vendor that operates a
subscription Platform of crypto-industry research, financial models, expert opinions,
data/insights, databases, sentiment reports and contact data on investment firms and
allocators. [[brooker-international-company]] is a CIG customer under the
[[2026-03-23-crypto-insights-platform-subscription]].

## Contract Terms (summary)

See [[2026-03-23-crypto-insights-platform-subscription]] for full detail. Key points:
firm-wide access (5 seats) at **US$18,000 / year**; **13-month** access term; billed
annually, due within 14 days of invoicing; **all purchases final and non-refundable**;
disputes resolved by **binding arbitration** (AAA) with a class-action waiver and 30-day
opt-out.
