# Brooker International Company Limited

## Overview

Brooker International Company Limited ("BICL") is the Hong Kong contracting entity used by
the [[brooker-group]] for the VCC-related agreements. It is the named counterparty on both
the [[2026-04-29-ternary-technical-services-agreement|Ternary Technical Services
Agreement]] and the [[2026-03-23-crypto-insights-platform-subscription|Crypto Insights
Group customer agreement]].

> Note: the two source documents cite different Hong Kong registered addresses for BICL.
> Treat the address as provisional pending reconciliation.

## Role

- Acts as **Technical Service Consultant** to [[ternary-fund-management]] under the
  [[2026-04-29-ternary-technical-services-agreement]], providing technical, brand and
  ecosystem support for [[brook-limited-partners-fof]].
- Operates the proprietary brands "The Brooker Group" and "Brook Technology Capital"; all
  IP in those brands remains with BICL.
- Is the **Customer** under the [[2026-03-23-crypto-insights-platform-subscription|Crypto
  Insights Group]] research-platform subscription.

## Relationships

- **[[crypto-insights-group]]** — research-platform vendor BICL subscribes to
- Director **Varut Bulakul** also signs as General Partner on the Brook LP FoF I deck.
