# Formidium Singapore Pte Ltd

## Overview

Formidium Singapore Pte Ltd ("Formidium" / the "Administrator") is the appointed **Fund
Administrator** for [[brook-limited-partners-fof]], engaged under a Fund Administration
Agreement with the sub-fund.

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Role | Fund Administrator of Brook Limited Partners FoF I |
| Address | 20 Cecil Street, #22-0, Plus, Singapore 049705 |
| Contact | investor.support@formidium.com |
| Appointed by | The sub-fund, via a Fund Administration Agreement |

## Responsibilities

- Calculating the sub-fund's **net asset value (NAV)**.
- Accounting, back-office, data processing and related administrative services.
- Certain **anti-money laundering (AML)** functions, including AML review on investor
  withdrawals.
- Paying the [[ternary-fund-management|Fund Manager's]] Management Fee out of sub-fund
  assets attributable to the Participating Shares.
- Providing quarterly NAV statements; issuing capital-call and distribution notices.

## Limitations of Role

- Services are **purely administrative** — Formidium provides no tax, legal or investment
  advice and assumes no fiduciary duties.
- It does **not have custody** of sub-fund assets and performs no due diligence on
  underlying investments or valuations.
- It is entitled to rely on valuation/pricing information from the sub-fund and third
  parties without independent verification.
- Either party may terminate the Fund Administration Agreement on **90 days' notice**.

## Relationships

- **[[brook-limited-partners-fof]]** — the sub-fund it administers
- **[[ternary-fund-management]]** — Fund Manager that selects and monitors Formidium
- Custodian is **DBS Bank Ltd**; auditor and tax advisor is **Ernst & Young LLP** (see
  [[brook-lp-fof-terms]]).
