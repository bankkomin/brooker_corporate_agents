# Ternary Fund Management Pte Ltd

## Overview

Ternary Fund Management Private Limited ("Ternary") is the MAS-licensed Singapore Fund
Manager appointed to manage [[brook-technology-capital-vcc]] and its sub-fund
[[brook-limited-partners-fof]]. Ternary makes all investment decisions for the sub-fund
and is responsible for selecting and monitoring all other service providers. Its CEO,
Edward Choi (Choi Eun Seug), is the named Director & Portfolio Manager / Key Investment
Professional on the fund.

## Role on Brook Limited Partners FoF I

- Appointed Fund Manager under an **Investment Management Agreement (IMA)** to make all
  investment decisions per the strategy, guidelines and restrictions of the
  [[brook-lp-fof-terms|sub-fund terms]].
- The VCC Board delegates all sourcing, monitoring, due diligence and investment/
  divestment execution to Ternary, retaining oversight only.
- Receives a **1.5% p.a. Management Fee** on aggregate Commitments (see
  [[brook-lp-fof-terms]]).
- May appoint an Investment Advisor and sub-consultants with Directors' approval.
