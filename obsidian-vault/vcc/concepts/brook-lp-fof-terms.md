## Share Classes

- **Class A Participating Shares** — open to all Eligible Investors; no regular
  distributions; distributions intended only on liquidation of investments, after the
  Investment Period.
- **Class B Participating Shares** — issued at Directors' discretion ("Founder Shares");
  entitled to Carried Interest under the distribution waterfall.

## Distribution Waterfall

1. **Return of capital** — 100% of Distributable Cash pro rata to Class A and Class B
   until all Capital Contributions are returned.
2. **Tiered split** — once capital is fully returned, Class B receives Carried Interest;
   remaining Distributable Cash goes to Class A pro rata.
- **Clawback** — Directors may recall amounts already distributed to Class B "Founder
  Shares" by increasing their Unused Commitments.

## Subscriptions & Closings

- **Initial Offer Period** at US$1,000/share; first Closing Date "[●] 2026".
- **Final Closing** one year after the Initial Offer Period (extendable, no later than end
  of Investment Period).
- **New Investors** at Subsequent Closings pay an **Equalisation Amount** plus an 8% p.a.
  compounded **Subsequent Closing Premium** (waivable by the Board).
- **Redemptions** — closed-end: no redemptions unless approved by Directors after
  consulting the Fund Manager.

## Segregation

As a sub-fund under the VCC Act, the sub-fund has statutory **asset/liability segregation**
from other sub-funds and the umbrella VCC — though foreign courts may not recognise this
(a disclosed risk factor).
