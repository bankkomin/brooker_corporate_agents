# BICL Audited Financial Statements — FY2025

## Summary

Audited Directors' Report and Financial Statements of
[[bicl-corporate-profile|Brooker International Company Limited]] for the year ended
**31 December 2025**, prepared under HKFRS for Private Entities and audited by Philip
Leung & Co. Limited. The auditor's report is dated 12 February 2026. All figures in USD;
FY2024 comparatives shown. **FY2025 was a loss-making year**, in sharp contrast to FY2024.

## Key Notes

- **Investment funds** fell to USD 13.45M (FY2024: 20.26M) with a USD 5.99M fair-value
  loss; **crypto-assets** USD 29,857,714 carrying amount with a USD 3,616,535 write-down.
- **I-RECs:** inventory of USD 600,000 at year end; USD 5,084,110 surrendered during the
  year due to unsuccessful tokenisation.
- **Dividend:** USD 2,300,000 declared in FY2025 (first dividend in the two-year window) —
  the same amount as the No.36 promissory note (see [[2026-01-16-loan-pn36-2.3m]]).
- **Loans from parent company:** unsecured, 3.5% p.a., repayable on demand — see
  [[bicl-related-party-financing]].
- **Subsidiaries:** Brook Technology Capital VCC (Singapore) added — see
  [[bicl-subsidiaries-and-funds]].
- **Ultimate holding company:** The Brooker Group PCL, Thailand (Note 17).

## Year-on-Year Read

- Revenue collapsed from USD 6.27M to USD 0.40M; the year swung from a USD 14.24M profit
  to a **USD 15.66M loss**, driven by investment-fund fair-value losses, a crypto-asset
  write-down, and I-REC tokenisation costs.
- Total equity fell from USD 23.9M to USD 5.94M; retained earnings cut to USD 0.34M after
  the loss and a USD 2.30M dividend.
- Trade & interest receivables dropped sharply (USD 6.40M → 1.05M).
