## Key Notes

- **Crypto-assets** held as inventory for trading, stated at lower of cost and net
  realisable value; FY2024 carrying amount USD 32,539,786 with write-back of USD
  2,001,175 (FY2023 write-back USD 8,296,779). (See [[bicl-going-concern]].)
- **Subsidiaries & funds:** see [[bicl-subsidiaries-and-funds]].
- **Loans from parent company:** unsecured, interest at 3.5% p.a., repayable on demand —
  see [[bicl-related-party-financing]].
- **Taxation:** directors consider no provision is required; operations do not make a
  profit apparent to tax (Note 6).
- **Ultimate holding company:** The Brooker Group PCL, Thailand (Note 17).

## Year-on-Year Read

- Revenue nearly tripled (USD 2.37M → 6.27M), driven by service provision and the new
  IREC trading line.
- Total assets up ~21% (USD 52.7M → 63.9M); equity more than doubled on retained
  earnings of USD 18.3M.
- Parent-company loan balance reduced from USD 43.1M to USD 40.0M.
