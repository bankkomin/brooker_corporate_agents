# BICL — Brooker International Company Limited (Corporate Profile)

## Overview

Brooker International Company Limited ("BICL") is a Hong Kong-incorporated private
company limited by shares and a wholly-owned subsidiary of [[brooker-group|Brooker Group
PCL]]. It is the Group's offshore vehicle for research and financial consultancy
services, investment funds, crypto-assets, and International Renewable Energy
Certificates (I-RECs). This is the finance-department detail record for the CEO-vault
entity [[bicl]].

## History

- **2010-09-09** — Incorporated in Hong Kong under the Companies Ordinance (Cap. 32 at
  the time of incorporation); Certificate of Incorporation No. 1503394.
- **2010-09-03** — Memorandum & Articles of Association subscribed; The Brooker Group Plc
  took the entire initial subscription of 600,000 shares.
- **FY2024 / FY2025** — Audited financial statements prepared under HKFRS for Private
  Entities; consolidated reporting exemption taken as a wholly-owned subsidiary.
