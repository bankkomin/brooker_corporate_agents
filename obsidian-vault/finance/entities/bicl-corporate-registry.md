# BICL Corporate Registry — Directors & Members

## Overview

Statutory registers of [[bicl-corporate-profile|Brooker International Company Limited]],
certified as true copies in April 2026 by ICKW Membership (CPA), per the Hong Kong
Companies Ordinance. Covers the Register of Directors and the Register of Members
(shareholders).

## Register of Directors (April 2026)

BICL's directors are members of the Bulakul family, consistent with the audited
Directors' Reports for FY2024 and FY2025, which list directors **BULAKUL Chan**,
**BULAKUL Varut** and **BULAKUL Varit**. The certified register confirms the active
directors and their particulars as at April 2026.

| Register certification | Certified true copy, April 2026 | Register of Directors |
| Certifying party | ICKW Membership — James Crossley-Smith, CPA | Register endorsement |

## Register of Members (Shareholders, April 2026)

The Register of Members records the allotment and transfer history of BICL's ordinary
shares. The company's issued and fully paid share capital is **5,600,000 ordinary
shares** (see [[bicl-audited-financials-2025]], Share Capital note). The founding
subscription, per the Memorandum & Articles, was **600,000 shares** taken by The Brooker
Group Plc on 3 September 2010; share capital was subsequently increased to the current
5,600,000 shares. The register shows entries dated through to 14 May 2022, certified as
a true copy in April 2026.
