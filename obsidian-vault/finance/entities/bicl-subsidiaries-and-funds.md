# BICL Subsidiaries & Investment Funds

## Overview

[[bicl-corporate-profile|BICL]]'s own group of subsidiaries and investment-fund holdings,
per Notes 7 and 8 of the FY2024 and FY2025 audited financial statements. BICL is itself
a wholly-owned subsidiary of [[brooker-group]] but holds investments in subsidiaries and
funds at cost.

## Subsidiaries (Note 7)

| Subsidiary | Place of incorporation | Ownership | Principal activity |
|-----------|------------------------|-----------|--------------------|
| Brooker Dunn Asset Advisory Limited | British Virgin Islands | 51% | Fund management |
| Arun Signal Company Limited | British Virgin Islands | 100% | Investment research & portfolio construction |
| Brook Technology Capital VCC | Singapore | 100% (new in FY2025) | Funds holding |

Investments in subsidiaries are carried **at cost USD 529,990** (FY2024 and FY2025).
The Singapore VCC ([[brook-technology-capital-vcc]]) first appears in the FY2025 Note 7.

## Investment Funds (Note 8)

Investment funds are held medium-to-long term and remeasured at fair value through
profit or loss.

| OP Crypto (BVI) Fund | 1,123,454 | — |

| Brook Limited Partners Fund of Funds I | — | 8,887,887 |

The FY2025 portfolio shows the Brook Limited Partners Fund of Funds I appearing as an
USD 8.89M holding, while OP Crypto and UVM Signum Blockchain holdings are no longer
listed — consistent with the Group's migration of fund products into the Singapore VCC.

## Other Investment (Note 9)

BICL also holds unlisted shares at cost in **AKT Ltd (formerly known as Civetta Capital
Limited)**, incorporated in the Cayman Islands, 12.5% owned, principal activity fund
management — carried at USD 17 (FY2024 and FY2025).
