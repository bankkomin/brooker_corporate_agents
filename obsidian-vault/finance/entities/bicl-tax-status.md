# BICL Tax Status — W-8BEN-E / FATCA Classification

## Quick-answer aliases (for retrieval)

- **Q: What is BICL's FATCA classification?** A: **Active Non-Financial Foreign Entity (Active NFFE)** — established by the signed IRS Form W-8BEN-E (Rev. October 2021).
- **Q: What FATCA form did BICL sign?** A: **IRS Form W-8BEN-E (Rev. October 2021)** classifying BICL as Active NFFE.
- **Q: Is BICL a Financial Institution under FATCA?** A: No — BICL is classified as a **Non-Financial Foreign Entity (NFFE), Active** category, not as an FFI.

## Overview

The signed IRS Form **W-8BEN-E (Rev. October 2021)** establishes
[[bicl-corporate-profile|BICL]]'s status as the beneficial owner of US-source income for
US tax withholding and FATCA reporting. It is the document a US withholding agent or
payer holds on file to confirm BICL's foreign status and chapter-4 (FATCA) classification.

## Why It Matters

As an **Active NFFE**, BICL certifies that less than 50% of its gross income is passive
income and less than 50% of its assets produce or are held to produce passive income —
so it is not treated as a Foreign Financial Institution (FFI) and is not subject to FFI
registration/reporting under FATCA. This classification is consistent with BICL's
operating profile as a research and financial consultancy company (see
[[bicl-corporate-profile]]).

## Agent Notes

A W-8BEN-E does not expire on a fixed date but is generally valid until the end of the
third calendar year after signing, or until a change in circumstances. If BICL's
business profile changes, the FATCA classification must be re-assessed and the form
re-filed with US payers.
