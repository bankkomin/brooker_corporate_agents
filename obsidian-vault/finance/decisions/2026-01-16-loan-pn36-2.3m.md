## Rationale

PN.36 is a new advance from the parent on the same 3.5%, on-demand, unsecured terms as
PN.35 (see [[2025-04-08-loan-pn35-39m]] and [[bicl-related-party-financing]]). The
USD 2.3M principal matches the **USD 2,300,000 dividend declared by BICL in FY2025** (see
[[bicl-audited-financials-2025]]) — the timing suggests the note funds or offsets that
dividend distribution.

## Impact

- Adds USD 2.3M to BICL's parent-company loan stack at the standard 3.5% rate.
- As an on-demand note, the principal is a current liability of BICL.
- Together with PN.35 it forms the bulk of the ~USD 39.37M parent-loan balance reported at
  31 Dec 2025.
