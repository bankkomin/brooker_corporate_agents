# Promissory Note No.35 — USD 39.02M Loan, Brooker Group → BICL

## Decision

Promissory Note **No.35**, made on **8 April 2025**, under which
[[bicl-corporate-profile|Brooker International Co., Ltd.]] ("Borrower") promises to pay
[[brooker-group|The Brooker Group Plc]] ("Lender") the principal sum of
**USD 39,023,179.25** (Thirty Nine Million Twenty Three Thousand One Hundred Seventy Nine
and 25/100 US Dollars). This note **renews Promissory Note No.34**.

## Rationale

PN.35 is the renewal of the prior PN.34, rolling forward the bulk of the parent-company
loan that funds BICL's investment-fund and crypto-asset book. The 3.5% rate matches the
"loans from parent company" terms disclosed in BICL's audited financial statements (see
[[bicl-related-party-financing]]).

## Impact

- **Carrying balance:** the parent-company loan totals USD 40,001,451 at 31 Dec 2024 and
  USD 39,367,439 at 31 Dec 2025 (see [[bicl-audited-financials-2024]],
  [[bicl-audited-financials-2025]]) — PN.35 is the principal component.
- **Finance cost:** parent-loan interest of USD 1,448,098 (FY2024) and USD 1,375,113
  (FY2025) flows through BICL's income statement.
- **On-demand risk:** as the note is payable on demand, the full amount is classified as a
  current liability in BICL's balance sheet.
