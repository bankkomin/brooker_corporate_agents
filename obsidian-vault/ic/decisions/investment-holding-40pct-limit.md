# Investment Holding 40% Rebalance

## Decision

Bring `Investment / Total Assets` below the **40% regulatory cap** by **30 June 2026** (end of the 2-year BoT grace period).

| Meeting | Ratio | Plan |
|---------|-------|------|
| 2025-01-23 | 54.5% (Q3) | 2-yr grace, no new investment |
