# Preferred Shares as Digital Credit (Funding Channel)

## Decision

Develop a **concept paper for Preferred Shares as a Digital Credit instrument** — a new fast-cash funding channel for the firm. New 2026 objective (#10) first recorded at the May 2026 IC meeting; directly operationalises a risk-mitigation idea from the [[capital-sovereignty-doctrine]].

| 2026-05-12 | **In progress** | Formalised as Objective #10 — "Preferred Shares as Digital Credit concept paper (Funding Channel) — In progress" |

## Context

Per the [[capital-sovereignty-doctrine]] (deck slide 18), the firm faces concentrated funding risk:

- **SCB might recall its Bt 300 mn loan in July 2026.**
- DAT sale is restricted while the loan is outstanding.
- The firm needs **additional funding channels with fast cash** that reduce middleman friction.

The proposed instrument is described as **"similar to an STRC instrument or financially-engineered digital credit"** — a Preferred Share structured to behave like callable credit, sourced without a traditional bank intermediary.

## Objective

> Reduce middleman friction in the firm's capital stack by issuing Preferred Shares that function as digital credit, providing a self-controlled funding channel independent of bank loan recall risk.
