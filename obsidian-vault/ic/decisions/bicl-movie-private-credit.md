# BICL Movie Private Credit Pilot

## Decision

Stand up the **BICL Pilot Private Credit Facility** as a senior-lender film-financing book, in 1:1 co-lender ratio with **[[obsidian-creek-capital|Obsidian Creek Capital]]**. Status: **DD for Q3 2026**.

## Facility terms *(deck slide 29)*

| Term | Value |
|------|-------|
| Senior Lender | BICL |
| Co-Lender | [[obsidian-creek-capital|Obsidian Creek Capital]] (1:1 ratio) |
| Borrower | Production Company |
| Purpose | Private credit bridge financing for film production |
| Collateral | Distributor presale, guarantees, tax credits, completion bond, collection account revenues, film IP |
| **Maximum LTV** | **50%** (presale + gap) |
| **Production value cap** | **< $5 mn per movie** |
| **Interest** | **9% p.a.** |
| **Maximum maturity** | **1.5 years** |
| Insurance | Production completion bond insurance — Brooker as beneficiary |
| Escrow | External Collection Account Manager required |
| **Total facility** | **$10 mn** |

## New structured loan deal *(deck slide 28)*

- **$5mn senior debt to Obsidian Creek** for collateralized film financing
- Sits within the **60% growth portion** of the strategic partner map (alongside structured loans, [[robinhood]], land, AI-integrated corporate startups)

## Connection to Engine 1 yield strategy

- **Brook Turtle Yield FoF** (SubFund 2 of [[singapore-vcc-structure]]) — Obsidian Creek Film Private Credit listed on the "Product Discovery" line of sub-funds in R&D, with **target 15-20% gross**.

## Why pilot now?

- 2026 Objective #8: "New Structured Loan for 60% growth portion — **private credit movie receivables**. DD for Q3."
- Diversifies the [[structured-loan-portfolio]] beyond Thai equity collateral
- Higher gross yield than current 14% structured loan book
- Provides Engine 1 a yield product with audited collateral chain

## Risks (to assess in DD)

- Concentration in single film vehicle if scaling beyond pilot
- Distributor counterparty risk
- Completion bond insurer credit
- Cross-jurisdictional enforcement (US film vs Thai/SG lender)
