The May 2026 IC deck restates the OKR-1 table. The North Star total is unchanged at **Bt 500 mn**, but the Engine 3 lines are now itemised differently:

| Engine | Line item | Mar 2026 | May 2026 |
|--------|-----------|----------|----------|
| Engine 3 | DA Treasury Yield | 100 | 100 |
| Engine 3 | Structured Loan Interest & Private Credit | *(folded into other lines)* | **80** (new explicit line — Bt 800M book × 10% avg) |

Net effect: Engine 3 fee/yield income (Bt 244 mn across the three lines) now **exceeds Engine 1** (Bt 210 mn excl. seeding) in the published build. See [[IC-2026-05-12]] §11.
