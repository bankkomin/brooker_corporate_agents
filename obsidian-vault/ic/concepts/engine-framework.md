# The Three-Engine Framework

## Summary

Brooker's strategic doctrine, formalised in the IC No1 March 2026 deck, organises the firm's revenue and balance-sheet activities into **three engines**, each contributing toward the **North Star [[okr-500mb-recurring-income|500 MB Recurring Income OKR]]**.

## The three engines

| Engine | Domain | Core line items | Bt mn |
|--------|--------|-----------------|-------|
| **Engine 1** | VCC Platform | VC FoF advisory fee · Yield FoF advisory fee · Yield perf fee · Seed yield income | ~224 |
| **Engine 2** | Advisory | Financial / Structure / Digital Advisory mandates | ~30 |
| **Engine 3** | DAT (Digital Asset Treasury) + Other Innovation | Lending / Validator / Staking · Structured loan & private credit · M&A · AI trading | ~244 |
| | | **TOTAL OPERATING INCOME** | **500** |

## Engine 1 — VCC Platform

Singapore VCC structure ([[singapore-vcc-structure]]) — Brook Technology VCC.
- **SubFund 1 — Brook Limited Partners VC FoF I** (target $600M AUM, $11.2M first close transferred Q3 2025)
- **SubFund 2 — Brook Turtle Yield FoF** (NEW; target $250M AUM, BICL $5mn seed for Q2)
- Top allocations: a16z Crypto Fund V ($15M target), Pantera ($5-10M)

## Engine 2 — Advisory

3 mandates @ Bt 10mn average. Funnels via "Brooker Club" LP events (target 60-100 LPs engaged).

## Engine 3 — Digital Asset Treasury + Other Innovation

- **DAT yield:** validator/staking, lending, OKR-1b Bt 250 mn annualised income (BNB validator, 7 ETH validators, 140 SOL validators)
- **Sell + Call options strategy** ([[dat-sell-call-strategy]]) — bring DAT into [[investment-holding-limit]] compliance while preserving optionality
- **Structured loan / private credit:** Bt 800M book @ 10% avg → Bt 80mn income; **+ BICL movie credit pilot** ([[bicl-movie-private-credit]])
- **Innovation:** Reallocation of treasury, M&A, AI trading, [[prediction-market-pilot]]

## DAT Doctrine (slide 15)

> Build positions that remain strong under multiple future board states (Bull or Bear). Place stones that maintain **optionality, influence, and survivability** even if the opponent's next move is uncertain.

Quantified:
- Crypto & VC MTM > 50%, **25% dry powder**
- Influence: **Bt 1,000 mn**
- Survivability: **Bt 500 mn**
- Optionality = increase volatility / beta / yield
