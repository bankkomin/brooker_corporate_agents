# DAT Round-1 Sell-down Proceeds (May 2026)

## Summary

The IC committee approved a Round-1 reduction of the Digital Asset Treasury (DAT) at
the **12 May 2026 meeting** (IC Meeting #2 2026). The sell-down covers 40% of BTC at
$80k, 5% of BNB at $650, and ALL ETH, SOL, and Mantle positions.

**Total cash raised from the DAT Round-1 sell-down: Bt 392 mn.**

## Breakdown of Round-1 Sales

| Asset sold | Action | Amount |
|------------|--------|--------|
| BTC | Sell 40% at $80k | Core DAT becomes 100 BTC |
| BNB | Sell 5% at $650 | Core DAT becomes 40,000 BNB |
| ETH, SOL, Mantle | Sell ALL at $5.9 mn | Fully exits these positions |
| Small ICO tokens | Sell all | Full exit |
| **Total cash raised** | | **Bt 392 mn** |

**Core DAT after Round-1 sale = 100 BTC + 40,000 BNB = Bt 1.09 bn.**
