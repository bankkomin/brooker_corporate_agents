# IC Liquidity Management Policy

## Summary

The IC monitors a forward-looking liquidity buffer covering the **expected FY cash outflow** (Bt 300mn for FY2025). The buffer is decomposed into Cash & Equivalents, Current Stocks, and Current Unlocked Funds. Liquidity Risk and D/E Ratio are reported at every meeting.

> NOTE: This is the **IC committee's** liquidity view, focused on portfolio-level liquidity for investment cash needs. The corporate **CAC** liquidity view (LCR/NSFR/HQLA) is tracked in [[cac/concepts/lcr]] and [[cac/concepts/nsfr]] and is the regulatory liquidity ceiling.

## Components

| Component | Definition |
|-----------|------------|
| Cash & Equivalents | Demand deposits + cash management instruments |
| Current Stocks | Listed positions liquidatable within 1 quarter |
| Current Unlocked Funds | Open-end fund redemptions available within 1 quarter |
| Expected FY Cash Outflow | Forecast committed outflows for the year |

## Risk Bands

| Component sum vs. FY outflow | Status |
|------|--------|
| ≥ 1.5× | Low |
| 1.0× – 1.5× | Adequate |
| 0.5× – 1.0× | Warning — flag |
| < 0.5× | Critical — immediate action |

## Why It Matters

This is the **investment committee's** working capital. A liquidity squeeze here forces premature exit of Red Flag or concentration positions at a discount, undermining the [[red-flag-portfolio-reduction]] plan.
