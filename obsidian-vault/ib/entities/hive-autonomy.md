## Overview

Hive Autonomy is a [[superseed-fund-iii|SuperSeed Fund III]] portfolio company in
**Buildings & Infrastructure**, providing **fleet autonomy for heavy industrial
vehicles**. Its AI, sensors, and remote-control technology convert forklifts, wheel
loaders, and diggers from one-operator-per-vehicle to **one-operator-per-fleet** — a
10-vehicle warehouse fleet runs with a single console operator.
