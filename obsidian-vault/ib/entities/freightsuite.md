# FreightSuite

## Overview

FreightSuite is a [[superseed-fund-iii|SuperSeed Fund III]] portfolio company in **Energy &
Logistics**, providing **AI automation for freight forwarders**. Its SaaS platform
automates the operational workflow of freight forwarders, replacing manual, error-prone
processes with AI-driven automation across booking, documentation, and customer operations.
