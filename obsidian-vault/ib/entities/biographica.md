# Biographica

## Overview

Biographica is a [[superseed-fund-iii|SuperSeed Fund III]] portfolio company in
**Agriculture**, providing **AI-driven crop genetic engineering**. Deep learning and
knowledge graphs identify optimal gene edits computationally before any plant work begins,
replacing years of field trial-and-error with computational precision. It has a particular
focus on drought-resistant and climate-adapted crops.
