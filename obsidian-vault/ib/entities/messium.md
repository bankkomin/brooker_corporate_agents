# Messium

## Overview

Messium is a [[superseed-fund-iii|SuperSeed Fund III]] portfolio company in
**Agriculture**, providing **precision agriculture from space**. Using hyperspectral
satellite imaging and AI, Messium tells farmers exactly how much fertiliser to apply at
every point of the growing cycle — achieving 90%+ accuracy versus lab tests with no new
equipment required from the farmer. The problem it targets: farmers routinely over- or
under-fertilise, wasting fertiliser or curtailing yields.

## Thai Relevance

Thailand is a major rice, sugar, and palm-oil producer. Globally, farmers over-apply
nitrogen by **35–65%**, creating **$114bn of annual waste** — the inefficiency Messium
targets.
