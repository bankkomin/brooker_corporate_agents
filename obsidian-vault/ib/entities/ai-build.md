# Ai Build

## Overview

Ai Build is a [[superseed-fund-iii|SuperSeed Fund III]] portfolio company in
**Manufacturing**, offering **AI-orchestrated large-scale 3D printing**. Its AI platform
for industrial-scale additive manufacturing reduces the human effort required to produce a
large 3D-printed object by up to **86%**, enabling what the brief calls the fastest
large-scale additive manufacturing globally.
