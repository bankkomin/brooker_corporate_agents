# ABB (ABB Motion Ventures)

## Overview

ABB is a global technology company (electrification and automation; HQ Zürich,
Switzerland). Its corporate venture arm **ABB Motion Ventures** is a **strategic investor**
in [[octaipipe|OctaiPipe]] — a [[superseed-fund-iii|SuperSeed Fund III]] portfolio
company — and a go-to-market / distribution partner for OctaiPipe's data-centre cooling
optimisation software. ABB appears in the IB knowledge base only via this OctaiPipe
relationship.

## Significance for OctaiPipe / Brooker

The ABB partnership is the most concrete validation point in OctaiPipe's story: a major
industrial automation incumbent both **investing** and providing **distribution** into
data-centre operators. For Brooker, ABB's involvement de-risks the "hyperscaler
distribution" claim in the [[2026-05-08-superseed-fund-iii-brooker-briefing|SuperSeed
briefing]].
