# OctaiPipe

## Overview

OctaiPipe is a [[superseed-fund-iii|SuperSeed Fund III]] portfolio company in **Energy &
Logistics**, providing **federated AI for data-centre cooling**. Federated AI agents sit on
data-centre cooling equipment, run real-time thermodynamic simulations, and fine-tune
cooling operations live. Cooling alone accounts for ~**40% of total data-centre energy
load**, making it the single largest energy line to optimise.

> **Provenance:** the *Thai Relevance* and the headline metrics below the line marked
> *(brief)* come from the confidential `SuperSeed_Brooker_Portfolio_Brief.pdf`. Company
> background, technology, funding, and customers marked *(public)* come from public web
> research accessed **2026-05-22** — see [[#Brief vs Public Sources]] and
> [[#Source References]].

## Company Background *(public)*

- **Founded:** 2016 · **HQ:** London, United Kingdom.
- Describes itself as a British pioneer in **federated machine learning** for AI-driven
  data-centre energy optimisation (edge / on-premises AI).
- **Leadership:** Eric Topham (CEO & Co-Founder), Ivan Scattergood (CTO & Co-Founder),
  George Hancock (CGO).

## Technology *(public)*

- **Federated machine learning** — AI agents learn across multiple sites without
  centralising data ("privacy by design"); cross-site learning is claimed to deliver up to
  **2× the savings** of a single-site solution.
- Combines **multi-agent reinforcement learning** and **digital twins** to dynamically
  optimise cooling setpoints in real time.
- **On-premises deployment, no new hardware required**; delivers recommendations and
  explanations in plain English.
- Certifications: **ISO 27001, ISO 9001, Cyber Essentials Plus**.
