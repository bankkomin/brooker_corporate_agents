# OctaiPipe

## Overview

OctaiPipe is a [[superseed-fund-iii|SuperSeed Fund III]] portfolio company in **Energy &
Logistics**, providing **federated AI for data-centre cooling**. Federated AI agents sit on
data-centre cooling equipment, run real-time thermodynamic simulations, and fine-tune
cooling operations live. Cooling alone accounts for ~**40% of total data-centre energy
load**, making it the single largest energy line to optimise.
