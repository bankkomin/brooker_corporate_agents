# All3

## Overview

All3 is a [[superseed-fund-iii|SuperSeed Fund III]] portfolio company in **Buildings &
Infrastructure**, offering **AI-designed, robot-built construction**. It runs an
end-to-end construction stack: AI generates building designs and permit-ready blueprints, a
robotic facility manufactures the modular components, and on-site **Mantis** robots
assemble the building.

## Thai Relevance

Addresses Thailand's construction productivity gap and the safety failures behind the
**2025 Bangkok earthquake building collapse** and the recurring **Rama 2 highway panel
incidents** — structural safety being a stated motivation.
