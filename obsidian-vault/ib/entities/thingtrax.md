# ThingTrax

## Overview

ThingTrax is a [[superseed-fund-iii|SuperSeed Fund III]] portfolio company in
**Manufacturing**, offering **computer-vision AI for the factory floor**. It provides
vision AI for production and quality control with multi-camera, multi-attribute
recognition, integrating with 300+ devices and protocols. It is described as the **UK
market leader in F&B manufacturing automation**.
