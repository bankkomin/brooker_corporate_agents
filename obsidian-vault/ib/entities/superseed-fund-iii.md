# SuperSeed Fund III

## Overview

SuperSeed Fund III is an external venture capital fund, managed by **SuperSeed Ventures
LLP**, that invests in **AI for the physical economy** ("physical AI"). In May 2026 the GP
delivered a confidential strategic briefing to Brooker Group proposing a **strategic, not
passive** relationship: Brooker would act as the introduction layer between the fund's
commercially-live portfolio companies and Thai customers. See
[[physical-ai-investment-thesis]] for the thesis and
[[2026-05-08-superseed-fund-iii-brooker-briefing]] for the source briefing.

## Strategic Proposition for Brooker

- Each company is at commercial scale-up, so a route to market is its priority — Brooker's
  Thai relationships are directly valuable.
- The return framing is **measurable benefit to Thai operating businesses**, delivered
  through the Brooker relationship, rather than a passive financial allocation.
