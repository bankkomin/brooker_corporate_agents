## Key Marketing Claims

- Pantera Venture Funds' IRRs and DPIs "mostly rank in the top decile" of traditional
  early-stage venture funds of similar vintages (PitchBook benchmark, 6/30/25).
- Distributions begin "as early as year four"; Fund I returned 4x within five years.
- Blockchain venture is positioned as "less crowded and competitive" due to limited
  regulatory clarity and a smaller specialist talent pool.

## 2026 Outlook (Pantera house view)

- **Bull case:** Iran-conflict resolution; strong US economy with mid-term fiscal
  stimulus; improving blockchain fundamentals; broadening institutional adoption;
  oversold near-term markets.
- **Bear case:** Iran contagion and inflation; private-credit and AI-bubble fragility;
  K-shaped economy; further bear-market consolidation and repricing; delayed legislation.
- **Adoption gap:** Bitcoin addresses >$10k make up ~0.5% (~4.4mm) vs ~99.5% (~900mm)
  traditional accounts >$10k; 67% of managers have 0% digital-asset allocation (BofA
  survey, 2025).
