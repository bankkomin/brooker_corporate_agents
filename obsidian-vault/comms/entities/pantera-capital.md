# Pantera Capital

## Overview

Pantera Capital is a US institutional blockchain investment firm and Brooker Group's guest
speaker / co-brand for the April 2026 [[2026-04-21-pantera-x-brooker-investor-lunch|Crypto
Venture Adventure investor lunch]]. The IC department also tracks Pantera as an allocation
target — see [[pantera-fund]].

## Key Facts

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Founded | 2003 by Dan Morehead (ex-Tiger Management Head of Macro Trading & CFO) |
| First blockchain fund | 2013 — first blockchain hedge & venture funds in the US |
| AUM | ~$3.5 bn in blockchain assets (est. 28 Feb 2026) |
| Portfolio companies | 260 (equity + token investments) |
| Unicorns | 25 portfolio companies have reached >$1 bn valuation |
| Employees | 79; 20 investment professionals |
| Deals led | 75%; 40% of investments outside the US |
| Investor description | First US institutional asset manager dedicated to blockchain |

## History

Pantera's macro strategy invested $1 bn of institutional allocations before the firm
pivoted to blockchain in 2013. It has invested through all market cycles and across every
major blockchain sector. Notable early portfolio names cited in Brooker materials include
Circle (NYSE: CRCL), Ethena, and MEGAETH.
