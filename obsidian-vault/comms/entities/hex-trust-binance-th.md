# BNB Chain, Hex Trust & Binance TH (Gala Partners)

## Overview

The three named institutional partners of the [[2026-bnb-1k-gala-night|BNB $1K Gala
Night]] event. Grouped here as a single light entity note — the comms source only
identifies them as event partners without deeper detail.

## Key Facts

| Partner | Role at event | Relevance to Brooker |
|---------|---------------|----------------------|
| BNB Chain team | Headline guests ("our stars"); infrastructure builder | Brooker holds BNB as a "digital infrastructure tollway" |
| Hex Trust | Institutional custody partner | Custody of digital-asset holdings |
| Binance TH | Thailand exchange partner | Local on/off-ramp and exchange access |

## History

The CEO's keynote frames BNB Chain as having built the "high-speed rails infrastructure"
that ended crypto's "Dial-Up Internet" era — the basis for celebrating BNB at $1,000 and
the partnership thesis (see [[crypto-capex-thesis]]).
