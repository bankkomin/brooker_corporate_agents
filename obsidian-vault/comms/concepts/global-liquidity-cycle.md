# The Global Liquidity Cycle — The One Indicator That Matters

## Summary

Brooker's recurring macro doctrine: out of the wall of macro data — government shutdowns,
geopolitics, interest rates, wars, oil, gold, GDP, AI, unemployment — there is **one
indicator that explains over 90% of the market's trend**: the Global Liquidity Cycle.
"Just follow the money. The rest is noise." It appears in both the CEO's
[[the-audacity-of-value-speech|BNB Gala keynote]] and the
[[2026-chinese-association-macro-talk|Chinese Association macro talk]].

## Historical Context

- **It is a liquidity cycle, not a credit cycle.** The Chinese Association deck states the
  game is "QE & QT while controlling inflation" — markets follow the *liquidity* cycle,
  not the credit cycle (slide 21).
- **Why liquidity keeps expanding.** Modern Monetary Theory mechanics — a global reserve
  currency enables money creation, coordinated central-bank printing, and M2 growth via
  QE, Yield Curve Control, and SLR adjustments. Governments "can't stop the spending
  train": government debt explodes while banks are not lending (slides 16–21).
- **Refinancing risk is the real crisis trigger.** "Financial Crisis = cannot refinance
  debt"; with average debt maturity around four years, refinancing windows define risk
  (slide 24).
- **Hiccups are noise.** Tariffs, trade negotiations, and political drama are "localized
  hiccups" — the underlying expansion trend dominates.

## Why It Matters

Liquidity expansion historically flows into hard assets — gold and digital assets — which
underpins Brooker's bullish positioning in [[crypto-capex-thesis]] and the
[[internet-of-value-thesis]]. It is the comms-facing version of [[brooker-worldview-2026]]
Worldview #3, "Global Liquidity Operating System."
