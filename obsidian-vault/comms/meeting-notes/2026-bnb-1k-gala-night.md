# BNB $1K Gala Night — CEO Speech & Event

**Event:** BNB $1K Gala Night — a celebration of the BNB token reaching the $1,000 mark
**Host:** Brooker Group; keynote by Andrew (CEO)
**Source:** `BNB $1K Gala Night CEO speech.docx`

## Purpose

A stakeholder gala marking BNB hitting $1,000, modelled on Michael Saylor's "Bitcoin 100k
party." The CEO recounts the idea was "born after our trip from a crypto conference in
Singapore back in early October" and "pulled off in just two months." It positions Brooker
as a thought leader and connector in the Thai digital-asset ecosystem.

## Attendees

A deliberately mixed stakeholder audience:

- **BNB Chain team** — the headline guests / "stars"
- World-class venture capital fund managers
- Partners: [[hex-trust-binance-th|Hex Trust]] (institutional custody) and Binance TH
- Young Presidents' Organization (YPO) members
- Distinguished family offices
- Project founders
- International guests flown in from Singapore, Hong Kong, and Amsterdam

## Topics Discussed

The keynote ("The Audacity of Value") and a separate partnership speech ("The Power of
Partnership"). The keynote argues three "tectonic plates" are shifting simultaneously —
the macro shift (the US "Genesis Mission" AI arms race), the tech shift (the crypto
"Broadband Moment"), and the convergence of AI and crypto. Full thesis content is
decomposed into [[the-audacity-of-value-speech]], [[internet-of-value-thesis]],
[[global-liquidity-cycle]], and [[crypto-capex-thesis]].

## Decisions Made

External marketing/relationship event — no governance resolutions. The speech publicly
reaffirms Brooker's two-pillar strategy: own the infrastructure (hold BNB as a "digital
infrastructure tollway") and back the founders via the [[brook-limited-partners-fof|VC
Fund of Funds]].

## Action Items

No formal action items recorded in the source. The speech functions as the public-facing
articulation of strategy already adopted.

## Follow-Up

Other event speakers (operators and builders in the digital-asset space) followed the
keynote. The "Power of Partnership" closing speech thanks the BNB Chain team, VC partners,
international guests, and YPO/family-office attendees.
