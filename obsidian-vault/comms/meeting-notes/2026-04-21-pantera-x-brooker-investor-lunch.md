# Pantera x Brooker Investor Lunch — Crypto Venture Adventure

**Event title:** Crypto Venture Adventure — "A Story of 2000%"
**Date / Time:** 21 April 2026 (alternative 22 April), 12:00–14:00
**Format:** Private, sit-down investor lunch, limited to ~40 participants
**Source:** `Pantera x Brooker April Event.docx` (arrangement brief + invitation)

## Purpose

A small, high-quality investor lunch featuring insights on the crypto venture capital
landscape and macro outlook — Brooker's macro perspective followed by a guest presentation
from [[pantera-capital|Pantera Capital]]. Co-branded "Brooker + Pantera."

## Attendees

Target audience profile (40–60 guests, capped at ~40 for meaningful dialogue):

- Family offices and multi-generation family businesses
- Institutional investors
- Key Brooker clients and partners

## Decisions Made

This is an external marketing event, not a governance forum — no formal resolutions. The
invitation positions the session around four themes: the driving forces of macro, venture
capital opportunities in blockchain innovation, lessons from early investments that
generated extraordinary returns, and how long-term family capital can participate in this
emerging asset class.
